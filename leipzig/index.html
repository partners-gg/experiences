LEIPZIG ·LEIPZIG WALKING TOURS
Leipzig Old Town Tour
Get the best first impression on this immersive Leipzig walking tour. Starting from the romantic Thomaskirchhof, you'll discover the most important sights of the compact city center. In front of the venerable St. Thomas Church, learn about the life and work of Johann Sebastian Bach and the world-famous St. Thomas Choir, and ask your guide anything you like about Leipzig Old Town. Follow the scent of one of the oldest coffee houses in Europe, "Zum Arabischen Coffe Baum". Wander through the impressive Barthels Hof and gaze up at the statues in the magnificent Mädler Passage. Along the way, you'll see the distinctive Renaissance-style Old Town Hall, plus many other sights in the beautiful city center.
