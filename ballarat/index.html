BALLARAT ·BALLARAT WILDLIFE PARK
Ballarat Wildlife Park: Entry Ticket
Visit the popular, family-run Ballarat Wildlife Park in Victoria and get up close to a wide variety of native Aussie wildlife and exotic animals! There are few friendlier wildlife attractions in Australia than this facility, where you can see a wide assortment of incredible creatures including kangaroos, koalas, wombats, crocodiles, and more, all located in pleasant, green surroundings. It's a wonderful place to visit for families or for first-time visitors to Australia. With tickets to Ballarat Wildlife Park, you'll get a true Aussie adventure that incorporates a range of live shows where you'll gain further insight into various species.
