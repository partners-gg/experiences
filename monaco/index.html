Oceanographic Museum of Monaco: Entry Ticket

📍 Oceanographic Museum of Monaco

The Oceanographic Museum of Monaco is one of the oldest and most visited marine science museums in Europe! Explore the marine world, from the 450,000-liter shark tank to the smallest aquariums, and see some unexpected treasures. Starting in April, check out the new ‘Mediterranee 2050’ exhibition. Begin your journey in Oceanomania, where each object has an interesting story. Then, explore the ‘Oceano Monaco’ area to learn about the Princes of Monaco and their commitment to the Mediterranean. Next, board the ‘Oceano Odyssey’ and dive to the bottom of the Mediterranean. Finally, relax on the rooftop terrace with views of the Pelagos sanctuary. Don’t forget to tackle the challenges in ‘My Oceano Med.’
