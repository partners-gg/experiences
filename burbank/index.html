Warner Bros. Studio Tour Hollywood: Guided Tour

📍 Warner Bros. Studio Tour Hollywood

Discover where Hollywood is made and go behind the lens at Warner Bros. Studio Tour Hollywood. This immersive tour brings you closer than ever to the entertainment you love, as you explore famous back-lot sets and sound stages from iconic Hollywood films. Learn how movie magic comes to life at Stage 48: Script to Screen, from classic Hollywood costumes to green-screen technology used to create special effects. Dine at the expanded Central Perk Cafe amongst recreated sets such as Monica's apartment, the famous fountain, and Joey and Chandler's pad, and shop for Friends merch in the Friends Boutique! The all-new Grand Finale lets you step into the shoes of a superhero (or supervillain!) with recreated sets from the DC Universe. You can also swish and flick your way through the Wizarding World of Harry Potter, and even enjoy your own Hogwarts house-sorting ceremony!

TCM Classic Films Tour

📍 Warner Bros. Studio Tour Hollywood

Warner Bros. Studio Tour Hollywood and Turner Classic Movies have partnered up to introduce the TCM Classic Films Tour. This new tour features bespoke clips from TCM hosts, visits to areas of the lot that are not part of the regular tour (including the Property Department and Rose Garden), and TCM-branded tour carts. This classics-themed tour focuses on Warner Bros. Golden Age of Film and TV, featuring award-winning titles that shot on the iconic backlot from the early days of the Studio through the 1970s. A specially trained Tour Guide, with the help of the hosts from Turner Classic Movies, will guide guests through the Studio Tour themed to iconic classic movies and TV shows from the early days of the Studio through the 1970s. While on tour, guests will visit the familiar places and spaces where movie magic has been happening for more than 100 years.
