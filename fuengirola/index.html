Bioparc Fuengirola: Entry Ticket

📍 Bioparc Fuengirola

Located in the heart of the Costa del Sol, Bioparc Fuengirola is a new model of animal parks based on nature and the preservation of its species. Its facilities recreate with great fidelity some of the most amazing rainforests in the world. Visitors can enjoy the most representative fauna and flora of Madagascar, equatorial Africa, Southeast Asia, the Indo-Pacific islands and, as a novelty, the jungles of Central and South America. These not only reproduce the vegetation or reflect the role of water, but also represent how species are organised and interact.
