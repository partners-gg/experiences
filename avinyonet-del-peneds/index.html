ArtCava: Vineyards and Winery Tour

📍 ArtCava – Celler Can Batlle

The Batlle family have been making wine since the 1700s, and you can discover their secrets and the history of Cava with this winery tour in the famous Penedès wine region. An average visit will include a walk through the vineyards and a tour of Masía Can Batlle, which dates back to the 12th century. You'll explore the winery and enjoy a complete tasting of ArtCava's incredible sparkling wine. Experience first-hand the whole process behind the production of a bottle of Cava, in a winery that blends history with modern techniques.
