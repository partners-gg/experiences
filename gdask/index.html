Gdańsk: Guided Beer Tasting Tour

📍 Gdansk Food & Drink Tours

Start your tour in the old town and visit a Gdańsk brewery to taste three freshly brewed beers. You'll also hear the brewer's stories about global and local beer history. Then, head to a craft beer pub to sample 4-5 Polish beers. The bartender will explain the pub's history and the local craft beer scene, including the breweries and ingredients. Enjoy snacks that complement the beers and learn about Gdańsk's beer culture and the beer-making process.
