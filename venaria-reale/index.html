Palace of Venaria Reale: Skip The Line Ticket + Guided Tour

📍 Palace of Venaria Reale

Set off on a three-hour guided tour of the magnificent Venaria Reale Palace, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore the palace adorned with Baroque art and opulent furnishings. Discover the Diana Hall, crafted by the ingenious Amedeo Castellamonte. Prepare to be amazed by the Great Gallery, a sight guaranteed to wonder you and fill your camera with amazing shots. Continue your expedition to the remarkable Juvarra complex. Witness the incredible architecture of the church and the Juvarrian Stables, home to the Venetian bucintoro. Finally, admire the Fountain of the Stag in the Court of Honor and uncover the history of Italy's last royal family, the House of Savoy.
