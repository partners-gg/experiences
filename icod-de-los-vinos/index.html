Tenerife Dragon Tree and Botanical Gardens

📍 Parque del Drago

El Drago Tenerife, also known as Drago Milenario, is the oldest dragon tree in the world at over 800 years old. Discover this ancient emblem of the Canary Islands and wander the surrounding oasis of beautiful botanical gardens of Parque del Drago, with this ticket. Get right up close to the iconic Tenerife dragon tree, and see gnarled snaking branches and spiky, scale-like canopy. The ancient Tenerife dragon tree is famed for its viscous, red-colored resin, known as dragon blood, which has historically been coveted for its beneficial medical properties and artistic applications. After seeing the iconic Tenerife dragon tree and snapping lots of photos, you'll get to stroll around the national botanical garden of Parque del Drago. This stunning expanse of native plant species, flowers, cacti, succulents and more, is complemented by rich exhibits that immerse you in the natural history and cultural customs of the Canary Islands.
