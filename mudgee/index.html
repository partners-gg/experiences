Mudgee: Hot Air Balloon Flight at Sunrise

📍 Mudgee Aviation Activities

Mudgee was made for ballooning! With its rolling hills, surrounding mountains, and beautiful valley, conditions are perfectly suited for hot air ballooning. The spectacular Mudgee region is located some three hours north-west of Sydney and four hours west of Newcastle – it's an ideal weekend getaway location, boasting an abundance of vineyards and cellars doors, as well as accommodation and attractions. Your Mudgee hot air ballooning adventures begin at sunrise, and after witnessing with impressive display of the balloon being inflated (you can even help if you like), you'll take to the skies for a serene one-hour flight over the verdant green vineyards of Mudgee. Once you've touched down, it's time to enjoy a delicious breakfast at a local brunch spot and reminisce over the experience.
