WILDLANDS: Entry Ticket

📍 WILDLANDS

Explore different worlds, see the animals up close, and deepen your love for nature. Travel through the jungle, savannah, and polar region. Visit the world's largest indoor jungle, _Jungola_. Start at the _Butterfly Temple_ with hundreds of butterflies. Sail the Rimbula River where elephants might swim near your boat. Hear the rainforest sounds and see tropical birds. Look closely to spot ring-tailed lemurs, black lemurs, and vari. Enjoy climbing and scrambling on the Jungle Trail. If the Jungle Trail isn't enough, head to _Animazia_, the XL indoor play area for young adventurers. Play on rope bridges, in treehouses, and on slides. Use the sandbox and pull the boats across the water. See tropical fish, sea turtles, and naked mole rats.
