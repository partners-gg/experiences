Otway Fly Treetop Adventures: Treetop Walk

📍 Otway Fly Treetop Adventures

Walk through the elevated treetop walkway situated in the Otway Ranges, approximately 2.5 hours from Melbourne's CBD. Observe the diverse flora and fauna of the rainforest from unique heights. Traverse the 600 m walkway that rises to 25 m above the ground level, and view the forest from ground to canopy. Visit the 45 m Spiral Tower and step on the cantilever suspended above Youngs Creek. Engage with the rainforest ecosystem and deepen understanding of the area's natural environment.
