Lindt Home of Chocolate: Entry Ticket
With Switzerland's incredible natural beauty – its snow capped mountains and crystal-clear lakes – it's almost unfair that it's also home to some of the best chocolate in the world. KILCHBERG ·LINDT HOME OF CHOCOLATE
Zurich: Guided Old Town Tour + Lindt Home of Chocolate Entry + Lake Cruise
This must-do tour will show you all the best bits of Zurich, and more! Join your guide and fellow coach passengers and take off past the castle-like Swiss National Museum. See the high-end shopping mile Bahnhofstrasse and Zurich's illustrious financial district. Take some snaps of the pristine Lake Zurich at Enge harbor, and witness gorgeous guild houses and emblematic churches on a guided walk through the medieval streets. Continue along the picturesque Limmat river to the port, and board a cruise boat on the pristine waters of Lake Zurich. After that, a 15-minute stroll along the shore takes you to the Lindt Home of Chocolate. Marvel at its vast atrium - home to the world's highest free-standing chocolate fountain. Join an interactive audio-guided tour through the chocolate museum, and journey through the history of chocolate from bean to bar. Don't worry, your sweet tooth will be catered for with an unlimited chocolate tasting!
