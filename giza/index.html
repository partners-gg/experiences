Saqqara Necropolis: Entry Ticket

📍 Saqqara Necropolis

Visit Saqqara, the extensive burial site associated with ancient Memphis. The area includes the Step Pyramid of Djoser, designed by Imhotep over 4,600 years ago. It is considered the first major stone structure. Beyond the Step Pyramid are mastabas, temples, and underground galleries. These structures are decorated with ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs and reliefs from the early dynasties. See well-preserved stone walls and chapels that demonstrate early engineering and artistic skill. Learn about the early development of pyramid design and pharaonic culture through ongoing archaeological discoveries.
