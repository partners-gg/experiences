📍 Mallorca Water Activities

Swim in the lakes of one of the most well-known caves in Mallorca. At the meeting point, receive a backpack with a wetsuit and helmet. You can choose to swim, jump from 4 meters and swim, or rappel 20 meters and swim to reach the cave, all requiring a 35-minute walk. Inside the cave, visit different chambers and swim in the lakes.
