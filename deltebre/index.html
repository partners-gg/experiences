River Cruise on the Ebro Delta

📍 Deltebre Cruises

Sit back and relax to the tweet of the birds on this peaceful 1.5-hour cruise to Buda Island and the mouth of the Ebro River. The wetlands of the Delta are home to as many as 300 species of birds. The Catalan stretch of the River Ebro winds its way through the Sebes Nature Reserve, right down to the mouth of the river in the Delta. A boat ride showcases some of Spain's most stunning landscapes and huge biological diversity. Your guide will tell you all about this magical environment as you cruise towards the Mediterranean Sea. Watch the water change its color, and expect a few fun bumps on the waves!

Ebro Delta: Kayak Excursion

📍 Deltebre Outdoor Activities

Craving a fix of peaceful nature with a side-order of invigorating activity? This little adventure ought to float your boat! Bob along the beautiful Ebro Delta of Tarragona on a guided kayak excursion, and earn your evening tapas as you drift into the calm heart of Catalonia's gorgeous wetland scenery. While your arm muscles become evermore shredded as you paddle your merry way downstream, see sunlight play on the water and let that sweet Mediterranean breeze keep you cool.

Ebro Delta: Kayak & Self-Guided Bike Excursion

📍 Deltebre Outdoor Activities

See a different side of Spain, and ditch the beach to explore the beauty of its wetlands. This natural park is a nesting ground for a large number of waterfowl and migratory birds, and rice production is still an essential part of the economy for towns on the delta. During this self-guided excursion, you'll get to experience a glimpse of both. With a bike ride between rice fields and a tranquil kayak cruise down the Ebro Delta, you'll end the day feeling refueled by nature.
