Mount Athos: Sightseeing Cruise + Ammouliani Island Visit

📍 Ouranoupolis Cruises

Visit the holy monasteries of Mount Athos on a 5-hour cruise journey. Witness the unique architecture and know more about the history of these sacred monasteries. Starting from the revered Monastery of Dochiarion, the voyage heads towards other monasteries, monastic hamlets (Skeets), and the bustling port of Dafni, the heart of Mount Athos. Covering 6 out of 9 monasteries, the cruise takes a 1.5-hour pause at Ammouliani island. Here, the clock stops ticking for a sweet hour and a half, offering ample time to explore, relax, and enjoy amazing views of the island.

📍 Ouranoupolis Cruises

Starting from the town of Ouranopolis, you'll get to see Halkidiki's natural beauty from the comfort of a cruise ship.
