ATHENS ·ATHENS BIKE TOURS
Athens Bike Tour: Ride and Bite
During this fun culinary bike tour, you'll cycle through the neighborhoods of Psiri, Plaka and Koukaki. Between the food stops, you'll learn fun information about Greece, both past and present. It's a great introduction to Athens if you've never been before. Pay a visit to one of the oldest taverns in Athens, sample various Greek dishes and continue on via Syntagma Square to the hip Koukaki district, where you'll sit down for the main course - a selection of different and delicious mezes. Mezedes are the Greek tapas, and an important part of the Greek food culture. Sharing these tasty snacks is a great way to eat together and meet new people. Afterwards, cycle a short distance before your last stop, which is a great place for Greek yogurt with honey and walnuts, or an ice cream.

ATHENS ·ACROPOLIS MUSEUM
Acropolis Museum: Guided Tour Only
Step inside the Acropolis Museum with a live guide and discover the wonders of ancient Greece with an expert’s knowledge always at hand. During this guided tour of the Acropolis Museum, you’ll get up close with wonders of the ancient world, including archaic statues that have retained their color, excavated treasures, and more. Highlights of the Acropolis Museum include the relics of the Temple of Athena Nike; sculptures depicting the Gigantomachy, the battle between the Gods of Olumpus and the giants; and Korai, marble statues of young women. Exhibits cover the period from the 5th century BC to the 5th century AD and you’ll have an expert shedding light on the most interesting events in that time span. The Acropolis Museum also offers insight into the development of the city-state and the country's transition from aristocracy to tyranny and, eventually, democracy.
