Everson Museum of Art: Entry Ticket

📍 Everson Museum of Art

Located in the heart of downtown Syracuse, the Everson Museum of Art is a cultural cornerstone with some 10,000 artworks inside. The iconic building – the first of I.M. Pei's internationally renowned museums – welcomes 50,000 visitors each year and with more than 20 exhibitions every year, it's no wonder. The space allows guests to relax, reflect, and find inspiration. If you feel like indulging on top of that, head to _Louise_, the Everson's cafe that serves a locally sourced menu on functional ceramic art.

Ortigia: Guided Boat Tour + Snorkeling + Swim + Drinks

📍 Syracuse Cruises & Boat Tours

Take a 90-minute boat ride around Ortigia, the historic center of Syracuse. See Maniace Castle and the Aretusa Spring as you follow the honey-colored walls. Head into limestone sea caves shaped by the waves. In a calm bay, swim with provided snorkeling gear or relax on the sunbathing cushions. Boats are equipped with safety gear and allow small groups to get close to the cliffs and caves.

Syracuse: Coast & Sea Caves Transparent Boat Tour + Swim Stop

📍 Syracuse Cruises & Boat Tours

Cruise along Syracuse's coast and slip into marine caves carved in limestone. See the Syracuse shoreline on Crystal Boat, a fully transparent craft that surrounds you with sea and sky. For 50 minutes, glide along the coast, enter sea caves, and observe the seabed and marine life below. The skipper will choose a great spot for a swim stop, allowing for underwater photos through the transparent hull.
