PONTA DELGADA ·PONTA DELGADA DAY TRIPS
Lagoa do Fogo & Hot Springs: Day Trip from Ponta Delgada
Begin your trip with a drive to Cerâmica Vieira, a local pottery hub featuring pots, cups, tiles, and plates. After that, visit Fogo Lake in the Água de Pau Massif stratovolcano. It is well-known for its green shores and turquoise waters. Then, go to Caldeira Velha hot springs; an hour of free time is included, and advance notice is required for swimming or visiting tickets. After a meal break, head to Gorreana, the oldest tea plantation in Europe, to see the layered tea crops. Finally, visit four viewpoints to capture coastal scenery and explore the landscape.

Ponta Delgada: Half-Day Guided City Tour + Transfer
Begin your tour at Plantação de Ananás dos Açores, a pineapple plantation. Learn about how pineapple farming became popular despite the island's climate challenges. After that, walk through the center of Ponta Delgada and visit the Church of São Sebastião, famous for its historical significance. Then, head to Campo de São Francisco, the largest town square on São Miguel Island, surrounded by historical buildings from the 19th century. Visit Ermida da Mãe de Deus to view Ponta Delgada's port and much of the city. Finish the tour at Cerâmica Vieira, the first pottery factory in the Azores, to observe the process of making traditional Azorean clay dishware.

PONTA DELGADA ·SAO MIGUEL ISLAND TOURS
São Miguel Island: Full-Day Sete Cidades Guided Tour + Transfers
Tour São Miguel Island's western coastline with a knowledgeable guide. Visit the Vista do Rei viewpoint in Sete Cidades, also known as the "King's view." Explore the village around the lake, one of Portugal's Seven Natural Wonders and a well-known sight in the Azores. Next, head to the village of Mosteiros. Stop at a local restaurant for a traditional lunch (not included). After lunch, travel through the western part of the island to the village of Capelas. See the Port, a former departure point for whale hunting. Finish with a visit to the Pineapple plantations in Ponta Delgada, where you can taste pineapple-based treats.

Nordeste: Waterfall, Hot Spring & Viewpoints Guided Tour
