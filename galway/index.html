Galway to Cliffs of Moher and Burren Full Day Tour
Leave Galway behind for the day during this fully-guided tour to the Cliffs of Moher and Burren. As you travel through the Burren, listen to your expert driver-guide tell tales of Galway and the surrounding area's rich history. You'll step off the coach into windswept landscapes at the Cliffs of Moher. You'll be left speechless by their staggering beauty, standing 700 feet tall and stretching along eight kilometres of the Atlantic coast. Take time for yourself to explore the walking paths along the most outstanding landscape in Ireland, seeing exposed limestone terrain, jagged cliffs, and the untamed Atlantic Ocean as you go. There's also time to pop into the official visitor centre. Alongside the cliffs, you'll see Dunguaire Castle (Kinvara), Aillwee Cave, Lisdoonvarna, Corkscrew Hill, Doolin, and Blackhead Lighthouse.
