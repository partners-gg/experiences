📍 Mount Titlis

Titlis Cableways

📍 Mount Titlis

Believe it or not, the scenery isn't the most mind-blowing thing about this day in the snow at Central Switzerland's Mount Titlis. Nope – the best thing is the ride to the peak on the TITLIS Rotair. It's the first revolving cable car in the world! Witness the breathtaking panorama of snow-capped mountaintops and untouched Alpine landscapes unfold around you. At the top of the only glacier in the entire region that is actually accessible, you'll be free to walk around at 3,020 meters above sea level. Time to practice your mountain yodeling?

Mount Titlis Snow Experience with Transport from Zurich

📍 Mount Titlis

Depart Zurich by coach for a scenic drive along Lake Lucerne to Lucerne, and enjoy free time to explore. Continue to Engelberg and ride the Titlis Xpress to Trübsee, the mid-station where your snow experience begins. Designed for beginners, the one-hour ski session includes access to a gentle learner slope. A staff member supervises the activity, but there is no ski instruction. Afterward, swap skis for snow tubes and glide down the hill, with a magic carpet bringing you back to the top; tubing at Trübsee is free of charge. In the afternoon, choose to remain at Trübsee for lunch at one of the restaurants and more time in the snow park, or take the revolving Rotair to the 3,028 m summit of Mt Titlis. In the late afternoon, descend by cable car and return by coach via Lucerne to Zurich.
