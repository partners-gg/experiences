📍 Thorpe Park

In 2024, Thorpe Park launches the UK’s tallest and fastest rollercoaster – Hyperia. Touch the sky and soar to a breathtaking height of 236ft all while reaching speeds surpassing 80mph. Also new to the park is *Big Easy Boulevard*, a laid-back mini-cityscape, and an awesome line-up of events including Mardi Gras, yodel-inducing Oktoberfest, and terrifyingly terrific Fright Nights. Enjoy world-class rides such as the UK’s only winged rollercoaster *The Swarm," brace yourself on *Stealth*, the UK’s fastest coaster, and make your way to the Safe Zone on *The Walking Dead: The Ride.* Thrill-seekers can also plunge the 100ft ‘beyond vertical’ drop of the movie-themed coaster *SAW-The Ride*.
