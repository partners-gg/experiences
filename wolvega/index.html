Museum 't Kiekhuus: Entry Ticket + Guided Tour

📍 Museum 't Kiekhuus

Imagine yourself back in time, a hundred years ago, and take a step into the grocery store and school class of your grandparents and great-grandparents. See and listen to the objects and unique stories of 't Kiekhuus, and admire the beautiful designs on the historic tin packaging and billboards used by manufacturers such as Douwe Egberts, Droste van Melle and Verkade to advertise their wares.
