Pittsburgh Botanic Garden: Entry Ticket

📍 Pittsburgh Botanic Garden

Visit the Pittsburgh Botanic Garden, a 65-acre site near Pittsburgh featuring gardens, woodlands, and walking trails. Explore areas such as the Garden of the Five Senses, Dogwood Meadow, and a homestead. Learn about the plant life and ecology of the Allegheny Plateau through botanical displays that highlight regional flora. See the Lotus Pond and walk through an ecologically restored landscape that reflects sustainable land use and natural regeneration.
