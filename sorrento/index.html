SORRENTO ·MUSEO CORREALE DI TERRANOVA
The Three Tenors In Sorrento
Inspired by the iconic 1994 'Three Tenors in Concert' performance by Luciano Pavarotti, Placido Domingo and José Carreras, Opera e Lirica reproduces this breathtaking musical experience with its own renowned artists. The enticing voices of Marco Ferrante, Enzo Tremante and Alessandro Fortunato, accompanied by soaring strings and mellifluous piano, take the audience on a spectacular journey through the masterpieces of Italian opera and traditional Neapolitan songs. This is a must-see Sorrento opera full of emotions, romanticism and lighthearted moments that comes to life in the magnificent Concert Hall of the Correale Museum of Terranova.
