Guided Visit to the Janubio Salt Flats

📍 Salt mines of Janubio

Does anyone have the pepper? There's definitely enough salt at Salinas de Janubio, one of the most unique areas on the island of Lanzarote. Join a guided tour for some historic facts and find out the hows and whys of this manmade cultural enigma. On a salty walk you'll experience this unique landscape created by humans, the sea, and the volcanoes of Lanzarote. The tour will end at the Bodega de la Sal, where you'll get to taste Flor de Sal and the Flavours of Janubio.
