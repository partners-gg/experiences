Titanic Museum Attraction Branson: Entry Ticket + Audio Guide

📍 Titanic Museum Attraction Branson

Learn about the Titanic’s story through a self-guided visit to the Titanic Museum Attraction. The museum holds over 400 real artifacts, valued at more than four million dollars. When you arrive, a staff member in costume will give you a boarding pass with the name of an actual Titanic passenger. As you walk through the museum, you’ll explore galleries that share stories of the 2,208 passengers and crew. You can touch an iceberg, feel 28-degree water, sit in a life-size lifeboat, see rare Titanic photos, play a piano from the 1900s, and visit recreations of a first-class stateroom and a third-class cabin. You can also try steering on the Captain’s Bridge and view highlights from co-founder John Joslyn’s Titanic dive in the Discovery Gallery. The museum also features a full-scale replica of the ship’s Grand Staircase, built using the original blueprints.
