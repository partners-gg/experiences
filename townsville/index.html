Great Barrier Reef & MOUA: Guided Snorkeling Tour + Breakfast + Lunch

📍 Magnetic Island Cruises & Boat Tours

Visit the Great Barrier Reef for a visit to its central section, departing from Townsville or Magnetic Island. See John Brewer Reef, which is accessible for snorkelers and divers. Observe fish, turtles, rays, and other local marine species around the coral structures. Snorkelers of all experience levels can enjoy the sculptures, under the supervision of guides. Join a trip to the Museum of Underwater Art featuring the Coral Greenhouse installation by Jason de Caires Taylor, integrating artistic and reef conservation themes. Learn about reef preservation efforts and observe life within this marine environment. Introductory scuba diving with a guide is also available for those looking to dive and see the art up close.
