Kuala Lumpur Suburbs & Batu Caves: Half-Day Tour + Roundtrip Transfer

📍 Batu Caves

Visit the Batu Caves, one of Malaysia’s famous landmarks. Walk up 272 steps to reach the entrance, where monkeys often gather and a large golden statue of Lord Murugan stands 140 ft (43 m) tall. Inside, see the limestone chamber with high ceilings and Hindu shrines carefully decorated. A professional English-speaking driver guide will share the history and cultural importance of this site before you enter. You can then look around the area at your own pace. Your tour also stops at a batik workshop. Watch how traditional Malaysian batik textiles are made, using wax and dye, either by printing or by hand-drawing.

Kuala Lumpur: Private Half-Day Batu Caves and Cultural Tour

📍 Batu Caves

Join a private guided tour of the best cultural and spiritual sites of Kuala Lumpur with tickets for a half-day private tour of the area. Enjoy roundtrip transport and convenient hotel pick-up and drop-off as you journey toward Batu Caves. Follow your English-speaking guide to the Hindu shrines and beautiful structures dedicated to various religious deities. Climb the 272 steps to the golden statue of Lord Murugan and visit Thean Hou Temple, a 6-tiered pagoda temple completed in 1987 and dedicated to the goddess Tian Hou (The Heavenly Mother). Next, see the stunning National Mosque of Malaysia (Masjid Negara), embedded within acres of lush green gardens and a symbol of Malaysia's newly-acquired independence.
