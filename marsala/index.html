Saline Genna: Guided Tour

📍 Saline Genna

Join a guided tour through the salt flats, where you will learn about the salt processing methods and historical architecture. See the Stagnone Lagoon with its islands and spot the Egadi Islands in the distance. Touch the sea salt, known as "white gold." This is the first certified organic salt production site in Italy.

Wine Tasting in the historic Alagna Cellar in Marsala

📍 Cantina Alagna Vini

Discover the history and traditions of Sicilian wine-making and indulge your palate in 12 of the finest Marsala wines at the historic Alagna Cellar wine cellar. An expert representative will give you a deep-dive tour into the origins of the Alagna wine-making family, the history of the Marsala wine, and the various areas of the building, and will be on-hand to clarify any area of curiosity regarding the Sicilian wine-making sector. After your tour, you'll be seated in a comfortable tasting area, where your senses will open to a dozen of the company's Marsala wines, as you enjoy a guided tasting of each unique wine – from dry to dolce. At the end of the tour you can buy local products and wines that you can take away with you, or have them shipped directly to your home.
