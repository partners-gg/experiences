Niagara Falls (US): Guided Tour + Optional Maid of the Mist Boat Tour
Explore Niagara Falls on a guided walking tour through Niagara Falls State Park, New York. With a local guide, you’ll learn about the history and geology of the Falls as you visit the American, Bridal Veil, and Horseshoe Falls. The 60-minute walking tour takes you to scenic viewpoints on the mainland and Goat Island. You’ll also visit Luna Island, where you can stand between the American and Bridal Veil Falls for a unique view. If you choose the 90-minute tour, you’ll continue to Terrapin Point for the closest land view of Horseshoe Falls. Upgrade your visit with a ride on the Maid of the Mist for a closer look at the Falls. Feel the spray as you pass the American and Bridal Veil Falls and head to the Horseshoe Falls. During the off-season, enjoy wide views from the Niagara Falls Observation Tower instead.

Niagara Falls: Guided Tour with Boat Cruise, Cave of the Winds & Skylon Tower
Take a tour of Niagara Falls from both, the American and Canadian sides. Get picked up from your hotel in Niagara Falls, US, or Canada. Start on the US side with a guided visit to Goat Island, Luna Island, and Terrapin Point for views of the falls. Descend 175 feet into the Niagara Gorge to the Cave of the Winds, where you can feel the mist and power of Bridal Veil Falls from the Hurricane Deck. Head back to Canada for a buffet lunch at the Skylon Tower, with views from the observation deck. Drive along the Niagara Parkway, passing Dufferin Islands, the Old Scow, and Whirlpool Rapids. Stop at Table Rock for a close look at Horseshoe Falls. Finally, board the Niagara Falls boat cruise to the base of the falls for a mist-filled finale.

Niagara Falls US: Small Group Tour + Maid of the Mist Cruise + Cave of the Winds
Start with convenient pickup from your downtown hotel. Meet the guide and head to Niagara Falls, US. Begin with the Maid of the Mist boat ride. Before boarding, view the falls from the Prospect Point Observation Tower. Sail to the base of the falls, feel the mist, and hear the thunder. Ponchos are provided to keep you dry. Next, go to Goat Island and descend 175 ft into the Niagara Gorge for the Cave of the Winds. Walk the wooden platforms to the Hurricane Deck, just feet from Bridal Veil Falls, and expect to get wet. Finish the tour with a visit to Whirlpool State Park, where you’ll find the swirling rapids in a dramatic setting. Return to your hotel afterward.
