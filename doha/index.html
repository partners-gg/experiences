National Museum of Qatar: Entry Ticket + Audio Guide

📍 National Museum of Qatar

Explore the National Museum of Qatar (NMoQ) to see the work of architect Jean Nouvel, inspired by the desert rose. Learn about Qatar's history from its geological beginnings to its present-day identity. Visit three main galleries: *Beginnings*, which covers Qatar’s natural history; *Life in Qatar*, highlighting cultural traditions and everyday life; and *The Modern History of Qatar*, detailing the nation's transformation. Key exhibits include the Pearl Carpet of Baroda and multimedia displays. Enjoy interactive installations, educational workshops, cafes, and gift shops.

Museum of Islamic Art: Entry Ticket + Audio Guide

📍 Museum of Islamic Art

Visit the Museum of Islamic Art in Doha, designed by architect I.M. Pei. The museum is located along Doha’s Corniche. See a collection that covers over 1,400 years of Islamic history. Walk through galleries showcasing ceramics, metalwork, manuscripts, textiles, and jewelry from the 7th to 20th centuries. The collection includes pieces from Asia, Africa, and Europe, showing the diversity of Islamic civilization. Rotating exhibitions and interactive displays provide different ways to engage with the collection. Whether interested in art, history, or learning something new, the Museum of Islamic Art provides a comprehensive look into Islamic culture.
