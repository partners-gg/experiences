Helsinki Sightseeing Tour & Porvoo

📍 Porvoo Cathedral

Head to some of Helsinki's top cultural landmarks and historic monuments on this 6-hour guided tour! Then get a totally different vibe as you explore the beautiful nearby medieval town of Porvoo - a haven for foodies! Your six-hour tour starts in the morning with a pick up from your hotel, then it's straight out to soak up the splendor of Helsinki. Get the facts on historic landmarks, from the Olympic Stadium and Sibelius Monument to Market square and Uspenski Orthoodox Cathedral. Then, join your guide as the fun continues in the charming medieval town of Porvoo. With a quieter vibe than Helsinki itself, much of this 800-year-old town still looks as it did when it was founded! Explore the second-oldest city in Finland, check out its cathedral and Town Hall Square, Devil's Stairs and the Statue of A. Edelfelt. Foodies will love Porvoo, too, it's well known in Finland for its artisan restaurants and cafes. Get stuck in!
