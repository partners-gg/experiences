Alanya: Full-Day Sapadere Canyon Tour

📍 Alanya Outdoor Activities

Say goodbye to Alanya's sunny beaches and go on a sightseeing adventure at Sapadere Canyon! Located about 25 miles from Alanya City, Sapadere Canyon invites you to explore the scenic Taurus Mountains on sturdy wooden walkways. Witness the scenic landscape up close and let it fascinate you. Convenient pickups are available from accommodations across Alanya, taking you on a guided tour of the canyon. Appreciate a delicious lunch featuring local dishes from Sapadere village. Feel the mountain breeze and gaze at the untouched beauty of nature. Walk along the 750-meter trail and be amazed by waterfalls cascading down rocky cliffs. For the adventurous, take a refreshing dip in the cool waters. The tour also includes a visit to Guceler (Smurffs cave), where you'll discover impressive stalagmite and stalactite formations.
