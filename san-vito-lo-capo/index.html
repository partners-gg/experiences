Zingaro Nature Reserve & Scopello: Guided Boat Tour from San Vito Lo Capo

📍 Sicily - San Vito Lo Capo Cruises & Boat Tours

Join a tour of the Zingaro Nature Reserve departing from San Vito Lo Capo, see the coastline and its natural colors. Snorkel at three swim stops, explore caves by the sea, and savor a local aperitif. After viewing the Zingaro Reserve, stop at the Faraglioni di Scopello (cliffs) for an aperitif with typical local products, including water and wine. Take a final swim before returning to the starting point. Swimming in the sea is optional, you can relax on the deck while others swim.
