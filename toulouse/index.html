Cité de l'Espace: Skip The Line Ticket

📍 Cité de l'Espace

Visit Cité de l'espace in Toulouse, a space science center focused on astronomy and space exploration. It offers interactive exhibits, large outdoor displays, an IMAX cinema, and two planetariums. The site includes over 4,000 m² of exhibition space and four hectares of gardens. See life-size models of major space hardware, including the Apollo lunar module, the Mir space station, the Ariane 5 rocket, Martian rovers, and the Orion crew module. Scientific presenters lead demonstrations and talks throughout the day. **Epsilon Mission**: A mission control–style exhibition about French astronaut Sophie Adenot’s time on the ISS, covering daily life and onboard experiments, **LuneXplorer**: A 40-minute immersive simulation of a Moon mission, from launch to landing, linked to NASA’s Artemis program with the European Space Agency (ESA).

Lourdes: Small Group Guided Day Tour from Toulouse

📍 Day Trips from Toulouse

Join a guided tour from Toulouse to visit Lourdes, one of the world’s renowned pilgrimage sites. Travel in an air-conditioned minivan with a convenient pickup and drop-off in central Toulouse. On arrival, see the Sanctuary of Our Lady of Lourdes and its main sites: the Basilica of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception, the Basilica of Our Lady of the Rosary, and the Basilica of St. Pius X. Visit the Grotto of Massabielle, where the Virgin Mary appeared to Bernadette Soubirous, and gather water from the spring. After a guided tour, spend free time walking through historic streets, shopping for religious souvenirs, or having lunch (at your own expense). Finally, return to Toulouse.
