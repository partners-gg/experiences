Villa Castelli: Walking Tour with Olive Oil Tasting

📍 Villa Castelli Tours

Take a tour through the Italian town of Villa Castelli in the Apulia region. The adventure starts at Via Kalivia, at the square in front of the cemetery, where the bus stop is located. With a guide, walk through the historic center to see the Gravina, the Mother Church, and other landmarks. Visit the Pezza Petrosa Archaeological Museum, home to the ruins of an ancient village. Explore the ancient ruins at the Pezza Petrosa site and learn about the town's history. Visit a traditional olive oil factory to see how olive oil is made and sample some of their product. The factory has been producing olive oil for four generations. Learn about their oil conservation methods and taste their extra virgin olive oil on a slice of bread.
