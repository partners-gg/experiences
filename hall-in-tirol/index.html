Guided Tour: Hall in Tirol

📍 Innsbruck City Tours

The clatter of wheels on the cobblestones, the creaking of old doors, the ringing of church bells, and conversation across narrow streets, from window to window. You'll be thrown back to the atmosphere of the Middle Ages on this 1-hour guided tour of the medieval Old Town. The 700-year-old town of Hall is just 10km to the East of Innsbruck, and makes for a fascinating journey back in time! Sightseeing opportunities include the Obere Stadtplatz, the Town Hall, St Nikolaus parish church, the St Magdalena chapel and the Stiftsplat.

Guided tour of Mining Museum, Bergbaumuseum Hall in Tirol, North Tyrol

📍 Bergbaumuseum Hall in Tirol, North Tyrol

Join a guide and journey into the artificial tunnel in the old town of Hall in Tirol, in Austria's North Tyrol region. The Hall in Tirol Mining Museum is a salty world designed to wow you in the middle of Hall's old town. The tunnel, recreated from the salt mines in the Hall Valley, gives you an insight into the fascinating history of salt production following the mine's closure in 1967. Shafts, tools, minerals, a slide and much more will give you the feeling of being "underground" yourself.
