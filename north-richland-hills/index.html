📍 PEPPA PIG Theme Park Dallas-Fort Worth

Visit PEPPA PIG Theme Park for family fun. Inspired by the PEPPA PIG television series, this standalone park includes five rides and nine themed playscapes with a water play area. Try Daddy Pig’s Roller Coaster and Grampy Rabbit’s Dinosaur Adventure. Kids can check out PEPPA’s Treehouse, George’s Fort, and Rebecca Rabbit’s Playground. Splash around at Muddy Puddles Splash Pad. See live shows at Mr. Potato’s Showtime Arena and indoor cinema screenings. The park also has many kid-friendly dining options, like the Muddy Puddles Milkshake.
