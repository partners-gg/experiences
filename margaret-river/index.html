Capes Raptor Centre: Bird of Prey Encounter & Forest Walk

📍 Capes Raptor Centre

Capes Raptor Centre (founded as Eagles Heritage) is a bird of prey sanctuary dedicated to the care of injured, orphaned, or displaced birds of prey. Visitors of all ages and abilities can get closer than ever imagined to these magnificent birds of prey during twice-daily bird of prey encounters and a self-guided 1km walk through the peaceful native forest. Encounter some of the magnificent resident birds of prey up close during twice-daily flight shows. Take a 1 km bushwalk among native flora and view aviaries housing resident birds of prey. You can even take a photo with a bird! Capes Raptor Centre is located 5 km south of the Margaret River Townsite.
