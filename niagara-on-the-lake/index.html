Reif Estate Winery: Premium Wine Tasting

📍 Reif Estate Winery

Discover the story of Reif Estate, a pioneer winery founded back in 1982 by Ewald Reif and currently owned by his nephew, the passionate Klaus W. Reif. With over four decades of winemaking expertise, Reif Estate Winery crafts exceptional wines that embody the unique terrain of their vineyards, originally planted in 1977. Immerse yourself in the world of wine with their knowledgeable staff, who will guide you through a curated selection of wines while sharing fascinating insights into the winemaking process. Indulge in a range of award-winning wines, from their signature Rieslings to their rich reds and complex Icewines, all carefully crafted to showcase the best of Niagara's wine country.
