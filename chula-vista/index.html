📍 Sesame Place San Diego

Visit Sesame Place San Diego, a theme park based on the beloved *Sesame Street* show, and the first of its kind on the West Coast. The park features 18 rides themed around *Sesame Street* and numerous water activities, including a roller coaster suitable for families and a large wave pool holding 500,000 gallons of water. Walk through an interactive Sesame Street Neighborhood that includes the famous 123 Stoop and participate in engaging activities. The park also offers live character shows, a parade, and opportunities to take photos with characters from the show.

📍 Sesame Place San Diego

Calling all Elmo fans! Let us tell you how to get to Sesame Street - or at least Sesame Place San Diego. This is the second theme park of its kind in the United States and the first on the West Coast, based on Sesame Street. Kids are in for all the thrills they can handle thanks to 18 Sesame Street-themed rides and a bunch of exciting water attractions, including a family-friendly rollercoaster and a 500,000-gallon wave pool – one of the largest in Southern California. Don't miss the musical play area, an interactive Sesame Street Neighborhood complete with the iconic 123 Stoop, daily live character shows, an award-winning parade, one-of-a-kind photo opportunities and of course, everyone’s favorite furry friends.
