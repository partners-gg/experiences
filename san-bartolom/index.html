Lanzarote: El Grifo Wine Lovers Tours

📍 Bodegas El Grifo

Embark on a journey through time and immerse yourself in El Grifo's passion for wine. This is the most comprehensive wine experience, limited to 10 visitors. Stroll through the century-old chabocos, visit the original winepress, and uncover the founding plaque from 1775. Explore the family house and a unique library, inaugurated by Nobel Prize winner José Saramago. Every corner of the tour reveals a story of oenological excellence. Experience the essence of the oldest winery in the Canary Islands during your Lanzarote island tour. Enjoy an extensive and detailed wine tasting in a setting meticulously renewed with love for detail.
