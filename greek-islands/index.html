Mykonos: Yacht Cruise to Rhenia + Delos Guided Tour

📍 Mykonos Cruises

Start your day at the yacht, where the crew will give you an itinerary and safety rundown. Sail to Rhenia and Delos islands, off the coast of Mykonos. Enjoy the views and relax as you sail. At Rhenia, swim and snorkel in clear waters by the sandy beach. Have lunch onboard, choosing from pasta with Greek dressing or a Mediterranean buffet, with house wine and soft drinks included. Then, head to Delos Island for a guided tour. Explore the museum, hike Mount Kynthos, and see ancient ruins. Finish the day with a farewell drink as you sail back to Mykonos.

Mykonos: Catamaran Cruise + Meal, Drinks & Transport

📍 Mykonos Cruises

Hop on board a brand-new luxury catamaran cruise, complete with a freshly cooked 3-course meal, hand-picked wines and beers, and complimentary transport from your stay or cruise port. Glide in high style through the picturesque Cycladic landscape on an unforgettable voyage to Delos & Rhenia or the sun-kissed Mykonos Southern Coast. Starting the adventure from the serene Platis Gialos bay, the yacht will head towards the enchanting Rhenia. Here, the captain will drop anchor in a captivating bay, offering the chance to dive into the crystal-clear waters, explore the pristine landscape, or simply bask under the warm Mediterranean sun. For those who crave more excitement, the yacht's water toys are ready to turn up the fun! If the weather is on their side, the captain will steer the yacht past the island of Delos, where the passengers get an exclusive view of the UNESCO World Heritage Site and its captivating archaeological monuments.

Mykonos Beach Safari & Rhenia: Sunset Yacht Cruise + Dinner

📍 Mykonos Cruises

Begin your sailing cruise at the yacht, where a crew member will greet you and go over the itinerary and safety information. Sail along Mykonos's southern coastline, passing stunning beaches like Ornos, Psarou, Platis Gialos, Paraga, and Paradise. Enjoy the views on this beach safari. Head to the uninhabited islet of Rhenia, a secluded spot accessible only by yacht. Swim in the turquoise waters using the yacht's ladder and snorkel with gear available onboard. Dinner includes traditional Greek dishes served buffet style with complimentary wine, beers, and soft drinks. Spend time relaxing at Rhenia before sailing back to Mykonos, enjoying the sunset over the Aegean Sea.
