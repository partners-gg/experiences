Birmingham City Centre Walking Tour

📍 Birmingham City Tours

Get your bearings and discover Birmingham from a _Brummie’s_ point of view as you tour the city with a local, expert guide. Immerse yourself in the sights and sounds of the city as you travel back through the centuries to discover how the city exploded from a small market town, to the second largest city in the UK. On this 90-minute tour, you'll not only see and learn about Birmingham's history, but you’ll also gain an authentic insight into modern life in the city today, as well as exploring some hidden gems along the way! From Birmingham’s boom during the industrial revolution, to its iconic canals, and of course, the home of Cadbury chocolate! There’s something for everyone on this unique experience.
