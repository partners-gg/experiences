Queensland Museum Kurilpa: Croc! Lost Giants to Living Legends Exhibition Entry

📍 Queensland Museum Kurilpa

Walk along and see one of the most powerful animals on the planet. Learn about ancient 'supercrocs' that lived with dinosaurs and present-day crocodile species around the world. Croc! Lost Giants to Living Legends covers the connections between people and these creatures. Have close encounters with realistic croc models and interactive exhibits, including the 130-million-year-old "Sarcosuchus". See life-sized prehistoric giants and detailed fossil displays. Engage with challenges to test your knowledge of crocs and view artworks that highlight First Nations connections to Sea Country. Explore global perspectives on crocodiles in culture and tradition. Discover tools used by crocodile researchers and conservationists. You can choose to visit during "Relaxed Hours" every Monday from 14:00. Sound levels are reduced and session capacity is limited. This session is suitable for guests on the autism spectrum.
