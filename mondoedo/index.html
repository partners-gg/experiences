Cathedral de Mondoñedo: Entry Ticket + Audio Guide

📍 Cathedral de Mondoñedo

Notice the cathedral’s exterior proportions, which stand out in the main square. The façade combines architectural styles from different periods, reflecting the changes and additions made over time. Three large arches mark the three interior aisles, with the central arch featuring a 13th-century rose window made of stained glass. Inside, the chancel contains a Rococo altarpiece by Francisco Terán that presents key theological themes. The sacristy dates to the early 16th century and follows a late Gothic design, with detailed wooden drawers and copper paintings based on Old Testament scenes from Rubens’ workshop. It also includes an alabaster sculpture of the Immaculate Conception. The visit concludes with a religious art museum and a cloister built in the Herrerian style.
