Treetops Adventure: Yeodene Park

📍 Treetops Adventure Yeodene

Whether you're a kid or a grown-up, there's fun to be had at Treetops Adventure: Yeodene. Located in the native bushlands of Otway Forest, work your way up from green courses that hover five metres above the ground, to fright-inducing black courses that reach heights of 20 metres! You'll put your balance to the test as you traverse your way through the treetops. After you've taken on aerial challenges, you can zip back to earth on the park's numerous flying foxes – the longest is a whopping 100-metres long!
