Landmark 81: SkyView Entry + VR Experience

📍 Landmark 81

Landmark 81 opened in 2019 and is the tallest building in Vietnam. Visitors can access the Saigon Skyview Observation Deck on floors 79 to 81, at a height of up to 461.3 m (1,513 ft). From the deck, you can enjoy wide views of Ho Chi Minh City from about 390 m above ground. The space uses Low-E glass for clear visibility and a large-screen viewing effect. You can choose from ticket packages that include add-ons such as virtual reality games and other interactive experiences. There is also an outdoor area with LED screen effects, floor lighting, and water displays.

Fito Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 Fito Museum

Visit the Fito Museum, also known as the Museum of Vietnamese Traditional Medicine, established in 2007, spanning an area of 600 m2, and built with traditional Vietnamese architecture. Observe the intricate motifs and antique wooden door frames that originate from the Northern Delta region. Inside the museum, view reconstructed models, engravings, and a collection of authentic artifacts showcasing Vietnamese traditional medical practices, such as examination, treatment, and medicine preparation. The museum holds nearly 3,000 exhibits, ranging from the Stone Age to the modern day, and provides a perspective on Vietnam’s diverse flora and fauna. The museum's design is influenced by architectural styles from Vietnam's three key regions, presenting a considered and cultural ambiance.
