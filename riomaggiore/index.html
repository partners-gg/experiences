Riomaggiore: Pesto Cooking Class

📍 Riomaggiore Food Experience

Get ready for a gastronomic adventure of a lifetime! Picture this: breathtaking views of the sea, the tantalizing aroma of fresh basil in the air, and the anticipation of an upcoming cooking class. The journey begins with a captivating tale about pesto's humble origins in Spezia. The Cesarina reveals the age-old secret to creating the perfect pesto, starting with the selection of the freshest basil and the choicest ingredients. The goal? To craft a flavor that dances delightfully on the tastebuds. Next, it's time for a rapid-fire pesto production tutorial, showcasing two revered techniques: the traditional mortar and pestle or the futuristic electric blender. The Cesarina also discloses the trick to preserving the freshness of pesto for an extended period. As the class concludes, guests receive their homemade pesto, ready to be taken home and enjoyed. Rounding off the experience, the class offers a refreshing glass of the finest from local Ligurian Possa winery and water.
