Rhodes: 3-Hour Yellow Semi-Submarine Swim Cruise with Drinks

📍 Cruises Faliraki

Set sail on a semi-submarine swimming cruise for a memorable journey above and below the sea. Start your journey from Rhodes and stop at Traganou Caves for a 25-minute swim among rock formations. Then, head to Anthony Quinn Bay, known for its beauty, and enjoy swimming and snorkeling, and look at varieties of marine life from the submarine's underwater cabin. Enjoy refreshing drinks like chilled beer and water at the open bar during the trip.

Faliraki: 3-Hour All-Inclusive Swimming Cruise

📍 Cruises Faliraki

Hop aboard Aphrodite, a 36-foot catamaran, for a relaxing cruise under the sun filled with Greek hospitality. The voyage covers three swimming stops with plenty of drinks and snacks. First, visit Antony Quinn Bay for a 40-minute stop to swim, snorkel, or relax with a drink and snacks. Then, sail to Afantou Bay and Ladiko Bay for 30-minute stops at each location to enjoy the sun, swimming, and peaceful surroundings. Although licensed for 45 passengers, Aphrodite limits guests to 28 for maximum comfort. Choose a comfortable spot, and enjoy the open bar with wine, beer, soft drinks, water, or a daily cocktail made with fresh fruit. Furthermore, try local snacks like melekounia.
