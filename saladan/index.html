4 Islands & Emerald Cave: Boat Tour from Koh Lanta + Optional Koh Kradan Visit

📍 Day Trips from Krabi

Start your day with a hotel pickup in a shared car from Koh Lanta Yai and head to Koh Lanta's Old Town pier. At 9 AM, board a longtail boat and sail to Koh Chuek or Koh Maa. Enjoy swimming and snorkeling in its clear waters. Depending on the tide, visit the Emerald Cave at Koh Muk. Swim through the dark cave for 80 meters with a headlamp to reach a hidden beach and lagoon. Next, relax and swim at Koh Ngai while waiting for your all you can eat lunch buffet. Swim or sunbathe on the beach. Discover another amazing snorkel spot or visit Koh Kradan. Return to your hotel around 4 PM.
