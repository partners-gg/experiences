Astra Lumina: Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary

📍 Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary

Visit Astra Lumina at Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary for a light and sound show in natural bushland. This nighttime event features a multisensory display of lights, projections, and sounds. Created by Moment Factory, it is the Southern Hemisphere's first permanent night attraction of its kind. Walk the illuminated pathway and see stars descend, regenerate, and shine brightly. This event merges nature with technology, providing a unique mix of light and sound. Whether with family or friends, Astra Lumina offers an exploration of cosmic scenes under the night sky. Discover the connection between nature and technology in a way never seen before.
