Teotihuacán Pyramids & Shrine of Guadalupe: Day Tour with Lunch

📍 Teotihuacán

Your trip begins with a stop at the Square of the Three Cultures, where you'll be able to appreciate how the pre-Hispanic eras, the colonial era, and modern Mexico combine. After this, you'll travel to Teotihuacán, and see the remains of one of the first ever cities with clear planning and vision. Not only was this site an important religious center, but it was a genuine cosmopolitan center with great designs for the future. Continue your mystical adventure towards the Basilica of Our Lady of Guadalupe, the second most visited site by Catholic pilgrims around the world, located near the imposing Tepeyac hill. At the end of the day, you'll have a clear insight into how contemporary Mexican culture was formed, and a first-hand appreciation of its ancient and recent history.
