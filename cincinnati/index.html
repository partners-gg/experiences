Cincinnati Zoo & Botanical Garden: Entry Ticket

📍 Cincinnati Zoo & Botanical Garden

Visit the Cincinnati Zoo & Botanical Garden, celebrating 150 years. One of the oldest zoos in the U.S., it combines adventure, education, and conservation. See over 400 animal species and 3,000 plant species. The zoo focuses on endangered species reproduction and eco-friendly practices, earning the title of “Greenest Zoo in America.” Interact with exhibits and join educational programs serving over 450,000 participants each year. Observe big cats and learn about conservation efforts for a learning experience suited for all ages. End your visit with a walk through the botanical gardens, ideal for quiet reflection and enjoying nature.

Cincinnati Reds Hall of Fame and Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 Cincinnati Reds Hall of Fame and Museum

Explore the rich history of the first all-professional baseball club at the Cincinnati Reds Hall of Fame & Museum. With five World Series wins to their name, the Cincinnati Reds are among the most decorated teams in baseball. With these Cincinnati Reds Hall of Fame & Museum tickets, you can catch a glimpse of the Reds' remarkable 150-year history. Learn about the past and present of the Reds and their part in the history of baseball. See classic jerseys, original lockers, the evolution of baseballs, video exhibits, and much more. You can even create your own baseball card! Exploring the museum, you’ll really feel its purpose, to record, preserve and promote the rich history and traditions of the Cincinnati Reds, honor on-field accomplishments and achievements, and create an everlasting memorial to baseball and the Cincinnati Reds.

Greater Cincinnati Police Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 Greater Cincinnati Police Museum

The Greater Cincinnati Police Museum is a time capsule that's full of unique, fascinating artifacts relating to the history of law enforcement in the Greater Cincinnati area. Inside you'll be able to browse all sorts of exhibits. You'll learn about Handsome, the dog who patrolled one of the toughest beats in the city, known as “Rat Row”. You can pore over old uniforms and equipment, including pistols, polygraph machines, homemade nail bombs, and much more. The museum galleries also remember and celebrate police officers who lost their lives in the line of duty. Put yourself in a police officer's boots with the Law Enforcement Situation Simulator. You'll face real-life situations in a virtual setting to experience the split-second decisions officers make every day. Billing itself as the only regional law enforcement museum in the world, the Greater Cincinnati Police Museum is one of few police museums in the country that operates without accepting municipal or state funds.
