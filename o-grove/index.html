Rías Baixas Cruise: Route of the Mussels

📍 O Grove Cruises

Hop on board a boat and enjoy a guided tour along the Rías Baixas, a region full of beautiful landscapes with a rich tradition of fishing, shellfish, and aquaculture. This cruise along the Ría de Arousa will let you admire the coastlines of O Grove, Cambados, the Isla de La Toja, the Illa de Arousa, and more, while giving you a glimpse into the unique fishing activity found in the area. See authentic rafts and discover how mussels, oysters, and scallops are farmed. You'll also see how mussels are cooked, then taste them on board paired with a delicious local wine!

Grove Aquarium: Entry Ticket

📍 Grove Aquarium

It's an oceanic adventure with these Grove Aquarium tickets! Dive into the lives of an abundance of sea creatures on a tour through 22 colourful aquariums that represent the flora and fauna of the Galician coasts and tropical seas. You'll be guided by the explanatory panels in each aquarium. Each one has a new fascinating fact about the characteristics of the different species of each ecosystem. A swimmingly good way to learn about our finned friends and more.
