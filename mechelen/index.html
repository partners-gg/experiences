ZOO Planckendael: Entry Ticket

📍 ZOO Planckendael

Visit ZOO Planckendael and explore different continents in one day. In Asia, see great apes like orangutans, gibbons, and crested macaques. Spot Indian rhinos and elephants. In Africa, observe bonobos and hyenas. Oceania features koalas and storks flying to their nests around the park. Kids can play in various playgrounds throughout the visit.

Technopolis: Entry Ticket

📍 Technopolis

At Technopolis, you learn by doing. Try activities like VR paragliding, cycling with square wheels, and interacting with a robot. Explore the ON/OFF exhibition for VR, stop-motion, and AR, the temporary AI zone, and Biotopia’s nature-and-human themes. Kids aged 4+ can visit the Doolhoofd zone with themes based on Pluto, “protjes,” and light. Enjoy live entertainment with Rob-e the robot and electricity demos. On weekends and school holidays, catch the science show with fire and smoke effects. During the 2025–2026 school year, you can also follow the Ketnet Tour and earn a Ketnet science diploma.

Toy Museum Mechelen: Entry Ticket

📍 Toy Museum Mechelen

Visit the Toy Museum Mechelen to explore the history of toys and games. The permanent exhibition features toys from different generations and looks at questions like 'What is playing?' 'How did people play in various regions in the past?' 'Why do people play?' and 'Who plays?' You'll see a collection of medieval toys still played with in Belgium today. Use information screens and a wristband to explore the displays. Play through the museum with interactive games. Collect points with your avatar and try out different game setups designed for all ages.
