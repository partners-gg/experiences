Magic Splash

📍 MagicSplash

Step into the tropical oasis of Magic Splash and discover Playa del Sol, a sweeping beach near Rome that spans nearly 10,000 square meters. Crafted from a staggering 5,000 tons of carefully chosen, brilliant white sand that stays cool under the sun's rays, it's a true paradise out of the city, and great family fun. The star attraction? Onda del Caribe - a colossal wave pool covering 2,000 square meters, churning out waves that reach an exhilarating 1.5 meters in height. It's the perfect setting for brave wave riders and thrill-seekers. The little ones will adore Tiki Bay, an aquatic playground nestled within a huge pool. With six exciting slides reaching up to 12 meters in height, and shallow waters for little swimmers, it's a safe and thrilling water wonderland. Want to relax? The exclusive area of Playa Paraiso offers six idyllic cabanas available to rent for the day.
