The Original Valletta Walking Tour

📍 Valletta City Gate

Malta's magical capital Valletta was built by the Knights of St John in 1565, and this story-filled tour serves up a taste of its history with the facts behind its growing modern appeal. Traverse the city’s highlights in a small group, from St. John’s Co-Cathedral to the Auberge de Castille, the Church of St. Paul's Shipwreck – built when Christianity came to town – and St. George’s Square, home of the opulent Grand Master’s Palace. Your guide will bring this World Heritage City to life before your eyes, diving into the details behind every Baroque masterpiece as old architecture blends with the new. If you don't know where to start in Valletta, start with this ticket to the best bits, and see life through the eyes of the locals, past and present. You'll be charmed in no time!

Valletta Self-Guided Audio Tour

📍 Malta Self-Guided Tours

Immerse yourself in the culture and history of Malta with a self-guided audio tour of Valletta. Tickets for this Valletta audio tour allow you to explore at your pace so you can savor the city, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Learn fascinating facts about iconic city landmarks – the City Gate, the Upper Barrakka Gardens, and Fort St. Elmo. With unlimited access to the audio guide during your stay, you can pinpoint your favorite spots and generate a multitude of walking routes for the best photo opportunities in Valletta.

Private Malta Airport Transfer

📍 Airport Transfers Malta

Whether you're planning a fun-filled family holiday or a quick business trip, make your transportation from Malta Airport easy and stress-free with the help of a private transfer.
