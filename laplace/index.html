New Orleans: Guided Swamp Zipline Tour

📍 Zip NOLA

Discover the world's first fully aquatic swamp zipline and a popular landmark of New Orleans. Over half a mile of ziplines and suspension bridges take you through the Maurepas Swamp, offering an exciting experience for nature lovers and adventure seekers. See swamp critters and a unique ecosystem from the treetops. Glide above the swamp, spotting alligators, turtles, and other wildlife. After the zipline, visit a local gift shop with New Orleans souvenirs, snacks, and beverages. Explore the enclosure for baby alligators and turtles. Enjoy the spacious restrooms, locker storage, green space picnic area, and an elevated seating area with scenic views of the Maurepas Swamp.
