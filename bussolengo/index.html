Parco Natura Viva: Open Date Ticket

📍 Parco Natura Viva

Visit Natura Viva Park in Bussolengo, near Lake Garda, a zoo and conservation center dedicated to protecting endangered species. Spanning 42 hectares, it houses 1,500 animals from 250 species, making it Italy’s largest zoological collection within the EAZA. Explore two main areas. Drive through the **Africa Safari** to see lions, giraffes, zebras, and hyenas up close. Walk through the **Faunistic Park**, which showcases ecosystems from five continents and is home to Siberian tigers, snow leopards, chimpanzees, vultures, and lemurs. Discover other highlights, including the **House of Dragon**, a greenhouse with species from around the world, such as Komodo dragons. Step back in time at **Jurassic Adventure**, featuring 35 life-sized dinosaur models. Support wildlife conservation through the park’s 109 scientific studies and 35 global projects. Join educational talks, events, and activities designed to raise awareness and inspire action.
