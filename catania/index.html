Aci Trezza: Sailboat Tour from Catania + Aperitif

📍 Day Trips from Catania

Enjoy a boat trip along the coastline to Aci Trezza, a small seaside village known for its unique archipelago. These formations were created by land elevation movements and offer a distinctive coastal view. Aci Trezza is also the setting for Giovanni Verga's novel "I Malavoglia." Take a break for a swim or snorkeling to see the vibrant marine life in this protected area. Visit the Castle of Acicastello, a historical site located on a cliff overlooking the sea. Enjoy an onboard aperitif of chilled prosecco with local cheese, salami, and stuffed croutons. End the day with a scenic sail back to the city of Catania.
