Kornati by Speed Boat

📍 Split Outdoor Activities

Experience the stunning natural beauty of Kornati National Park – one of Croatia's many hidden gems – by island-hopping in a speedboat. Escape the crowds and skim the translucent turquoise water, soak in the sun on the Adriatic Sea, and allow the skipper to whisk you away from your worldly worries. On your journey through Kornati National Park, you'll relax around several islands with the option to leisurely float or go for a swim. Enjoy a complimentary lunch package – selected just for you – and feel time stand still while you watch the clouds sail by. Stay on the water for the spectacular sunset view from St. Anthony Channel to conclude your visit to Kornati National Park.

Cetina River: Rafting & Cliff Jumping

📍 Split Outdoor Activities

Get to know the Cetina Canyon from a whole new perspective as you raft down a ten-kilometer stretch of river! Along the way, stop for swimming and try out a thrilling cliff jump from up to six meters high! During this three-hour trip, you’ll get to paddle along the river and see some of Croatia’s most stunning scenery, before taking a five-kilometer-long ride down river rapids. The most impressive part of your trip is the chance to make a dramatic entrance into a secluded cave by rafting beneath a waterfall! Inside, stalactites and stalagmites grow around a spring water lake, and you’ll have the chance to swim in its crystal-clear water.
