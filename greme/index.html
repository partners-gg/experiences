GÖREME ·GÖREME OPEN-AIR MUSEUM
Goreme Open Air Museum Guided Walking Tour – 1.5 Hours
The Göreme Open Air Museum, one of Turkey's first UNESCO World Heritage Sites, is a group of monasteries in the heart of Cappadocia. The museum's cave homes are part of the landscape, presenting interesting views and rich history. First, you'll have lunch at a local cafe. Then, a certified guide will lead your tour. They'll talk about the history of the Byzantine monastic settlement and show you important places like churches, cave homes, rock formations, and more. You'll learn about the monks' daily lives and find out more about this UNESCO World Heritage Site from your guide.
