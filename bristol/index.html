Lake Compounce Amusement & Water Park: Entry Ticket

📍 Lake Compounce Amusement & Water Park

Visit Lake Compounce Water & Amusement Park for family fun. Explore a mix of classic rides, kiddie rides, rollercoasters, and water attractions. Try Wildcat, a historic ride that has been entertaining visitors for almost 100 years. Ride the Storm Surge, a long waterslide starting at a lighthouse that sends you speeding through a colorful enclosed tube. Enjoy Thunder N Lightning, a ride that creates a sensation of weightlessness at 60 mph and 90 feet in the air. Board the Mammoth Falls, a raft ride for the whole family that takes you down a thrilling descent.
