LEGOLAND® New York Resort: Entry Ticket

📍 LEGOLAND® New York Resort

LEGOLAND New York Resort is more than an amusement park. With seven themed lands set across 150 acres, it's like stepping into another world. The park is home to many rides, shows, and attractions. There are thrilling, kid-friendly rollercoasters as well as a host of LEGO workshops, a 4D cinema, and much more! Block out the day, and get lost in New York's enormously vibrant world of LEGO. Visit the LEGO® DUPLO® PEPPA PIG Playground where the popular world of PEPPA PIG is merged with the LEGO DUPLO universe in a play area. Meet Peppa and George on their puppet theater stage. Explore Peppa’s tree house, the slide, and the muddy puddles. Also, you can hop aboard Grandpa Pig’s boat.
