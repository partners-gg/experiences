Warwick Castle: Entry Ticket

📍 Warwick Castle

Done with your Shakespearean sightseeing in Stratford-Upon-Avon? Get your **Warwick Castle** tickets – it's bursting at the turrets with medieval fun! It's centuries of fun, all in one place!

Warwickshire Immersive Self-Guided Walking Tours

📍 Warwick Castle

Discover Warwickshire's web of intriguing history, with two self-guided walking tours of Warwick and Stratford-Upon-Avon. Local historian Katie, and her friend Colm will provide podcast-style, informal audio commentary as you wander the vibrant streets of Warwick, learning all manner of fascinating facts and anecdotes. From William Shakespeare’s birthplace and the RSC & Swan Theatre in Stratford to Warwick Castle and the River Avon in Warwick, these tours are perfect for anyone interested in Celtic settlements, Saxon invasion, medieval warfare, Shakespeare’s plays, and the legacy of traditional towns in modern Britain. The tours are flexible, Covid-safe and independent, which allows you to discover Warwickshire at your own pace.
