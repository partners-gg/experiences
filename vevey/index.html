Alimentarium - Food Museum Vevey

📍 Alimentarium Food Museum

Brimming with vibrant animations and captivating cooking, this experience plunges you into the scrumptious sphere of sustenance. From the birth of a dish to its cultural impact, the exhibition offers an immersion into the flavorful essence of life itself. Wander through the botanical wonderland of the Alimentarium Garden, a crucial thread in the museum's rich tapestry. Tucked away in an enchanting spot with a stunning lakeside view, the garden is alive with fragrant herbs, juicy vegetables, robust grains, and a host of other plants that form the backbone of the human diet. At the core of the museum lies the Discovery Area, a riveting space spotlighting the industrial might behind the world's food production. Here, observe culinary experiments in real-time, interact with industry professionals, and partake in enlightening food and nutrition discussions. Mmm, tasty!
