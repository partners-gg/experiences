Plopsa Indoor Coevorden: Entry Ticket

📍 Plopsa Indoor Coevorden

The most beautiful indoor theme park in the Netherlands is celebrating its anniversary! Join your favorite Studio 100 heroes to celebrate 15 years of Plopsa Indoor Coevorden and enjoy 25 indoor and outdoor attractions. Spin around on the wild Anubis ride or go on an adventurous journey on the back of a real frog! Next to the Bumba's Merry-Go-Round, you can burn off all your energy in Bumba’s Playground, where you can climb, slide, crawl, and spin on the colorful play structures with Bumba and Bumbalu. Then, let loose and dance with K3 in the K3-Disco. Be sure to hop aboard the famous Crooked Barge and fire a cannonball like a true pirate. For the bravest adventurers, The Vikings’ Rollercoaster offers a fun time as you zoom past rocky cliffs!
