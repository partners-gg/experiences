Start your tour with a morning pickup from your chosen location, and relax in a private, air-conditioned vehicle on the way to Sanjay Gandhi National Park. Explore Mumbai’s tourist hotspots by visiting top attractions like the Taj Mahal Palace, Dhobi Ghat, and Town Hall. See the grand Victoria Terminus, Municipal Building, and stroll along Marine Drive.
