Levi's Stadium: San Francisco 49ers Football Game

📍 Levi’s Stadium

Watch the San Francisco 49ers play an American football game at Levi's Stadium. Use your game ticket for assigned seating and catch the live action on the field as top NFL stars compete. Located in Silicon Valley, Levi's Stadium features 1,700 high-tech beacons to help with seat directions and notify you about specials at concession stands. Get a smartphone app along with the ticket to find bathrooms with the shortest lines, watch replays, and order food and drinks delivered to your seat. The stadium offers a variety of concessions, facilities, and matchday entertainment.
