Fátima Sanctuary: Small-Group Day Trip from Lisbon

📍 Shrine of Our Lady of Fátima

Fatima is one of the largest sanctuaries in the world, with more than 5 million visitors each year. After arriving at Fatima, you'll start your spiritual journey with a visit to the workshop. Shop for rosaries and other religious articles for the blessing at the Shrine of Fatima. Visit the specific place where the Virgin Mary appeared to the three young shepherds in 1917 and entrusted her three precious secrets. Visit the Chapel of the appearances and if you like, attend mass. You'll see the statue of the Virgin Mary, standing on a pedestal. Visit the village and the home of the three young shepherds, and examine their lives through family photographs, clothes, kitchen utensils, furniture, and more.
