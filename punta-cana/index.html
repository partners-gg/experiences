Bavaro Park: Buggy + Zipline

📍 Bavaro Adventure Park

Not every beach holiday has to be spent lounging on the sand with the occasional dip in the sea to cool down. A trip to Bavaro Adventure Park adds a thrill to your chill. Get ready for a dune buggy tour around the beautiful Dominican coast! This educational trip avoids all public roads and takes a scenic route with plenty of nature to see along the way. Learn about the local plants – including orchids and cacti – and keep an eye out for butterflies. After exploring the coast, get ready for your second activity - a zipline ride over scenery that will blow your mind, and one thrilling splash-landing in a waterfall pool!

Bavaro Park: Zipline Mega Splash

📍 Bavaro Adventure Park

Our tests show an unusually high amount of adrenaline around the Dominican Republic. The reason? Bavaro Adventure Park's Zipline Mega Splash! This high-flying ride takes you rushing through leafy treetops, and dunks you feet-first into cool waters. Soar along the canopy and feel your face flapping majestically in the breeze, clamber across the Himalayan Bridge, ride the Splash Cable, and plunge with an almighty plop into the pool below. Tarzan noises are optional. After your adrenaline levels stabilize, you can chill out in a relaxing waterfall pool, and listen to the tranquil jungle sounds. Bliss!
