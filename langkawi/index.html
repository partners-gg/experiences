LANGKAWI ·LANGKAWI WILDLIFE PARK
Langkawi Wildlife Park: Entry Ticket
Take the whole tribe for a day of up-close and personal encounters with stars from 150 species. Meet canaries, owls, eagles, toucans, hornbills, flamingos and more. Langkawi Wildlife Park is an animal-packed sanctuary with a tropical garden that's filled with the sounds and squawks of its 1,500 free-flying exotic birds! If you want to get even closer, step into the walk-in aviary, or check out one of the feeding sessions where kids can help to feed donkeys, deer, eagles and pelicans under the supervision of the park's experienced handlers.

LANGKAWI ·UNDERWATER WORLD LANGKAWI
Underwater World Langkawi: Entry Ticket
Take a deep dive into Underwater World Langkawi and educate yourself on marine life at one of the largest marine and freshwater aquariums in South East Asia! Built with the aim of raising awareness of the importance of conservation, you might come away feeling a deeper bond with nature. Learn about the lives and native domains of over 200 marine and freshwater species, catch a movie in the 3D theatre and pull a funny face at the fish on the other side of the glass in the cool underwater tunnel!

LANGKAWI ·MEGA WATERSPORTS
Langkawi Dayang Bunting 8 Islands Jet Ski Tour
Welcome to Dayang Bunting (meaning Pregnant Maiden), the star of the Langkawi archipelago and one of eight islands you'll visit on this thrilling four-hour jet ski tour. Explore the hills and rocky outcrops of Dayang Bunting on your own; there's plenty of time for sightseeing while your guide watches the jet skis. Trek in the lush jungle, go kayaking, rent a paddle boat, or go for a dip in the freshwater lake. The lake, which is steeped in myth, is believed to have fertility powers!
