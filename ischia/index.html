Ischia: Poseidon Thermal Gardens Entry Ticket

📍 Ischia Poseidon Thermal Gardens

Step into a relaxed setting at Ischia Poseidon Thermal Gardens. Noted for its thermal hot springs, it provides a peaceful retreat, set against the background of the Mediterranean. The warm healing waters are a feature, in a place where you're encouraged to take your time. The Poseidon Thermal Gardens stands out as the largest thermal park in Ischia. Over twenty pools, fed by thermal waters known for their healing properties, are found here, hidden amidst greenery. Temperatures vary from 28° C to 40° C. Also available are three sea water pools, a thermal steam grotto based on Greek-Roman tradition, three "Kneipp" groups for aiding circulation, and a cardiovascular exercise course.

Aragonese Castle Ischia: Entry Ticket with Hydrofoil + Transfer from Naples

📍 Ischia Aragonese Castle

Depart from Naples and reach Ischia by hydrofoil. Visit the Aragonese Castle, a historic fortress on a volcanic islet, linked to Ischia by an ancient stone bridge. Built in 474 BC and expanded in 1441, the castle offers a look into a rich history of conquests and intrigue. See the views of the sea and surrounding areas. Walk through ancient churches, medieval buildings, and gardens. View exhibitions that explain the island's past. The Aragonese Castle offers you a chance to see its cultural and historical significance on your own schedule.
