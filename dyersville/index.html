📍 National Farm Toy Museum

Visit a museum designed for kids of all ages! The National Farm Toy Museum in Dyersville, Iowa, aka “The Farm Toy Capital of the World,” is the place to visit to reap some serious farmyard fun! With thousands of toys, exhibits, and displays scattered throughout, the museum lets visitors explore the ways in which farming has changed over the years, and the history of the tractors that help make successful harvests happen. The museum has all of your favorite brands, like John Deere, Case IH, and Massey Ferguson, among numerous others. You don't have to know much about tractors or farming to enjoy this fun, educational and interactive experience, so plan your visit today!
