LOISIUM WineExperience: Fast Track Ticket

📍 LOISIUM WineExperience

With its modern vinotheque and the cellar worlds below, LOISIUM WeinWelt—a spectacular design conceived by New York architect Steven Holl—takes you on an exciting journey. Join an unforgettable wine experience with an interactive audio guide leading you on a tour through 900-year-old wine cellars. A fascinating and entertaining journey through time and the history of wine that will take you to interactive stations and offer you a glimpse into the cellars of the Steininger winery. The intriguing world of WeinWelt holds numerous highlights—including 1.5km of barrel-lined subterranean corridors with 18 stations and a vintner’s house from 1924, giving you an idea of the life and winemaking the past. To finish off the tour, taste some of the fine wine at the vinotheque and shop.
