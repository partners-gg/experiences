Pellestrina: Guided Venetian Lagoon Boat Tour

📍 Chioggia Boat Tours

Set off from Chioggia for a relaxing journey through the Venetian Lagoon in a small group. Led by a knowledgeable skipper, you'll pass the Ca' Roman nature reserve, home to diverse birdlife, and see the historic Murazzi walls built to protect the lagoon from the sea. Continue through the lagoon, spotting mussel farms and fishermen’s stilt houses, showcasing the area's cultural heritage. Stop at Pellestrina, known for its colorful houses lining the water. Enjoy this unique view of the lagoon and capture memorable moments along the way.
