LUXOR ·LUXOR TEMPLE
Luxor & Karnak Temples: Private Day Tour + Transfer + Lunch
Meet a guide at your hotel and visit the Karnak Temple, the largest religious complex in ancient Egypt. Walk through its Hypostyle Hall, view the Sacred Lake, and see the Avenue of Sphinxes. Learn about its historical significance. Then, head to the Luxor Temple, a riverside monument once linked to Karnak by the Avenue of Sphinxes. Look at its well-preserved columns and statues while the guide explains its role in ancient rituals. Afterward, have lunch at a local restaurant with authentic Egyptian food.

LUXOR ·DAY TRIPS FROM LUXOR
Luxor: Guided Day Tour to West and East Banks
Start the day with a pickup from your hotel. A representative will pick you up in a private air-conditioned car and take you to the West Bank. Visit the Valley of the Kings, where you can explore the tombs of ancient Egyptian kings such as Thutmose I, Thutmose III, Tutankhamun, Ramesses VI, Merneptah, and Amunhotep II. Next, head to the temple of Queen Hatshepsut, also known as Deir el-Bahari. This temple highlights the reign of Queen Hatshepsut, the only female pharaoh of the 18th dynasty. Then, see the Colossus of Memnon and the remains of the mortuary temple of Amenhotep III.

LUXOR ·DAY TRIPS FROM LUXOR
Luxor: City Tour
Step into a world of awe-inspiring history and embark on a journey to the city of Luxor, home to a third of the world's most magnificent monuments. The journey begins with a visit to the Karnak Temple, a testament to the grandeur of ancient Egypt and a marvel of construction that predates modern tools. Next, the tour whisks you off to the Temple of Hatshepsut, a tribute to one of Egypt's most extraordinary queens. There, capture timeless memories at photographic hot spots like the Valley of the Kings and the statuesque Colossi of Memnon. But the adventure doesn't stop there! The tour makes a pit stop at Banana Island for a bite of fresh bananas straight from the Nile's fertile land. While there, indulge in a unique shopping experience with gifts crafted from Alabaster stone. The talented local artisans can even whip up a custom statue of your choice on the spot! To top it all off, feast on the most delectable Egyptian cuisine, with a menu that lets you pick and choose your favorite dishes. This unforgettable tour is an experience of a lifetime, so don't miss out!
