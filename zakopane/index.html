Zakopane Tatra Mountains Private Full Day Guided Tour

📍 The Gubałówka Funicular Railway - Ground Station

Ready for an adventure, while laying eyes on some of Poland's prettiest scenery? This private Zakopane tour from Krakow takes about 8-9 hours and is all about you. Get collected between 8-10 am and settle in for a 1.5-2 hour journey to Zakopane. This comfy minibus cruise will be made even more enjoyable by your driver and guide, who'll explain all you need to know about Poland, any landmarks you pass, and the country's customs. Once you get to Zakopane, you and your guide will visit the sculptor’s house in Chocholow village, Gubalowka Hill, Krupowki Street and Ski Jumping Hill, all while the majestic Tatra Mountains smile over you. Any questions, just ask The itinerary can always be adapted to meet your desires.
