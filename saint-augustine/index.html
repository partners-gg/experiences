St. Augustine Aquarium: Entry Ticket

📍 St. Augustine Aquarium

Prepare to be mesmerized by the captivating world of marine biology at the St. Augustine Aquarium. Nestled on Rt. 16, a mere hop and skip from I-95, this landlocked aquarium is a slice of oceanic paradise that promises to leave guests spellbound. Immerse yourself in the wonders of the deep with a plethora of indoor and open-air exhibits that offer a glimpse into the aquatic universe. Guests can embark on a snorkeling adventure in an 80,000-gallon tank that teems with hundreds of Florida reef fish, including the majestic angelfish, blue-lined grunts, and high-hats. The knowledgeable staff at the St. Augustine Aquarium is passionate about sharing their love for marine biology and invites guests to explore the sea and its inhabitants. Guests can embark on a thrilling adventure to search for seahorses hiding in the seagrass, or touch starfishes, horseshoe crabs, and other invertebrates.

St. Augustine Old Jail: After Dark Tour

📍 St. Augustine Old Jail

Join a team for a paranormal investigation of the St. Augustine Old Jail. Old Jail is recognized as one of the most haunted buildings in the country and shrouded in mystery and lore. The historic building housed prisoners from 1891 to 1953 when it was closed due to its harsh conditions and cruelty. Multiple hangings were carried out on this site in addition to many other deaths that can only be described as unnatural. These are the reasons that leads many to believe it is a hotspot for paranormal activity and become a popular destination for ghost hunters everywhere. During the investigation, you'll be provided with and trained to use traditional and modern ghost-hunting equipment. You can also bring your own gear. Feel the thrill of searching for evidence of ghosts and other supernatural beings at this famous location.
