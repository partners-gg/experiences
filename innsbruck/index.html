Swarovski Crystal Worlds: Entry Ticket

📍 Swarovski Crystal Worlds

Swarovski Kristallwelten is located in Wattens, about 20 km from Innsbruck. The venue features the Chambers of Wonder, where artists and designers present crystal-inspired installations. Throughout the year, special seasonal events take place, including a Winter Wonderland during the Christmas season, Easter Magic in spring, and circus performances in summer. One of the themed spaces, *The Art of Performance*, displays original costumes and stage pieces inspired by Hollywood productions. Visitors can also explore the Giant’s Park, which combines art installations with outdoor spaces.

Alpenzoo + Hungerburgbahn

📍 Alpenzoo Innsbruck

Innsbruck's Alpenzoo is Europe's highest zoo, at 750m above ground level, and its function as a themed zoo is unique in the world. The zoo covers a total of 4 hectares and houses approximately 2,000 alpine animals from around 150 species in natural enclosures, terrariums and aquariums. This ticket to the Alpenzoo includes transport; you can comfortably reach the zoo from the city center with the scenic Hungerburgbahn. In just a few minutes, you can get from the Congress station to the Alpenzoo station – from there the zoo is only a few meters away. Please note that dogs are not allowed in the zoo!

Ambras Castle: Skip The Line

📍 Schloss Ambras

Want to know what royal hygiene was like in the 16th century? Skip the line and find out at Ambras Castle, which offers the chance to see the only preserved bathing facility left from that era! See several amazing art collections, the intricately decorated Spanish Hall, and step back to Innsbruck's imperial past.

Roundtrip Cable Car: Top of Innsbruck

📍 Innsbrucker Nordkettenbahnen - Nordkette

The 2300-meter-high Hafelekar is now easier to reach than ever before, thanks to unique and impressive cable car connection. In only a few minutes, you'll be able to admire beautiful mountain views as you ascend your way up to the top. Keep an eye out for the local wildlife – usually too shy to say hello – and admire the spectacular Karwendel Mountains, Austria's largest nature park as you climb up into the skies. Your journey departs from Congress station, and will only take a few minutes to bring you to the Top of Innsbruck in a safe and scenic cable car.

Roundtrip Funicular Ride: Innsbruck to Hungerburgbahn

📍 Innsbrucker Nordkettenbahnen - Nordkette

The stations of the Hungerburgbahn, designed by architectural superstar Zaha Hadid, are now characteristic of Innsbruck's cityscape and represent the most modern architecture found in the Alpine region. Based on alpine glaciers and their bluish ice formations, the four Hungerburgbahn stations are some of the most unique in the world. Only eight minutes out from the city center, you'll be enjoying wonderful views over Innsbruck. The cable car museum in the station building of the Seegrubenbahn takes you into the history of the Nordkettenbahnen, while the Theresienkirche station is only a few steps away from the viewing platform.

Tyrolean Evening Show + Optional Dinner

📍 Tyrolean Evening Show in the Alpensaal

Prepare for a merry evening of Austrian music, dancing, yodeling, and... shoe slapping? The Gundolf family have been wowing crowds with traditional Tyrolean folk performances for over 50 years, so they know a thing or two about lederhosen-clad showmanship. So sit back, sip a complimentary drink, and enjoy a riotous night of knees-up fun in Innsbruck. Feeling peckish? You can also choose to enjoy a mouthwatering three-course meal to your ticket.

Swarovski Crystal Worlds Store Innsbruck: Guided Tour + Drink

📍 Swarovski Crystal Worlds

Visit the Swarovski Crystal Worlds Store in Innsbruck, located near the Golden Roof in the city's historic Old Town. A Swarovski Crystal expert will guide you through a 30-minute tour, showcasing various crystal installations that highlight Swarovski's craftsmanship. See the Timeless Wall, where key pieces and stories illustrate the brand’s history. Go to the first floor via the crystal staircase and see the Cascade Chandelier, featured in the movie Ocean’s 13. After the tour, enjoy a drink in the bar area. Before leaving, walk through the full collection, including jewelry, accessories, SWAROVSKI OPTIK products, and unique art pieces. Take home a special crystal keepsake from your visit.
