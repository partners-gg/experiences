Wave Island: Entry Ticket

📍 Wave Island

Located in Monteux, 10 minutes from Avignon, the Wave Island waterpark welcomes you to enjoy its assortment of slides, a lazy river, and splash zones for little ones. Hop in a rubber ring and float the 650 m long Rivière Tropicale. Feel the speed on Wave Island's high-octane slides. See a replica of the Tokyo Olympics' training pool for surf events. Surf with Marsupilami on the world's largest artificial wave and have a great time in restaurants.
