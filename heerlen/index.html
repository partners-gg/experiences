Thermenmuseum: Entry Ticket + Audio Guide

📍 Thermenmuseum

The Thermenmuseum, with its ancient Roman bathhouse, is perfect for history-hungry visitors who want to delve into the Roman history of the Netherlands. Walk inside the enormous, factory-like structure that protects its incredibly preserved ruins. Admire a 500-meter Roman wall and delicate artifacts galore, as an audio guide introduces you to life under Roman rule. Discover the role of thermal baths in Roman society, and see them come to life before your eyes with projections and a dazzling 3D light show. There's also the '400 Years of Bathing' exhibition, which traces the site's history and includes a 360° video experience placing you at the baths through the ages.
