Van Gogh Etten-Leur: Experience and Museum Entry

📍 Van Gogh Kerk

Etten-Leur is where Vincent van Gogh began his artistic career at the age of 28. It was here he opened his first studio and registered as a painter. Visit the Van Gogh Church, where he often came and where his father served as pastor for 7 years. The multimedia Van Gogh Experience offers a 15-minute show with life-size projections, powerful sound, and imagery. Experience how he thought, dreamed, drew, and painted. At the Sint Paulushofje Museum, located in a picturesque courtyard, see how people lived and worked in Van Gogh's time and beyond.
