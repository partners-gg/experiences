Mödling Vienna: Alpaca & Llama Hiking Tour

📍 Vienna Hiking Activities

Go on a delightful journey through the green landscapes of Eichkogel/Anninger Mödling, just moments from Vienna. Join a friendly group of Alpacas, including Gandalf, Legolas, and Aragorn, along with charming Llamas like Frodo and Bilbo. These lovable animals will be your guides on an extraordinary group expedition. The trek lasts for a refreshing hour and a half, with an additional half-hour for animal bonding and a 15-minute break. Junior explorers aged 9 and above can even lead an animal themselves, while those aged 5 to 8 can share the task with an adult. Younger adventurers are welcome but should bring their own carriers. Children under 9 can join the fun for free, accompanied by an adult companion.
