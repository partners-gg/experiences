Sardinia in Miniature

📍 Parco Sardegna in Miniatura

Feel like a giant at Sardinia in Miniature! While the star attraction is a miniature Sardinia, there's also a fascinating world of dinosaurs, predators, and sharks to explore. That's not all though, there's also many species of parrots to see, and the chance to learn about the customs and traditions of the inhabitants of Sardinia 3,000 years ago. You can even travel among the stars and discover the solar system in the planetarium.
