From Málaga: Day Trip to Gibraltar

📍 Saint Michael's Cave

Get a taste of the unique multicultural mix of Gibraltar with tickets for a full-day excursion from Málaga, complete with roundtrip transport. Begin your day trip to Gibraltar in Málaga on a comfy bus as you make your way down the gorgeous Spanish coastline of Costa del Sol. Your multilingual guide will tell you anecdotes and curiosities surrounding Gibraltar, a British territory with a fascinating and complex past. Once you've reached the border checkpoint, you'll be greeted by Gibraltar's dominant landmark – the Rock of Gibraltar. Make your way through tunnels and over the cliffsides of Europe's southernmost point as you ascend to the limestone fortress, established in the Middle Ages by the Moors! At the top of the Rock, soak up the panoramic views of the Strait of Gibraltar that straddles both Europe and Africa. Explore the Cave of San Miguel and spot Gibraltar's most famous resident – the Barbary macaque – a must-see on this day trip to Gibraltar from Málaga!

Rock of Gibraltar: Day Trip from Málaga

📍 Saint Michael's Cave

Located around two hours south of Málaga is a little patch of Britain with a twist. While it might have Queen Elizabeth as its sovereign, this territory has its own unique culture waiting for you to explore. Alongside air-conditioned return transport from Málaga, you'll also get a guided tour of Gibraltar. Visit the Rock of Gibraltar, and what lies beyond and beneath – the Punta de Europa and San Miguel's cave. Keep an eye out for Barbary macaques while you're there!
