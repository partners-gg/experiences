Malta: Gozo Full-Day ATV Tour

📍 Outdoor Activities in Gozo

Take a full-day ATV tour on the island of Gozo. Visit the Mgarr Harbour, Qala Belvedere, Simar Valley, and the Ggantija Temples. Stop at the Citadel in Victoria and swim at Xwejni Bay. Pass the Xlendi Cliffs, with views of the cliffs meeting the Mediterranean Sea. Visit Dwejra, home to unique coastal formations like the Inland Sea and the site of the former Azure Window. Drive past the Ta Pinu Sanctuary, known for its healing history and architecture. Stop at traditional salt pans, active for centuries in sea salt harvesting.
