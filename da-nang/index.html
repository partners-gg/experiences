Hoi An City & Cam Thanh Coconut Village: Guided Tour from Da Nang + Dinner

📍 Outdoor Activities in Da Nang

Visit Hoi An, an ancient town in Central Vietnam. See a blend of Vietnamese, Chinese, Japanese, and European architecture. Walk through lantern-lit streets that glow with colorful paper lanterns. Taste the local cuisine and find tailor-made clothing. Learn about the town's cultural heritage. Nearby, explore Cam Thanh Coconut Village. Discover dense mangrove forests and traditional water coconut palms. Take boat tours through the canals and observe the local fishing culture. Engage in eco-friendly activities like paddling small basket boats and learning about the area's history and biodiversity.
