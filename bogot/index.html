Guatavita: Guided Day Tour from Bogotá in Spanish
Visit Laguna de Guatavita (Guatavita Lagoon) in the town of Sesquilé, once home to the Chibcha people. It was here that the famous Muisca ceremony of El Dorado occurred. Learn about the history of this ceremony and how it shaped the country's past. After your visit to the lagoon, take time to explore the town, including its Plaza de Toros (bullring). You'll see famous sites and hear the story of how the original town was submerged by the Tominé Reservoir. Visit La Casa Loca, a unique upside-down house perfect for fun photos.
