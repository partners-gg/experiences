Phoenix des Lumières: Prehistoric Planet - The Immersive Experience

📍 Phoenix des Lumières

Visit the *Prehistoric Planet - The Immersive Experience* exhibition to learn about life in the prehistoric era. Using advanced projection technology over an approximately 2,200 m2 area, step into a depiction of the world where T. rex dinosaurs, Pterosaurs, and Mosasaurs inhabit land, sky, and water. This exhibit is located in Dortmund, within the Phoenix des Lumières cultural space, housed in a historical industrial site. Built within a repurposed former mill, this digital art center hosts significant exhibitions focusing on both art history and engaging projects for visitors.
