Morro Jable: Dolphin & Whale Watching Tour

📍 Boat Tours in Morro Jable

Join a dolphin and whale-watching trip from Morro Jable harbor in Fuerteventura. Meet at designated points and enjoy a comfortable transfer to Port Morro Jable. Hop on a speedboat and cruise along the coastline to the Jandia peninsula, spotting dolphins, whales, and other marine life. Enjoy panoramic views of the mountains, clear waters, and beautiful beaches. After the tour, return to Morro Jable harbor and get a transfer back to your pickup location.

Morro Jable: Glass-Bottom Boat Tour + Lunch

📍 Boat Tours in Morro Jable

Prepare for a boat tour along the beautiful coastlines of Janía Natural Park on Odysee 3, a glass-bottom boat for dolphin and whale watching. Enjoy hassle-free hotel pickup before setting sail to spot these marine creatures. Relax under the sun, feel the sea breeze, and groove to music onboard. Take a break to snorkel, kayak, paddle surf, or swim in the clear blue waters. At lunchtime, enjoy a delicious Canarian meal. Watch seagulls glide above and explore the underwater viewing area onboard. As the day ends, return to shore and head back to your hotel in south Fuerteventura or your pickup point.
