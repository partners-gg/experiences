Tierra del Fuego National Park and End of the World Train: Tour from Ushuaia

📍 Tierra del Fuego National Park

Tierra del Fuego means 'Land of Fire'. Don't fret; there are no dragons here, just stunning vistas and endless natural beauty. With a half-day tour from Ushuaia, one of Earth's southernmost cities, you'll be introduced to the history and glorious surrounds of an island at the end of the world. Pass the blue lakes and lagoons of National Route 3, gaze at Beagle Channel, and take in Ushuaia Peninsula. Inside the park, you'll visit the serene Acigami Lake and end your adventure at Lapataia Bay.
