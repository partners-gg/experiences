Convento Museo Las Claras: Entry Ticket + Audio Guide

📍 Convento Museo Las Claras

Visit the Convento Museo Las Claras, a historical site featuring two distinct choirs. The upper choir allows close viewing of its medieval wooden ceiling. The lower choir complements the experience. Enjoy observing a collection of murals discovered in the late 20th century and exploring the tower, which offers a view of Salamanca, highlighting its historical buildings. After the renovation managed by the Fundación Las Edades del Hombre, the convent now operates as Museo de Pintura Medieval. Inside the museum, observe the mural paintings and the polychrome roof of the church, crafted in the late 14th century. Tour the convent's spaces to view its art pieces and devotional objects that belonged to the religious community.

Monterrey Palace: Self Guided Audio Tour

📍 Monterrey Palace

Appoint yourself the leader of this fact-packed self-guided around the 16th-century Monterrey Palace, a marvellous monument of the noble House of Alba. During this specially-recorded tour you'll gain a rare insight into the family rooms, which are still used every day. Despite the palace's modest size, it's a trove of art and tapestry, antique furniture, and even armor. The audio guide provides a fusion of voice, images and music to truly set the scene as you wander through a building which dates back to 1539 and the third Count of Monterrey. To top off the tour, you'll also get one of the panoramic views of Salamanca from the top of the palace.
