Private Newcastle Walking Tour

📍 Newcastle City Tours

Begin your Newcastle walking tour and take a stroll up **Pink Lane** past some authentic bars, pubs, and cafés before heading towards the great City Walls, dating back to 1280. Venture along through Chinatown to **Gallowgate** (don't worry, no gallows here anymore) and gaze at the magnificent **St. James Park football stadium**, home to Newcastle United. This guided tour allows you to experience the fabulous **Grainger Market**, and discover Earl Grey himself (yes, he of the tea). Traverse to the historic quayside and take a stroll along the riverside on **the banks of the river Tyne**. Cross over one of the seven bridges and take a stroll through Norman Foster's stunning philharmonic, **Sage Gateshead**, and end your Newcastle walking tour at **BALTIC**.
