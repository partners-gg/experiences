Islamorada: Snorkel Adventure & Sandbar Trip

📍 Islamorada

This 4-hour snorkeling trip in Islamorada Key also includes a visit to a well-known sandbar. You'll snorkel at a prime spot in Islamorada, seeing a variety of fish and colorful coral reefs. Afterward, the trip heads to a sandbar that is like a small beach in the middle of the ocean. There, you can relax, play in the shallow water, or try your hand at paddleboarding. Snorkeling gear, paddleboards, and refreshments are all provided. You'll also learn about the local marine life and how to help preserve it. Whether you want a relaxing day or an active one, it's all possible on this snorkeling and sandbar trip!
