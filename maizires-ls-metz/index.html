📍 Walygator Park Grand Est

Located in the heart of the Lorraine region, this amusement park offers a wide range of exciting attractions and rides for visitors of all ages. From roller coasters and water rides to live shows and themed areas, Walygator East has something for everyone. One of the main highlights of Walygator East is its impressive selection of roller coasters, including the renowned _Anaconda_ and _Monsterr_ rides. The park also boasts a variety of family-friendly attractions, such as the enchanting Fantasy Land and the interactive Adventure Land. With over 40 different rides and attractions to choose from, visitors are guaranteed to have a day full of thrills and excitement.
