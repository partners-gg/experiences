Lychnostatis Open-Air Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 Lychnostatis Open-Air Museum

Located in one of Crete's main tourist areas, Lychnostatis Open Air Museum details the folk tradition, culture, and environmental heritage of the island. Set across multiple buildings and outdoor spaces, Lychnostatis Museum is a trove of Cretan treasures. Visitors can admire old wine and olive presses, visit a small windmill, wander herb & fruit gardens, see a shepherd's shelter, and enjoy a gallery of work by Cretan artists. By exploring this time-capsule museum, you'll immerse yourself in a unique island culture.
