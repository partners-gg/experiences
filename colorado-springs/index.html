📍 U.S. Olympic & Paralympic Museum

On your marks, get set, go... to the U.S. Olympic & Paralympic Museum! This state-of-the-art museum in Colorado Springs takes you on an interactive marathon, delving into the USA's gold-standard athletes and stories from down the years. See authentic artifacts; Olympic torches, medals, and more. Consider the legacies of icons like Jesse Owens, Michael Phelps, Trischa Zorn, and every athlete who has worn the Team USA uniform. You can try your hand at archery, and even skiing, with fun digital exhibits across the 60,000-square-foot museum.
