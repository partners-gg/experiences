SPLIT ·BLUE CAVE
Blue Cave, Hvar & 5 Islands: Speedboat Tour
Board a speedboat to the Blue Cave on Biševo island. Next, visit Komiža, a fishing village on Vis Island shown in *Mamma Mia!*. Spend an hour walking its streets, relaxing on the beach, or having breakfast and coffee at outdoor cafes. Cruise past WWII military bunkers and limestone caves. Swim at Stiniva Bay, another *Mamma Mia!* location. Stop at Budikovac island’s Blue Lagoon for 1.5-hours of snorkeling and swimming. Finally, ride to Hvar Island. Spend over two hours exploring the historic town, visit the fortress, and have lunch. End your tour in Split in the evening.

SPLIT ·SPLIT BUS TOURS
Split: 24 or 48-hr Hop-on Hop-off Bus Tour + Guided Walking Tour
Get to know Split on a comprehensive tour, combining five different tours. The Red Line hop-on hop-off bus tour has stops near Split’s main viewpoints, landmarks, museums, and beaches. Hop on and off at any stop and explore at your own pace. The Blue Line tour goes beyond Split with an English-speaking guide at each stop. It starts in the Old Town, then visits the Olive Museum, and ends at Klis Fortress. With the Green Line tour, you can visit Salona Archaeological Park or the Hidden Dalmatia multimedia center. It also includes stops at Klis Fortress, the Olive Museum, and Žnjan Beach. You can choose two sites based on your interests. Join the Klis Sunset tour to visit the Klis Fortress (entry not included). You can walk around the site, enjoy the sunset, and see the city, mountains, and nearby islands. The fortress was used as a filming location for Game of Thrones. Your ticket also includes a guided walking tour of Split Old Town with the famous Diocletian's Palace.
