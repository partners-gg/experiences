📍 Forteresse de Salses

If Game of Thrones was set entirely in the 15th-century Spanish-French frontier, the Fortress of Salses would be Winterfell. Captured by the French in the 17th century, the castle has seen its fair share of siege action over the years; now it's your turn to storm the castle! But, put away that battering ram – this ticket will get you in through the front door. Once inside, you'll be ambushed by cavalry-charged history and see some of the most impressive medieval architecture in France.
