📍 Abbaye de La Sauve-Majeure

Visit the abbey of La Sauve-Majeure, a **UNESCO World Heritage** site 25 km from Bordeaux, set between the Garonne and Dordogne rivers in the Entre-Deux-Mers region. You'll see Romanesque art, medieval architecture, sculpted decorations, and a tall bell tower. Look at detailed capitals, walk among the cloister ruins, and check out archaeological displays in the exhibition area.
