📍 Idlewild + SoakZone

Located in Pennsylvania’s scenic Laurel Highlands region, Idlewild is the place for classic amusement rides, the splashtastic SoakZone waterpark, live entertainment, and a host of unique attractions. Scream your way through thrilling adventure aboard the Rollo Coaster, a classic wooden roller coaster packed with heart-pounding drops and twists. Hop on the Flying Aces, a family-friendly ride that lets you soar through the sky. Then, let your imagination run wild in Daniel Tiger's Neighborhood, where you can explore a charming interactive play area inspired by the beloved children's television show. Bring your bathing suits and beach towels and cool off in the SoakZone. Float down the lazy river, zoom down waterslides, and take the pipeline plunge, if you dare. Little ones can have a blast in the Little Squirts area, while older kids can race down the Hydro Racers slides. It's the perfect place to make a splash and beat the heat!
