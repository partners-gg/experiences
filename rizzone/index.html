Dallara Academy: Entry Ticket + Simulator Access

📍 Dallara Academy

Visit the Dallara Academy, an educational and exhibition center established by Giampaolo Dallara. Designed by Atelier(s) Alfonso Femia, the building features a glass spiral ramp connecting two floors. On the ground level, see interactive workshops and practical spaces. On the upper floor, find MUNER university classrooms and an auditorium. The Academy displays cars like the Miura, IndyCars, Le Mans prototypes, Formula 3, Formula E, and the Dallara Stradale. Try the driving simulator to understand racing. The glass wall and layout offer a clear view of motorsport, technology, and design.
