Mucem - Museum of European and Mediterranean Civilizations: Fast Track Ticket

📍 Mucem

You could go to Mucem in Marseille twice and have two entirely different experiences. The stunning museum of European and Mediterranean Civilizations is home to one of the most extensive collections of Mediterranean art and culture anywhere in the world. 📍 Château d’If

Step inside a literary classic when you visit the island fortress prison Château d’If, which was the inspiration for Alexandre Dumas’ 1844 novel _The Count of Monte Cristo_. Learn about the prisoners incarcerated here and get amazing views of the Vieux Port!

Marseille: Cosquer Cave Entry Ticket + City Audio Tour

📍 Cosquer Cave

Visit the Cosquer Cave to learn about early humans and view prehistoric cave paintings. Begin your tour on a floating walkway and see the diving gear used to find the cave. Then, enter the recreated cave and ride an exploratory vehicle. Use an audio guide to hear about early human life, cave art, and the cave's discovery. Visit the Mediterranean Gallery to see what the world looked like during prehistoric times. After the cave visit, use a sightseeing app on your smartphone for a self-guided audio tour of Marseille. Hear stories and facts about the city's history as you explore at your own pace.

Sky Centre Marseille: 30th Floor Access

📍 Sky Centre Marseille

Visit the top of the La Marseillaise Tower for a deep look into Marseille's culture. Head to the terrace on the 30th floor for a 180° view of the city, designed by Jean Nouvel, capturing the Old Port and the calanques. Use an interactive robot and a mobile app to learn about the city. Kids can enjoy a fun game made just for them. Play a game of pétanque on the highest court in the city (boules provided). Explore sensory booths with different smells and sounds. Afterward, try Mediterranean dishes like tapenade, panisses, anchoïade, cheeses, and charcuterie, with optional wine from Provence or pastis (not included in this ticket).

Cassis & Aix-en-Provence: Guided Tour from Marseille

📍 Day Trip from Marseille

Visit Cassis, its calanques, and Aix-en-Provence during your stay in Provence. Start your journey in Marseille and visit Cassis. Take photos from Cap Canaille, one of the highest sea cliffs in Europe. Enjoy free time in the fishing port, where you can have lunch or sunbathe. Take a cruise through the calanques (not included in this ticket) and see the narrow, steep-sided valleys partly submerged by the Mediterranean Sea. Continue to Aix-en-Provence for the rest of the afternoon. Walk along Cours Mirabeau with its fountains and 17th and 18th-century mansions. Return to Marseille in the minivan.
