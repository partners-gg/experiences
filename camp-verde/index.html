Out of Africa Wildlife Park: Entry Ticket

📍 Out of Africa Wildlife Park

Nestled in the Mingus Mountains, Out of Africa Wildlife Park is your Stateside Serengeti, a 103-acre reserve that's home to some of the world's most wondrous animal life. The park strives to both educate and entertain. As well as marveling at the residents – giraffes, rhinos, black bears, leopards, caimans, zebras, and the like – you can catch expertly led displays and feeds to get a real understanding of the animals' nature. With a mixture of vehicular and on-foot experiences, you'll really feel like you're on safari!

Verde Valley & Montezuma's Castle: Day Tour from Phoenix

📍 Montezuma's Castle

Montezuma Castle National Monument and Tuzigoot National Monument both feature ancient homes and treasures from the Native American Sinagua people, who lived in Arizona centuries before Christopher Columbus showed up. Learn about their remarkably advanced culture as you're led around these historic sites by a Native American guide.

Camp Verde: The Predator Zipline Experience

📍 Outdoor Activities in Phoenix

Try a two to three-hour eco zip line tour with five lines and a suspension bridge. Glide through the air and see wildlife from above at the Out of Africa Wildlife Park. Spot lions, cougars, tigers, and take in the red rock views. Guides will share tips and point out animals below. This course is great for beginners and experienced zip liners alike. Ask guides for details about the animals you see.
