Via Ferrata Rio Sallagoni

📍 Lake Garda Canyoning

The play of lights and waterfalls inside the gorge makes this an extra special romantic setting for a hike! Tie up the laces and get out onto this beautiful trail that winds through a mystical ravine. Test your balance and courage on the Tibetan rope bridge and try to pass the steep walls without getting wet? Don't worry if you do, it's all part of the adventure! Once you've made your way along the short trail in a wild fairy-tale setting, ascending this canyon will lead you to the magnificent Drena Castle - perfect for some idyllic photos and a little refreshment.
