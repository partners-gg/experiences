📍 Château de Vaux-le-Vicomte

Visit Château de Vaux-le-Vicomte, located about 40 minutes from Paris. The château is known for influencing the design of Versailles. You can explore the interior rooms, including state rooms, private apartments, the library, basement kitchens, and the Coach Museum. The estate also includes formal gardens designed by André Le Nôtre, covering 33 hectares, along with a larger surrounding forest.
