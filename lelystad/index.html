Museum Batavialand: Entry Ticket

📍 Museum Batavialand

Visit the Museum Batavialand, where you can see a replica of the VOC ship Batavia, learn about traditional crafts at the shipyard, and learn about the history of Flevoland in the exhibition spaces. Walk on the deck of a full-scale replica of a 17th-century VOC ship and see how people lived and worked onboard. See the 4,000 square meters of exhibition space, and learn about Flevoland’s history from the prehistoric Swifterbant culture to modern land reclamation. The exhibits also include an 18th-century shipwreck, the 60-meter-long Flevowand, and the Watertheater. Next, go to the shipyard, watch craftsmen demonstrate 17th-century techniques in forging, wood carving, and sail making. They are also building a replica of a 16th-century water ship using traditional methods.

Aviodrome: Entry Ticket

📍 Aviodrome

No need for passports, suitcases, or tantrums in the terminal. Whatever the weather, grab your Aviodrome tickets, and get the family down to Lelystad for fun with planes – without flying anywhere! See a real Boeing 747 jumbo jet up close. Help the kids discover Morse code signals in the radio room, or watch them train to be a pilot or flight attendant. Learn about Dutch aviation and walk around a fascinating collection of over 100 aircraft. Take the family to Aviodrome for a fun, educational day among over 100 aircraft!
