GATLINBURG ·ANAKEESTA
Anakeesta: Entry Ticket
Visit Anakeesta, a mountaintop adventure park above downtown Gatlinburg. Take a Chondola ride from downtown to Anakeesta Mountain. Enjoy treetop activities, a botanical garden, shopping, and dining with views of the national park. Walk the Treetop Skywalk with hanging bridges 880 feet above the forest floor. Climb the AnaVista Tower for views of downtown. For more adventure, try the Dueling Zipline tour and Rail Runner mountain coaster for an extra cost. Kids can play in netted areas like the treehouse-themed TreeVenture Challenge course.

GATLINBURG ·GATLINBURG SKYPARK
Gatlinburg SkyPark: Skip the Line Entry + Chairlift Ride
Start your visit to SkyPark with a ride on the SkyLift - this yellow Chairlift will take you up to Crockett Summit. Then, cross SkyBridge, the longest pedestrian cable bridge in North America, for incredible views. After your walk across the bridge, take in the sights of Great Smoky Mountains National Park from Clayton’s Landing, and grab a smash burger at Smoky Mountain Smash if you’re hungry. Explore the new 1.5-mile trail system, including the SkyTrail, which leads to the Tulip Tower. SkyPark also offers plenty of other activities for families to enjoy together.

Ripley's Aquarium of the Smokies: Entry Ticket
Discover the wonders of Ripley’s Aquarium of the Smokies in Gatlinburg, Tennessee, where you can explore the underwater world and interact with marine life like rays and sea turtles. Get up close to a variety of sea creatures, including sharks, jellies, and eels, while knowledgeable staff offer hands-on experiences and behind-the-scenes encounters. With thousands of marine creatures from over 350 species, this aquarium is a must-visit destination in Gatlinburg.
