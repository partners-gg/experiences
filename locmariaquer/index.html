Megalithic Site of Locmariaquer: Entry Ticket

📍 Megalithic Site of Locmariaquer

Passage graves, monumental burial structures, and the Broken Menhir of Er Grah, which stood 20 meters tall and weighed 330 tons – this glimpse at our ancestors will leave you fascinated. These relics are impressive not only for their sheer size, but also for what they represented. Through them we gain a glimpse of the cultures that came before, pay our respects to the dead who were honored, and remember that our will to engineer has been around for millennia.
