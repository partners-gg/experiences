Salento & Cocora Valley: Guided Tour from Pereira

📍 Day Trips from Pereira

Get ready to embark on an aromatic journey through the heart of coffee country! This tour takes you to one of the region's most prestigious coffee farms, unveiling the magic behind the cultivation, harvest, and production of the beloved bean. And the best part? You'll savour a superb cup of coffee straight from the source! Next, you're off to the quaint municipality of Salento, nestled in the Central Andes. To round off the day, you're invited to indulge in a local delicacy at a popular restaurant in the area. This is more than just a tour - it's a feast for all the senses!
