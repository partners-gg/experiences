Caumont-Centre d’Art

📍 Caumont-Centre d’Art

See a slice of life from old Provence at the remarkable Caumont Centre d'Art in Aix en Provence. Complete with a gorgeous French garden, this stunning, newly renovated space has been restored to its former grandeur. It offers a huge space, where international exhibitions are waiting to wow you, but idle a while over the Paul Cezanne paintings on display. Visit the historical lounges, a bookstore-boutique, and the restaurant both inside and outside on the terrace. This belle epoque house and garden is a must-see when you're looking for things to do in Aix en Provence.

Luminiscence Aix-en-Provence: Immersive Light Experience

📍 LUMINISCENCE Aix-en-Provence

LUMINISCENCE comes to Aix-en-Provence after attracting over 500,000 visitors across France. Enjoy this unique performance at the Saint-Sauveur Cathedral. The show combines 360° video mapping, poetic narration, and live music to highlight the architecture of the monument. Enjoy a live performance on weekends, featuring one of the region’s top choirs led by Bénédicte Pereira, performing the original soundtrack. On weekdays, watch the story unfold with the original digital soundtrack.

Luberon Villages: Guided Day Tour from Aix-en-Provence

📍 Day Trips from Aix-en-Provence

Visit the hilltop villages of the Luberon on a full-day small-group tour from Aix-en-Provence. An English-speaking guide will take you to Gordes, Roussillon, and Lourmarin, known for their history and distinct character. Walk through the cobbled streets of Gordes, one of France’s noted villages. In July, see the Senanque Abbey, which is surrounded by lavender fields. Spend some free time at the ochre cliffs of Roussillon and see the distinct architecture and artistic spots in Lourmarin. Travel in a small group of no more than eight people for a more personalized experience. Learn about the local culture and return to Aix-en-Provence with clear memories of these notable hilltop locations.

Valensole Lavender Fields: Guided Day Tour from Aix-en-Provence

📍 Day Trips from Aix-en-Provence

Visit the lavender fields of Provence on a half-day tour from Aix-en-Provence. Travel to the Valensole Plateau, where rows of lavender cover the landscape. Photograph these fields at selected spots and learn to distinguish between true lavender and lavandin. Tour traditional lavender farms, see the distillation process, and try lavender-infused treats like honey and ice cream. Travel in small groups with comfortable transport for a quiet trip through rural scenery. Great for photographers and nature enthusiasts, this tour provides insights into lavender farming and Provence’s cultural heritage.
