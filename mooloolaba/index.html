MOOLOOLABA ·SUNSHINE COAST CRUISES
2-Hour Sunshine Coast Whale Watching Experience
This Sunshine Coast whale watching excursion allows you to see majestic creatures in their natural habitat, above and below the water! With a maximum group size of 20, you'll take to the sea aboard a vessel designed for speed and comfort. Your expert skippers will sail out to known whale waters, where you'll be mesmerized by breaches, loud tail slaps, spy hops and peduncle throws performed by giants of the ocean. As well as informative commentary and your all-natural live stream, the boat is equipped with HD cameras so you can scan beneath the blue for a unique view. Better still, a hydrophone on board lets you hear the whales sing as they play – it's an unforgettable experience in the making!
