Avoca Mount Usher Gardens: Self Guided Experience

📍 Mount Usher Gardens

Avoca Mount Usher Gardens is just **one hour from Central Dublin**. An award-winning example of Robinsonian Gardens, booking your Mount Usher Gardens entry tickets secures a wonderful day out in County Wicklow. A haven of rare beauty and tranquility, Mount Usher Gardens welcomes **self-guided tours or guided group tours** throughout the year. Across 22 acres, with these tickets, you can explore at your leisure, soaking up the stunning greenery in the Garden of Ireland. After you redeem your ticket, **you'll be given a tree trail map** so that you can make the most of your visit and learn about the site. On-site, you'll **find a wonderful Avoca Food Market**, complete with a gourmet deli and bakery offering freshly made sandwiches, salads, sweet treats, coffees, and more. The **table service Garden Café** is also located here, where you can sit and enjoy a delicious seasonal menu overlooking the beautiful gardens.
