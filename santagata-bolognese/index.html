Lamborghini Automobile Museum: Entry Ticket + Factory Tour

Join a guided tour of Lamborghini’s museum and production facilities. Your visit begins with access to the museum and the Factory 4.0 production area, where you can watch luxury supercars being built. Skilled workers and advanced technology handle each step with precision. During the tour, find out how craftsmanship, automated vehicles, and collaborative robots help the assembly line run smoothly. Spend about 45 minutes in the assembly lines, observing key stages of the process.
