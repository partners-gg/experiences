Tenuta Bossi: Guided Tour + Wine Tasting

📍 Tenuta Bossi

Visit a historic estate still owned by Florence's oldest family. In Tuscany, under the villa’s shade and surrounded by vineyards and olive groves, learn about the Bossi family's wine-making tradition dating back to the 1500s. Tour the cellar under the villa, where wine is aged in wooden barrels, and hear about the different grape varieties they have cultivated for centuries. Learn about the traditional wine-making process and see the orciaia and caratelli used for Vinsanto. Finally, enjoy a wine-tasting session featuring three distinct Tuscan wines, which vary by season and availability.
