The Barolo Palace is a work of pure love! Imaginative architect Mario Palanti's symbolic references to Dante Alighieri's poem the _Divine Comedy_ make this structure truly unique. Blending neo-Romanesque and neo-Gothic elements with a Hindu-designed dome, guests are reminded of the tantric union between the protagonists, Dante and Beatrice, and the three distinct realms of the _Comedy_.
