Palacio Barolo: Guided Tour

📍 Palacio Barolo

The Barolo Palace is a work of pure love! Imaginative architect Mario Palanti's symbolic references to Dante Alighieri's poem the _Divine Comedy_ make this structure truly unique. Blending neo-Romanesque and neo-Gothic elements with a Hindu-designed dome, guests are reminded of the tantric union between the protagonists, Dante and Beatrice, and the three distinct realms of the _Comedy_. At night, the lighthouse flickers into action, giving you great city views of exotic facades from up high.
