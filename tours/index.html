Château de Chenonceau & Chambord: Guided Tour

📍 Loire Valley Day Trip from Tours

Join a full-day guided tour of the Loire Valley from Tours to visit two important French castles. Start at Chenonceau, the "Château des Dames," to see its unique architecture and Renaissance gardens. Learn about the notable former residents who lived there. After lunch (at your own expense) in a nearby village, head to Château de Chambord. Explore its double helix staircase, large rooms, and terraces with wide views. Gain insights from expert guides at both sites as you learn about the art and history of each location.

Château de Chenonceau & Chambord: Guided Trip from Tours + Lunch

📍 Loire Valley Day Trip from Tours

Enjoy a drive by minivan to Chenonceau and arrive at Château de Chenonceau, a castle built on a bridge across the Cher River. Walk through the gardens named after the famous residents, Catherine de Médicis and Diane de Poitiers. Observe the architecture and rooms filled with antiques and fresh flowers. Have lunch at a special location hosted by the resident Countess and continue to Chambord. Look at the artworks and architecture as your guide highlights key rooms and hidden details. See the view of Château de Chambord before returning to Tours. Also, the drop-off at the end of the day is at 6:30 PM.
