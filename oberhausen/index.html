SEA LIFE Oberhausen: Entry Ticket

📍 SEA LIFE Oberhausen

SEA LIFE Oberhausen is no regular aquarium experience. With over 5,000 marine creatures, it's one of the largest water kingdoms in Germany. On a typical trip to Oberhausen's SEA LIFE aquarium, you'll go nose-to-glass with beady-eyed sharks, graceful rays, and beautiful sea turtles. There's even playful otters and marvelous axolotls! Wander a tunnel with sea creatures swimming all around you, and board a glass-bottomed boat to explore a tropical reef. Then visit the Amazon, a shipwreck, and a flowstone cave without ever leaving Oberhausen!
