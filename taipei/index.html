Beitou & Yangmingshan National Park: Guided Day Tour from Taipei

📍 Day Trips from Taipei

Discover the volcanic beauty of Taiwan with a guided hot spring tour to Beitou and Yangmingshan. Meet your tour guide and driver in the morning and set off on an unforgettable day-long adventure. On the way to Yangmingshan National Park, you'll also stop by the incredible architecture of Taipei Public Library Beitou Branch. First, visit Beitou Hot Spring Museum and Geothermal Valley to learn about the hot spring culture history of Beitou. Then you will visit the centerpiece of the park – Yangmingshan Flower Clock, and Yangming Shu-Wu Mansion, the summer residence of the late president Chiang Kai-shek. After that, the tour continues to the igneous landscape of Xiaoyoukeng, and the lava terrace of Qingtiangang. The last stop is Lengshuikeng, a barrier lake formed by volcanic lava flowing down from the mountain where you can enjoy a hot spring foot bath!
