📍 Acquasplash Franciacorta

Aquasplash Franciacorta is a water park making a splash in Brescia. Covering a huge space, the park promises wet and wild fun for the whole family. There are swimming pools and water games for all ages, and thrilling slides like Vortex, Kamikaze, and the Drop. There's even a dark slide - do you dare enter the Black Hole? Little ones can enjoy their own special play area, designed for toddlers and little ones, while adults can soak up the sun in a green grassy area, great for relaxing. Hungry after all that fun? Stop the rumbling tums with a variety of restaurants inside the park - there's something to suit everyone's taste buds.
