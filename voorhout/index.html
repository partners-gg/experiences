Bulb Region: Dahlia Gardens, Windmills & Castle Bike Tour

📍 De Tulperij

The best way to experience the enchanting scenery of the Dutch countryside? On a bike tour, of course! Starting from the 'Tulipfarm' in Voorhout, hop on a bicycle and let the vibrant colors and captivating sights of South Holland work their magic. The adventure begins at the 'Tulipfarm', where stunning flower fields are waiting to be admired. Next, journey back in time with a visit to the Lisserpoelmolen and Zemelmolen, where ancient windmills stand tall and proud, and waiting for a selfie! Continue peddling towards the charming Agatha Church, a tranquil oasis of beauty and serenity. But hold on to your handlebars, because the pièce de résistance awaits at Kasteel Keukenhof. Here, the breathtaking dahlia garden will dazzle the senses with its kaleidoscope of colors and fragrances!
