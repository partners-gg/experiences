Vlissingen: Guided City Walk

📍 Zeeland City Tours

Experience Vlissingen through the eyes of a local expert on this guided walk through the old city. See the home of Michiel de Ruyter, and learn the story behind the bullet above his front door! Who visited Vlissingen in 1809, and how high did the water rise during the infamous flood of February 1, 1953? All these questions and more will be answered as you listen to tales – both past and present – while enjoying the scenic streets, courtyards and squares of Vlissingen. You can also choose to combine your walk with a ride on the Zonnetrein, which will take you through another part of Vlissingen (including the Boulevard).

Solar Train Zeeland

📍 Zeeland City Tours

Explore the charming city of Vlissingen aboard the environmentally friendly Solar Train Zeeland! Powered by electric motors which run on solar energy, this futuristic sightseeing locomotive consumes no harmful fossil fuel, emits no nasty gases, and makes hardly any noise. You can choose between two options. Either enjoy a direct ride from De Punt (close to Vlissingen Station) to the city center, or sit back and relax as you take a hop-on hop-off tour of all the local sights in the afternoon. Breeze around the harbor scenery, check out the marina or the bustling beachfront promenade, and enjoy the pleasant seaside vibes with plenty of time to explore at your leisure.
