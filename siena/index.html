📍 Pubblico Palace

Visit the Civic Museum of the Palazzo Pubblico in Siena, home to over 100 years of history. See richly frescoed halls and prestigious collections that span Sienese history and art from the early 14th century to today. View works by Simone Martini and other renowned artists of the Sienese School. This visit provides insight into the civic pride, religious devotion, and artistic culture that defined Siena’s golden age.
