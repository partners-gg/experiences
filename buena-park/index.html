Knott's Berry Farm

📍 Knott's Berry Farm

At Knott’s Berry Farm, fun is always in season, with dozens of thrilling rides, shows and attractions across four themed areas inspired by the history and culture of California. A day at Knott’s Berry Farm means taking on mammoth roller coasters, embarking on Old West adventures in the authentic 1800s Ghost Town, paying tribute to California’s Hispanic roots in Fiesta Village, experiencing the Southern California beach lifestyle along the Boardwalk, and exploring the High Sierras with the world’s most lovable beagle — SNOOPY®. Located just 10 minutes from the Anaheim resort area, the once–small family farm has grown into today’s family fun destination, home to Knott’s Berry Farm theme park, Knott’s Soak City Waterpark and Knott’s Hotel. Whether you’re looking for a weekend getaway, family road trip or overnight stay, you’ll find something for everyone at Knott’s Berry Farm.
