Acua Water Park Fuerteventura: Entry Ticket

📍 Acua Water Park Fuerteventura

Visit Acua Water Park, located in the tourist area of Corralejo, which is the only waterpark in Fuerteventura. With an area covering more than 25,000 m2, the waterpark offers over 14 attractions suitable for all ages. There are slides like the Splash Race Track, Dark Cave, Zig-Zags, and Kamikazes reaching speeds of up to 30 km/h. Relax on the Lazy River or enjoy a variety of dining options, entertainment services, and a kids’ club.

Fuerteventura: Jandía Natural Park & El Puertito Guided Buggy Tour

📍 Fuerteventura Outdoor Activities

Travel through southern Fuerteventura on a guided buggy tour. Ride through the mountains of the oldest island in the Canary Islands. Visit Jandía Natural Park and the city of Puerto de la Cruz. After being picked up from your hotel, go to the Morro Jable Port. Get a lesson from the guide on how to drive the buggy and understand its signals. Learn about safety precautions, put on your goggles, and start the ride. Drive through Jandía Natural Park and see local wildlife, such as squirrels, goats, sheep, and donkeys. Arrive in the town of Puerto de la Cruz, also known as El Puertito. Hear stories about this place before starting the return trip.

Fuerteventura: Guided Buggy Tour + Hotel Pickup

📍 Fuerteventura Outdoor Activities

Join a buggy adventure with over 70% of the route on land and rural roads. Visit the rural area of Fuerteventura, passing old villages, natural salt flats, and a local cheese farm. Stop at an old cheese farm to try goat cheese and purchase some if you desire. Learn about goat cheese production and see birds, camels, donkeys, and other farm animals. Visit Barranco de la Torre for ocean views. From the center of the island, follow a path along the Atlantic Ocean and return to Caleta de Fuste.
