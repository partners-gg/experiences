DARWIN ·CROCOSAURUS COVE
Big Croc Encounter VIP Tour
Crocosaurus Cove's Big Croc Feed VIP Experience offers a unique behind-the-scenes look at this reptilian wonderland, giving visitors deep insights into reptiles and how they are cared for. On your first stop, learn how to feed a 5-meter crocodile and witness the true power of his bone-crushing jaws! Finish the tour with an intimate walk through the Reptile House, and safely interact with slithering snakes and beady-eyed lizards. This fantastic tour is offered at 10:00 and 13:00 daily, and is the ultimate experience for every reptile lover. All animal handling opportunities are optional and supervised by professional experts.

DARWIN ·RFDS DARWIN TOURIST FACILITY
RFDS Darwin Tourist Facility
The RFDS Darwin Tourist Facility shares the incredible story behind Reverend John Flynn's Royal Flying Doctor Service, which launched in 1939. Using VR displays, immersive films, and interactive exhibits – including a cockpit experience inside a decommissioned plane – the RFDS Darwin Tourist Facility gives you a bird's-eye view of the charity's history. Get ready to fly into the past using the power of virtual reality. As you put on the headset you'll be transported back to 19 February 1942, when Darwin harbour was attacked by 242 Japanese aircraft.
