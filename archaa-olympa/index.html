📍 Ancient Olympia Archaeological Site and Museum

Visit Ancient Olympia, the birthplace of the Olympic Games and a **UNESCO World Heritage Site**. Start at the archaeological site in the Peloponnese. Walk through ancient ruins and see the remnants of sacred temples, including the **Temple of Zeus**, known for its Doric architecture. You'll also see the **Temple of Hera**, one of Greece's oldest monumental temples. Continue to the ancient stadium, hippodrome, palestra, and gymnasium where athletes once trained for the Olympic Games. Go to the **Archaeological Museum of Olympia**, which displays artifacts from the sanctuary dedicated to Zeus. Finish at the **Museum of the History of the Olympic Games** to learn about the origins and evolution of the games, complete with artifacts and exhibits.
