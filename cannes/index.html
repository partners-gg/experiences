CANNES ·CANNES CITY TOURS
Cannes: Guided Vintage Van Tour
Hop on the French bus, a vintage minibus, for a 2-hour tour of Cannes. See well-known locations like Palm Beach, Port Canto, the Croisette, and the Palais des Festivals. Admire the palaces and boutiques along the Croisette, enjoy views of the Lérins Islands, walk through the old Suquet district, and visit the local market with your guide. The vintage minibus ride offers a fun, retro touch and often draws attention from people passing by. Take photos, learn about the city, and explore some of Cannes’ most popular and historic areas.

CANNES ·CANNES BOAT TOURS
Saint Tropez: Boat Tour from Cannes
This boat tour lets you avoid the summer traffic and travel by sea on our semi-rigid *Black Tenders*. You’ll take a one-hour ride (weather permitting) to Saint-Tropez, where you’ll have free time in the morning to explore the port, walk through the town, visit Place des Lices market, or relax at a café. In the afternoon, your captain will take you on a short coastal excursion. You’ll visit Port Grimaud and pass under its small bridges, then continue along the Estérel Massif to see coves, sea caves, cliffs, and clear water from the boat.

CANNES ·CANNES BOAT TOURS
Lérins Islands: 2.5-Hr Fireworks Cruise from Cannes
Start your evening with a sunset cruise between the Lérins Islands. Aboard our semi-rigid *Black Tenders*, you’ll be guided by an experienced captain in a small, private setting. When the Cannes International Fireworks Festival begins, the boat will be positioned about 300 m from the launch area, giving you a clear view of the show from the water. The fireworks and music can be seen and heard easily from this spot. Watching the fireworks from the sea also keeps you away from the crowds onshore.

CANNES ·CANNES BOAT TOURS
The Calanques of the Esterel: 1.5-Hr Boat Tour from Cannes
Enjoy a boat tour along the French Riviera. Departing from Cannes or Mandelieu-la-Napoule, you’ll board one of our *Black Tenders* for a short Estérel cruise that highlights local scenery, architecture, and calm coastal views. This tour works well at sunrise or sunset. During the cruise, you’ll pass notable Riviera landmarks including Château de La Napoule, Pointe de l’Aiguille, Palais Bulles by Pierre Cardin, the architect-designed homes of Port La Galère, and waterfront estates such as Villa Trident and Villa Montgolfière. You’ll also have a chance to swim near the Estérel Massif. On board, you’ll find saddle-style jockey seats for a steady and comfortable ride. Your local captain will share clear and helpful information about the coastline throughout the trip.

CANNES ·CANNES BOAT TOURS
Massif de l'Esterel Excursion by Boat
Discover the Estérel coastline on this boat tour from Mandelieu-la-Napoule aboard *Black Tenders*. Travel along the red cliffs, coves, and inlets of the Estérel Massif, and visit areas that larger boats cannot access. Your captain will share information about the region’s geology and marine life. See landmarks such as the Château de La Napoule, Château des Mineurs, Maison Lacoste, and the Palais Bulles. Continue to Cap Roux, where you can see the cliffs and surrounding waters.
