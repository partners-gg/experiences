KAMLOOPS ·BC WILDLIFE PARK
BC Wildlife Park: Entry Ticket
Spend some time learning about British Columbia’s animals at this wonderful wildlife rehabilitation center and CAZA-accredited facility. Conservation and education are the main goals of the park, where you can see over 200 animals in expansive habitats. It's a perfect place for a family visit, with plenty of activities to keep all ages engaged and interested. Visit small and large mammals and birds in carefully constructed habitats, learn about wildlife rehabilitation and the work of the BC Wildlife Park, ride the small train, climb in the playground, cool off at the spray park, and more! Finish your visit to the BC Wildlife Park with a unique souvenir from the Naturally Yours Gift Shop, where your purchase helps to protect and rehabilitate British Columbia's beautiful wildlife.
