Portsmouth Historic Dockyard: Ultimate Explorer

📍 Portsmouth Historic Dockyard

There are ships, submarines, physical challenges, simulators and more at the Portsmouth Historic Dockyard. This ticket gets you access to the site for a whole year! Take a harbour tour for the history of Portsmouth Historic Dockyard, and amuse the little ones with a water bus across to Royal Navy Submarine Museum or a 4D experience Dive the Mary Rose. At The National Museum of the Royal Navy, use your audio guide to learn about a huge bronze cannon lifted from the wreck of HMS Victory 1744 – the predecessor to Nelson's famous flagship – and marvel at the HMS Warrior, a remnant of the Victorian era that still stands proudly in the harbour. This ticket allows you to create your own perfect day out by visiting the attractions you want to, whether it's travelling back to Tudor England to marvel at Henry VIII’s ship the Mary Rose, or going out to sea on a harbour tour.

The Spinnaker Tower: Entry Ticket

📍 The Spinnaker Tower

If you've eaten too many portions of British fish and chips, fear not. The sturdy Spinnaker Tower can hold the weight of two black rhinos on its 60mm-thick glass floor! There's no better place for panoramic views of Portsmouth than this harbour highlight (emphasis on the _high_).

Water Country Waterpark: Entry Ticket

📍 Water Country Waterpark

Visit Water Country, New England’s largest waterpark. Enjoy 18 family-friendly water attractions, including the Giant Wave Pool and Adventure River. Make a splash at Tahiti Tree House or share a snack at the Dippin’ Dots stand. For over 40 years, Water Country has been a favorite spot to cool off on a hot summer day. It’s a popular spot for many in New England looking to beat the heat.
