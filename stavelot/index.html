Plopsaland Ardennes: Entry Ticket

📍 Plopsaland Ardennes

Discover Plopsaland Ardennes, located at the Coo Waterfalls, the perfect destination for a day full of adventure. With over 25 attractions, this park offers a blend of nature and excitement, making it ideal for the whole family. Let the adventurer in you loose on over 25 attractions, set amidst natural surroundings! Daredevils can take on the 600-meter Bobsleigh or enjoy the views from the 65-meter-high Mega Mindy Flyer, where you'll get a view of Plopsa Coo Ardennes and the impressive waterfalls. For those who love heights, The Bel RTL Chair Lift offers views of the Ardennes. Be sure to visit Wickie the Viking and his friends, and enjoy a high-speed ride on Vicky the ride. Rescue the Smurfs trapped by Gargamel in The Smurfer! And don’t miss DinoSplash, an exciting water attraction with three slides inside a giant volcano surrounded by prehistoric dinosaurs! This summer, a brand-new Maya Zone will open with four magical attractions for the whole family to enjoy.
