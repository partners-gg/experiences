Plaza Premium Lounge at Toronto Pearson International Airport

📍 Plaza Premium Lounge Toronto Pearson Airport

Upgrade your travel experience by visiting the Plaza Premium Lounge. Conveniently located just past security screening on Domestic Departures Level 3, the lounge consists of 4,900 square feet and can seat up to 110 guests in 3 separate seating zones. Enjoy a relaxing environment with comfortable sofa chairs and leather-covered swivel chairs, and sample a variety of hot food items, a soup and salad bar, hot and cold beverages, and a self-serve bar. With marble and dark wood accents, New Zealand wool carpet, and incredible comfortable seating, the lounge is a beautiful, quiet and comfy place to spend some time.
