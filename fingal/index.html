Mornington Peninsula Hot Springs & Bathing Boxes

📍 Peninsula Hot Springs

On this guided adventure on the Mornington Peninsula, you'll enjoy some of Victoria's most popular attractions. With return travel from Melbourne included, it's the most convenient way to experience the Mornington Peninsula Hot Springs. Head straight to the Mornington Hot Springs and soak in the mineral-laden water. With a hilltop pool that boasts panoramic views of the region, it's an oasis of calm and natural beauty. Muscles soothed, you'll stop at Murrays Lookout in Arthurs Seat. Admire stunning views stretching from across the bay to the city from the highest point of the Mornington Peninsula, then cap off a dreamy day with a visit to Mount Martha's colourful beachside bathing boxes.

Peninsula Hot Springs: Entry Ticket + Transport

📍 Peninsula Hot Springs

At Peninsula Hot Springs, you can move between over 50 different pools, hydrotherapy areas, saunas, and a Turkish hammam. Natural mineral-rich water fills each setup, giving you several ways to unwind. There are spaces suited for both quiet time and family visits, with private bath options if you want more privacy. You’ll find a hilltop pool with a wide view, along with areas designed for different ages. When you need a break, the Bath House kiosk offers light meals and snacks for purchase.
