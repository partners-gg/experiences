Dolphin Discovery Centre Bunbury: Eco-Cruise Tour

📍 Dolphin Discovery Centre Bunbury

Enjoy a 90-minute Dolphin Eco-Cruise with the Dolphin Discovery Centre in Koombana Bay. The custom boat has a 300-degree viewing platform to see dolphins up close without getting wet. Watch dolphins and other wildlife in their natural habitat. See Bottlenose Dolphins as they go about their daily routines, including mothers with calves, playful juveniles, and hunting or traveling behaviors. Listen to the skipper who will be providing informative commentary throughout the cruise. Also, the boat has onboard toilet facilities and stays in sheltered waters for a comfortable experience.

📍 Dolphin Discovery Centre Bunbury

Dolphin Discovery Centre: Marine Discovery & Interpretive Centre Tour

📍 Dolphin Discovery Centre Bunbury

Visit the Dolphin Discovery Centre, a year-round destination for aquatic enthusiasts. Explore 14 aquariums and tanks featuring sharks, rays, fish, and many other marine species. Witness the world's first 360-degree Digital Dolphinarium with interactive displays and learning centers. Watch 3D and 2D movies, Virtual Reality sets, and a Discovery Touch Pool. Discover the Sea Turtle Rescue Station, Indigenous and Aboriginal History exhibits, and enjoy an Octopus display. If the wild dolphins of Koombana visit the beach in front of the center, guests can meet them in the Interaction Zone.
