Teplice: Royal Spa & Castle Day Trip from Prague

📍 Church of St. Bartholomew

Beethoven and Goethe once came to Teplice to soak their creative bones in what's now Central Europe's oldest spa town. However, it is Mozart who got a statue erected here, even though he never visited! Find out why on this fact-packed self-guided tour of Teplice. See the remains of the 4th-century martyr, St. Clarus, who became the patron saint of the spa. Tour Teplice Castle and step inside the magnificent Church of St. Bartholomew.
