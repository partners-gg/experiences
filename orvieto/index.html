📍 Orvieto Cathedral

Visit the Orvieto Cathedral, a historic cathedral in the Umbrian hills. The facade features golden mosaics and detailed sculptures. Inside, Gothic architecture showcases columns and frescoes. See the Chapel of San Brizio, where Luca Signorelli painted the Last Judgment. Access the museum and other areas within the complex with your ticket.

Cave System Pozzo della Cava

📍 Pozzo della Cava

Pozzo della Cava is a unique look at life dating back 27 centuries. Descend into the depths beneath Orvieto for a self-guided adventure through archaeological finds such as pre-Roman wells, pottery, kilns, and more. Imagine tunneling out a cave network with no hydraulic equipment! Find out how the caves have been used over the years, from bomb shelters to wine cellars. And don't miss a delicious home-cooked lunch in the restaurant afterward (at your own expense) complete with cave views.

Orvieto City Pass

📍 Orvieto Cathedral

Immerse yourself in Italy's historic city of Orvieto with a single-pass ticket to all of its best museums and attractions. Explore ceramics and bronze items excavated from the area, on view at the National Archaeological Museum. Glimpse the city's spiritual history at Orvieto Cathedral. See the vast complex that winds under the ancient quarter of Orvieto at the Well of the Quarry, and explore the foreboding Crocifisso del Tufo, an Etruscan necropolis that owes its name to a crucifix engraved in the tuff inside a rock chapel. By purchasing the Single Card, you're also entitled to discounts at other attractions.
