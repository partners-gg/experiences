📍 AccroCamp: Paris-Jablines

Fly through the trees with the birds and the bees! Imagination and adventure is the key at AccroCamp: Paris-Jablines. This adrenaline-pumping outdoor escape from Paris offers up everything you might need to get the heart pounding. Think rope bridges, climbing walls, ziplines and even a Tarzan liana (Tarzan impressions are optional). Don't miss the famous Quickjump, which lets you step up at the edge of the diving board at 15 meters high and literally leap from a tree!
