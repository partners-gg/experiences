Chinese Teacups Painting Workshop

📍 Ka-Atelier Painting Studio

If learning to decorate ceramics sounds like your cup of tea, escape the busy Star Street District to a cozy art studio, and immerse yourself in the fusion of Western and Chinese painting techniques, with a Chinese Teacups Painting Workshop. During the workshop you'll learn the decorative art of painting on dainty teacups, using a combination of Western and Chinese painting techniques. Learn to paint the shapes and shades of typical Chinese art motifs such as bamboo and plum blossoms during a relaxing class where you're free to experiment and get creative. The studio's resident artist will teach you how to combine Western and Chinese painting, and help you choose a theme to decorate your teacups with. You'll soon master the basics and can even bring your newly painted creation home as a gift for your favourite tea lover.
