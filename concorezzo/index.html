Acquaworld: Indoor and Outdoor Water Park

📍 Acquaworld

Acquaworld is the first indoor water park in Italy. It is open year-round with controlled air and water temperatures, where all pools are heated. The park is divided into four areas, namely, Fun Area, Wellness Area, Summer Area, and Tiki Bay. The Fun Area includes over 1,100 m of water slides for all ages, a wave pool, an outdoor pool usable year-round, and Miniworld, a play area for young children with small pools, slides, and water games. The Wellness Area is a 5,000 m² area with indoor pools, a waterfall cave, hydromassage tubs, a sauna, a Turkish bath, a salt room, and an outdoor relaxation pool open year-round. The Summer Area is an outdoor family area with children’s attractions such as the Pirate Spray Park and Mini Summer. Tiki Bay is an island-themed area with a white sand beach, water slides, water games, a restaurant, cabanas, Balinese beds, and the 800 m² Blue Paradise outdoor pool for adults and children.
