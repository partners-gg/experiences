AMA Motorcycle Hall of Fame Museum

📍 Motorcycle Hall of Fame Museum

Swing a leg over and roll on the throttle as you tour the AMA Motorcycle Hall of Fame Museum. A place where the stories of motorcycling greats are preserved and shared for all to enjoy. Learn about the triumphs and achievements of hall of famers as you tour the eight categories of the museum's Hall of Fame gallery: design and engineering, dirt track, road racing, motorcross and super cross, speciality competition, off-road, ambassadors and industry, and leadership and rights. The Hall of Fame is a self-guided tour across two floors, featuring vintage bikes, stories of the greats, and more. On the lower level, you'll find rare and one-of-a-kind objects, art, and of course, motorcycles from the museum collection. The upper level honors the lives and careers of AMA motorcycle hall of famers.
