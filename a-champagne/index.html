PRESSORIA – A Sensory Journey to the Heart of Champagne

📍 Pressoria - A Sensory Journey to the Heart of Champagne

With these tickets for PRESSORIA – A Sensory Journey to the Heart of Champagne, you can immerse yourself in the culture and history surrounding 'the wine of kings'. Pressoria is an interactive walkthrough experience at the restored former pressing center of Maison Pommery in Aÿ-Champagne, part of a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore at your own pace and treat your five senses. Learn about geology, climate, the hidden side of the vine, harvesting, champagne elaboration, effervescence, and so much more. Through this rich and playful experience, this famous tipple takes on a whole new dimension. The visit ends with a tasting experience of two champagnes, or grape juice if you prefer. Discover the diversity of the region's wines while enjoying breathtaking views over the vineyard.
