Speelland Beekse Bergen

📍 Beekse Bergen

Pack the kids into the car and explore a haven of outdoor escapism at Speelland Beekse Bergen. They'll adore the fresh air activities and the holiday atmosphere of this fun family destination. Head to the exciting aqua shuttle, where you are launched onto the water. You can climb and clamber on one of the many playground equipment. You can also obtain the one and only Speelland driving license on the Safari Buggy Track. While the kids dance along with Djambo or have fun in the various attractions, you as a parent can relax on the terrace or sandy beach.
