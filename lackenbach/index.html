Lackenbach Palace Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 Schloss Lackenbach

Step back in time and take a peek into the lives of the wealthy Esterházy family with tickets to the Lackenbach Palace Museum. Learn about the Battle of Lackenbach that took place around the property in 1620, and take in views of the 17th-century mound named "King's Hill". Then, head to the restored gardens and orchards surrounding the property with centuries-old trees and learn about the diverse habitats found in Burgenland at an interactive exhibit.
