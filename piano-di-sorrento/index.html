Pompeii: Guided Tour & Skip The Line Entry + Roundtrip from Sorrento

📍 Day Trips from Piano di Sorrento

Go on a trip back in time, roaming the cobblestone lanes of the ancient city of Pompeii (now, Pompei). This frozen-in-time town, encased by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius in 79 AD, earned a proud spot on the UNESCO World Heritage list. Pompeii’s excavation has been an epic archaeological adventure since the 18th century, unearthing priceless treasures. With the guidance of an expert, enter into the city's hidden corners, from the animated forum and grand Basilica to the luxurious patrician houses from 80 BC. See lovely frescoes that decorate the walls and floors, each narrating a unique story of the opulent Roman lifestyle. The archaeological site is so exquisitely preserved that it feels like stepping into a snapshot from 79 AD.
