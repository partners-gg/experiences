Fota House Tour

📍 Fota House, Arboretum & Gardens

Fota House offers a truly unique experience of the lives of the gentry and the staff who worked for them, and is one of Ireland’s finest examples of Regency-period architecture with superb neoclassical interior design. On this fully guided tour of Fota House, you'll learn all about the Smith-Barry family who lived on the estate over 200 years, and about the people who worked in the house. From the formal rooms to the working quarters, you'll get a perfect insight into life for everyone at Fota House.
