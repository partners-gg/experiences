📍 Tula Archaeological Zone

Mexico is home to some pretty spectacular remnants of ancient civilizations, and the remarkable Tula archeological site is one of the most awe-inspiring Mesoamerican site of them all. From Tula, you can see the beautiful Tula Valley and the historic town of Tula de Allende. Explore this incredible ancient site and admire the stunning views from the top of the pyramids, discover the Temple of Quetzalcoatl, and the archaeological dig sites. Meet the Atlanteans, the magnificent huge dark basalt statues of Toltec warriors, and learn the incredible myths and history surrounding this age-old site.
