📍 Maritime Museum & Marino Moretti House

Visit the Maritime Museum in Cesenatico and see a large arsenal with boats that once sailed the Adriatic Sea. Look at the Land Section to get an idea of historical maritime life, and check out the Floating Section, featuring ten boats. Learn about the life of seafarers: lower a sail, practice boat knots, and understand navigation techniques. Discover Cesenatico's past through sailing boats and archaeological finds from Roman settlements and Ad Novas. Explore Marino Moretti's house, now a museum. The poet bequeathed it to the city, keeping it intact for visitors to learn about his life and work.
