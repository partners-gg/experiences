Hard Rock Cafe Baltimore

📍 Hard Rock Cafe Baltimore

Located in the Inner Harbor of Baltimore, the Hard Rock Cafe sits in a uniquely retro building, between Harbor East and the world-renowned National Aquarium. Enjoy a 2- or 3-course meal, plus one non-alcoholic drink with your ticket as you gaze at the restaurant’s collection of musical memorabilia. Nothing pairs better with a juicy burger than a piece of American nostalgia. If you feel yourself craving more, menu items are available to purchase separately. Then, stop by the Rock Shop for some exclusive Baltimore souvenirs and gifts.

Spirit of Baltimore Lunch Cruise

📍 City Cruises Baltimore

Make it a trip to remember with the Spirit of Baltimore lunch cruise. No matter the occasion, City Cruises offer the perfect environment to get together and make memories.
