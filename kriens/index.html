KRIENS ·MOUNT PILATUS
Mt. Pilatus: Self-Guided Tour by Cable Car + Aerial Cableway from/to Kriens
Take an exciting trip to the beautiful Kriens Valley station, where a scenic 30-minute gondola ride awaits to take you to the snowy wonderland of Fräkmüntegg. Adventure-seekers can enjoy sledding routes or snowshoe trails back to Krienseregg. Furthermore, if you need gears, the Kriens Valley station has everything you need. Next, hop on the "Dragon Ride," a spectacular cableway that will transport you to the summit of Pilatus Kulm at a height of 2132 meters in just five minutes. At the summit, admire the incredible views or explore the Dragon World, where interactive games make the mythical Pilatus dragons come alive. When hunger strikes, eateries at Krienseregg, Fräkmüntegg, and Pilatus Kulm offer delicious local cuisine. Afterward, glide back to Kriens via cable car, concluding your mountain adventure.

KRIENS ·MOUNT PILATUS
Mount Pilatus: Self-Guided Silver Roundtrip from Lucerne + Train
Prepare for an exciting adventure starting from Lucerne. Begin by catching bus number 1 at the train depot for an 11-minute ride to "Kriens, Zentrum Pilatus." From there, take a short walk to reach the valley station of the panoramic gondola. Enjoy a 15-minute ride to the middle station Krienseregg, offering scenic views. Then, hop on the cable car for another 15-minute ride to Fräkmüntegg. For adventure-seekers, there's an option to visit the rope park at Fräkmüntegg (additional cost). Next, board the "Dragon Ride" aerial cableway for an exciting 5-minute ascent to Pilatus Kulm. Enjoy the highlight of the trip with a ride on the world's steepest cogwheel railway from Pilatus Kulm to Alpnachstad. Finally, relax on a train ride back to Lucerne. You can also take the trip in reverse, starting with the train from Lucerne. Just be sure to check the timetable beforehand.

KRIENS ·MOUNT PILATUS
Mount Pilatus: Self-Guided Golden Roundtrip from Lucerne + Boat Cruise
The Mount Pilatus adventure begins from Lucerne. Hop on bus number 1 at the train station for an 11-minute ride to 'Kriens, Zentrum Pilatus.' From there, a short walk takes you to the valley station of the panoramic gondolas in Kriens. Appreciate a 15-minute gondola ride to the mid-station Krienseregg, offering beautiful views. Then, continue for another 15 minutes to reach the station Fräkmüntegg. At Fräkmüntegg, adventurous souls can try the rope park (additional charges apply). Change to the 'Dragon Ride' aerial cableway for a brief yet exciting 5-minute ride to Pilatus Kulm. Feel the thrill of the descent with the world's steepest cogwheel railway to Alpnachstad, followed by a peaceful boat ride back to Lucerne. The trip can also be done in reverse, starting with the boat ride from Lucerne. Just remember to check the timetable beforehand, especially for the boat ride.

KRIENS ·MOUNT PILATUS
Mount Pilatus: Self-Guided Trip from/to Alpnachstad or Kriens
Prepare for an exciting adventure to Mount Pilatus. From either Alpnachstad or Kriens Valley station, enjoy a thrilling 30-minute ride on the world's steepest cogwheel railway or take in the beautiful landscapes from above on the panoramic gondola and aerial cableway "Dragon Ride." Your destination is the peak of Lucerne's iconic mountain, boasting unparalleled scenery. Take in the beauty of the Swiss mountains, Lake Lucerne, and the cityscape of Lucerne. Choose between the outdoor terrace or the indoor gallery for the best views. Explore the three peaks of Pilatus Kulm - Esel, Oberhaupt, or Tomlishorn. Walk amidst the Swiss mountains or along the Dragon Path. Learn about the legendary dragon resident of Pilatus in the Dragon World at Pilatus Kulm.
