SEA LIFE Malaysia: Entry Ticket

📍 SEA LIFE Malaysia

Be engaged and inspire children to learn, love, and care for the ocean through SEA LIFE Malaysia's compelling storytelling and up-close, interactive education journey. Showcasing 25 display tanks in 11 habitat zones, featuring over 13,000 sea creatures from 120 species, SEA LIFE Malaysia lets you meet some of the world's most incredible marine wildlife, from glowing jellyfish to bobbing seahorses and a rather famous black tip reef shark. Gently stroke a starfish at the Rockpool, and immerse yourself in a kaleidoscope of color at the Coral Reef, where you'll see recognizable clownfish and the regal blue tang. Be sure to stop by the one-of-a-kind Malaysian Rainforest zone, a specially crafted area that showcases Malaysia's rare and unique biodiversity.

Opt for access to SEA LIFE Malaysia, and you'll explore the deep while meeting its fascinating and mysterious creatures. Visit one, two, or all three parks over one or two days.
