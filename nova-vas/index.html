2 activities available for your group

Aquapark Istralandia: Entry Ticket

📍 Aquapark Istralandia

Put on your swimsuit and dive into Istralandia Aquapark in Croatia, recipient of the 2016 European Star Award for Europe’s Best Water Parks. Take your whole family for a memorable day out, with attractions suitable for thrill-seekers, smaller kids, and parents just looking to chill out. Relax at hydromassage pools, glide down exhilarating slides, or test your balance in the wet and wild wave pools. With your Istralandia Aquapark tickets, you also get access to activities like body painting and aqua zumba. So there’s always something to keep you entertained, if for some reason the water slides dry up! Forgot your goggles or swimming cap? Fret not! The Grand Bazar has everything you need to ensure your visit goes swimmingly. From swimming gear to souvenirs, the Grand Bazar has got you covered. All that splashing around is bound to make you hungry, so head on over to the restaurants where you can grab a bite, have a full meal, or delight in a tasty dessert.
