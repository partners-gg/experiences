CANBERRA ·CANBERRA CITY TOURS
Canberra: Guided Tour + Roundtrip Transfer
Explore Canberra's key sites with this tour of Australia's capital. Start with hotel pickup in a comfortable vehicle, then head to Mount Ainslie Lookout for views and an introduction to Canberra's design and history. Proceed to the Royal Military College Duntroon and Mount Pleasant for a brief stop. Visit the National Capital Exhibition to learn more about the city. Tour Old Parliament House to learn about over 50 years of political history. Have lunch (at your own expense) at the Queen's Terrace Café in New Parliament House. Continue with an in-depth tour of the Parliament House, guided by experts with political backgrounds. Finish the tour with a drive through the embassy district and a visit to the Australian War Memorial. Explore the exhibits and attend the Last Post Ceremony, a tribute to fallen service members.
