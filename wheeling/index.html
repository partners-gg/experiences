Oglebay Good Zoo: Entry Ticket

📍 Oglebay Good Zoo

Book your Oglebay Good Zoo general admission tickets in advance and prepare for an exciting, educational day at West Virginia's only AZA-accredited zoo. Kangaroo and Wallaby Encounter

📍 Oglebay Good Zoo

Make your wildlife dreams a reality with a once-in-a-lifetime animal encounter at Oglebay Good Zoo! Oglebay Good Zoo gets you up close and personal with some of the animal kingdom’s most fascinating species. Hop on a golf cart and head with the keeper to the kangaroo and wallaby habitat, where you'll meet your hoppy new friends. Work alongside zookeepers to feed and train the animals, and see why encounters like this aren’t just enjoyable for humans, they’re a form of animal enrichment, which stimulates the wallaby's senses and curiosity, and makes you a friend for life.
