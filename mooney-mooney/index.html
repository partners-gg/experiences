Broken Bay Pearl Farm: Entry + Boat Cruise

📍 Broken Bay Pearl Farm

Sail into the serene beauty of the Hawkesbury River's tranquil waterways and travel to the saltwater pools of the Broken Bay Pearl Farm. Let the breathtaking scenery charm you as the farm's expert skipper explains the fascinating symbiosis between the diverse world of oysters and their environment. Explore the intriguing life cycle of oysters, from humble beginnings to their grand finale, be it gracing a dinner plate as a seafood delicacy or transforming into a lustrous pearl.

📍 Broken Bay Pearl Farm

Learn about the features of the Sydney rock oyster, the Pacific oyster, and the Akoya pearl oyster through a guided tasting. Observe shucking techniques around the harvest table for fresh oyster preparation at home. See the waterways of the Hawkesbury River on a boat cruise while heading to the saltwater paddocks. View the landscape that enables optimal oyster cultivation and understand the interaction between the oysters and their environment.

Broken Bay Pearl Farm: Guided Tour + River Cruise + Optional Transfer

📍 Broken Bay Pearl Farm

Visit Broken Bay Pearl Farm to see the workings of New South Wales' exclusive pearl farm. Learn about the Australian Akoya pearls and the history of the family's pioneering methods in cultivation. Take a boat trip on the Hawkesbury River to observe an oyster growing area and understand pearl and oyster farming techniques. See how oysters contribute positively to the marine environment. Inside the pearl grading room, get an understanding of what makes Australian pearls rare. Conclude your trip with an oyster tasting session and observe a shucking demonstration, highlighting the efforts of local farming in the area.

Broken Bay Pearl Farm: Entry + Oyster Tasting

📍 Broken Bay Pearl Farm

Have a *shucking* good time at Broken Bay Pearl Farm with oyster tasting. This tour offers a taste and demonstration of the unique qualities of Sydney Rock Oyster, the luxuriously creamy Pacific oyster, and the Broken Bay Pearl Farm's crowning glory - the Akoya pearl oyster. Each guest has the pleasure of savoring 2-4 oysters, freshly plucked from the sea. Enhance your oyster escapade by coupling it with a delightful farm cruise. Be the first to witness the journey of these ocean gems from farm to plate.

Broken Bay Pearl Farm: Shell to Showroom

📍 Broken Bay Pearl Farm

Discover Broken Bay Pearl Farm on a relaxed two-hour guided experience. Meet the guides and learn how New South Wales’ only pearl farm operates, including the story of the family behind it. Find out how the Australian Akoya pearl is grown and what makes it unique. Take a boat cruise along the Hawkesbury River to visit an oyster lease. See how pearl and rock oysters are farmed and learn about the steps involved in producing pearls and seafood. You’ll also hear how oysters support the local marine environment. The tour ends in the pearl grading room, where you’ll see how pearls are assessed for quality and value.
