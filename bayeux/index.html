Normandy: D-Day Landings Tour + US Cemetery

📍 Normandy Day Trips from Bayeux

Visit the important sites of the D-Day landings with an English-speaking guide. Begin at Utah Beach, where American soldiers began their landing on June 6, 1944. Spend time at Omaha Beach, the site of heavy fighting and losses. At Pointe du Hoc, see the high cliffs where U.S. Army Rangers carried out a difficult mission. In Sainte-Mère-Église, learn about the first paratroopers who arrived before dawn and the impact on the local community. The tour includes a visit to the Normandy American Cemetery at Colleville-sur-Mer, a place of remembrance for those who gave their lives. Travel in a comfortable minivan with clear, informative commentary throughout the day.
