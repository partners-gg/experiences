Plitvice Lakes: Day Tour from Zagreb

📍 Plitvice Lakes National Park

Lose yourself in the beauty of the Plitvice Lakes. Centuries worth of chalk buildup in the lakes which reflect the sky has created the bluest water you've ever seen, with cascading rivers and dramatic waterfalls. It's the perfect chance to reconnect with nature. Your tour will take you from Zagreb to 'the Small Lakes of Plitvice' in Rastoke, before continuing on to Plitvice Lakes National Park. Learn about the natural landscape from a guide and walk enough to enjoy a hard-earned lunch.
