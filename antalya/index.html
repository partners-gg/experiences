Watch the Fire of Anatolia dance performance, recognized as a leading Turkish dance group, featuring 120 performers sharing Turkish classical and folk dance traditions. They perform in the well-known Aspendos Theater, a large ancient Roman amphitheater with notable seating capacity. Performances showcase choreography, music, and stories reflecting the cultural heritage of Turkey and Anatolia. Each performance presents intricate and fast-paced dance routines, exemplifying a historical and artistic tradition. Expect a visual presentation of Turkish dance forms within a historically significant architectural venue.
