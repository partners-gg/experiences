Turkish Night Marmaris

📍 Kervansaray Turkish Night

Welcome to an evening of Eastern Promise in the coastal town of Marmaris. Tap your feet to the sounds and movements echoed by generations of cultural heritage. See traditional belly dancers, whirling dervishes, and folk dancers twirl and groove in their vibrant costumes in a starry-night courtyard setting. A buffet of Turkish cuisine with a selection of local meze, barbecued chicken, and unlimited beer, wine, and soft drinks will keep you more than content!
