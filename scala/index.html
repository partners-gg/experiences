Ferriere Waterfalls: Guided Tour from Amalfi

📍 Scala Outdoor Activities

Take a guided walk in a Mediterranean forest in the Valle Delle Ferriere, a gorgeous path that winds from Amalfi through the lush hills of the Amalfi Coast, past ancient abandoned mills, ironworks, waterfalls, and sweet-smelling lemon terraces. On the way to the Ferriere Waterfalls, learn about the area's biodiversity, from wild orchids and spectacled salamanders, to a rare carnivorous plant (watch out!). Opt for lunch in an idyllic farmhouse on the way back. You can't beat those views!
