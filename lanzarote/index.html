La Corona Volcano & Famara Cliffs: Guided Tour + Transfers

📍 Lanzarote Outdoor Activities

Visit the northern part of the island to see two protected natural areas, La Corona Volcano and Famara Cliffs. Start in the village of Ye and head towards La Corona Volcano. On the way up to its crater, see agricultural landscapes and lava flows with large basaltic blocks extending towards the sea. Walk around the east side of the crater to view the volcano's 7.6 km-long lava tube, with 1.6 km submerged underwater. After descending, walk several kilometers along the central part of Famara Cliffs, which rise from sea level to over 500 m.

Parque Natural de los Volcanes: Guided Hike + Transfers + Snack

📍 Lanzarote Outdoor Activities

Visit the Parque Natural de los Volcanes, a site impacted by eruptions in 1730-1736 and 1824. Follow a route crossing lava flows, climbing volcanic cones with channels, tubes, lava lakes, and craters. Due to Lanzarote's arid climate, little vegetation grows, preserving the landscape like an open-air rock museum. Learn about the slow growth of life on lava and understand the Earth's ancient forces. This circular route starts at Testeina, circling Montaña Negra, Caldera Colorada, and Volcán del Cuervo.
