PIGEON FORGE ·TITANIC MUSEUM ATTRACTION
Titanic Museum Attraction: Entry Ticket + Audio Guide
Learn more about the story of the Titanic at the Titanic Museum Attraction. The museum features over 400 real artifacts valued at over four million dollars and offers a self-guided, interactive experience. When you arrive, you’ll be greeted by a staff member and given a boarding pass with the name of a real Titanic passenger. As you walk through the exhibits, you’ll learn about the 2,208 people on board through their personal stories. You can feel how cold the water was at 28 degrees, sit in a full-size lifeboat, view original photos from the ship, play a period-style piano, and explore recreated rooms, including a first-class stateroom, third-class cabin, and the Captain’s Bridge. You’ll also walk the Grand Staircase, built to match the original using the ship’s plans. It includes wood and ironwork made to look just like the original design.
