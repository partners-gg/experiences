The Salt Mountain of Cardona: Guided Visit

📍 Cardona Salt Mountain

Any adventurer worth their salt knows there's one mountain that's more savory than any other. The Cardona Salt Mountain is one of the most unique geological formations in Europe, and yep, you guessed it – it's made almost entirely out of salt! Plunge into the depths of this extraordinary and obscure landmark with a guided visit through the salty mines, and explore a spectacular underground world of otherworldly rock formations, as you learn about the amazing history of the site.
