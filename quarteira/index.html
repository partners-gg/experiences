Aquashow: Indoor Waterpark Entry

📍 Aquashow Park

Visit the Aquashow Indoor, the first covered waterpark in the Iberian Peninsula. Enjoy a space with heated air and water, making summer a reality all year round. In this new space, find family pools, Jacuzzis, water slides, a baby pool, water jets, waterfalls, an adventure pool with a climbing wall and hammock, a wave pool, and a children's pool with slides and water cannons. There is also an exclusive spa area for guests over 13 years old. Relax in a pool with Jacuzzis, water beds with bubbles, a fast-flowing river circuit, a sensory tour, a bucket shower, a Scottish shower, a shower with essential oils, an essence pool, a sauna, a Turkish bath, a spa, an ice-cold well, an ice fountain, and two massage rooms. Take a break from your routine and enjoy a unique experience.
