📍 LEGOLAND® Discovery Center Bay Area

Brick by colorful brick, LEGOLAND® Discovery Center is the ultimate indoor LEGO® playground, located inside the Great Mall in Milpitas, California. Inspire young imaginations with over 2 million LEGO bricks in one location. The indoor LEGO adventure is a 2-3 hour play experience with a LEGO themed ride, a 4D cinema featuring all of your favorite LEGO characters, and MINILAND™ – a LEGO recreation of the Bay Area’s most popular landmarks. Kids can get tips and tricks from the Master Model Builder in the Creative Workshop, as well as design their own LEGO car and test it out in LEGO Racers: Build & Test. They can also imagine their very own LEGO world in LEGO Friends: Heartlake City and more!
