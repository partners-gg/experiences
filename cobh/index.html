Titanic Trail: Guided Walking Tour of Cobh

📍 The Titanic Trail

Your heart (and feet) will go on, and on during this walking tour of Cobh, the Titanic's last port of call and home to Cobh's Titanic Experience. The brainchild of historian Dr. Michael Martin, the Titanic Trail walking tour brings the history of Cobh and the tragic ship to visitors each day at 11 am or 2 pm. Soak up eyewitness accounts and vivid narratives, all designed to bring the stories of the ship and its passengers back to life. Haunting, but fascinating stuff.

Cobh Cultural Tour

📍 Cobh Heritage Centre

Get some Atlantic air into your lungs and travel back through time on a guided heritage tour of Cobh. A touchstone of Irish history, this charming harbour town was the Titanic's final port of call and the last sight of home for so many emigrants on their way to the New World. A lively local guide will take you on a wandering walk along Cobh's coastal scenery and the Queenstown Story Heritage Centre, narrating the poignant stories of Irish emigration in rich detail along the way.

The Queenstown Story at Cobh Heritage Centre

📍 Cobh Heritage Centre

The Queenstown Story at Cobh Heritage Centre provides an emotive yet interesting and educational tour where the story of Irish emigration is told, from North America to the West Indies, Australia, and other destinations where the Irish diaspora settled. Learn the stories behind Irish emigration, and why not all Irish emigrants left the shores of the Emerald Isle voluntarily. Learn about the Great Famine and its major contribution to mass emigration, as well as the story of Titanic, the Lusitania, and their Cobh connection.
