Monkeyland and Plantation: Guided Tour + Tasting + Roundtrip Transfer

📍 Monkeyland

Prepare for a jungle adventure in the Dominican Republic. Start with a visit to 'Casa del Coco' to learn about coconut oil production and sample fresh organic products like roasted coffee, cacao, and mamajuana. Next, enjoy a 45-minute guided tour to meet the squirrel monkeys in their 5-acre jungle playground. These friendly monkeys might jump onto your shoulder and eat from your hands. Look out for mothers with their babies, jumping and climbing with agile grace. The monkeys also love posing for photos, so have your camera ready for some great shots!
