Puerto Morelos: Guided Taco & Tequila Tasting + Roundtrip Transfer

📍 Puerto Morelos City Tours

Visit the beautiful seaside town of Puerto Morelos, known for its fishing culture. Start with a taco tasting at a popular beachside restaurant, where you can enjoy flavorful fish tacos. Stop at a local shop for beef and chicken tacos served with zesty salsas for those who like it spicy. Head to the flea market area to try authentic Maya tacos featuring cochinita pibil, a slow-roasted pork dish. End your culinary adventure with a guided tequila tasting. Afterward, take some time to explore the main square. This tour offers a mix of delicious food and cultural experiences.
