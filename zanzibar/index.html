Zanzibar: Swimming with Dolphins Day Trip

📍 Zanzibar Activities

Starting from Stone Town, a quick 45-minute minivan ride will take you to Nungwi, where a boat will be waiting to sail you to Mnemba for an incredible dolphin encounter. Dive in and swim alongside these playful marine animals. Afterward, explore another underwater wonderland perfect for snorkeling. Take a plunge into the clear waters and marvel at the variety of colorful fish and vibrant coral reefs. Next, enjoy the sweet and refreshing flavors of watermelon, red banana, pineapple, coconut, passion fruit, mango, and avocado. The day culminates with a savory seafood lunch at a quaint restaurant near the picturesque Blue Moon.

Old Zanzibar Town: Guided Tour

📍 Zanzibar Activities

Set out for an exhilarating tour with a guide through Stone Town, the UNESCO-shielded gemstone of Zanzibar City. Explore the city's rich history and culture. The expedition begins with a meander through Stone Town's most iconic landmarks. Admire the grandeur of the House of Wonders, experience the historical resonance of the Old Fort, appreciate the royal charm of the Sultan's Palace, and pay respect to the former slave market. With each bend and turn through the city's tight lanes and concealed alleyways, the team will be swept up in the lively atmosphere, the humming sounds, and the irresistible aromas that form the city's dynamic culture. As the guide takes the team through the city, intriguing stories of Stone Town's history will unravel. From its modest origin as a trading port to its shadowy chapter in the slave trade, each narrative paints a vibrant portrait of the city's rich cultural heritage.
