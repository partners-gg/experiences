KGB Prison Cells: Entry Ticket

📍 KGB Prison Cells

Peer into the past and witness the dark shadow hanging over Tallinn's recent history, with a visit to the notorious KGB Prison Cells. Housed in one of the city's most elaborate Art Nouveau buildings, this detention center was used by the KGB to interrogate and torture suspected enemies of the state. In this powerful museum experience, you'll hear tales of bravery and defiance, and discover how the Estonian spirit remained unbroken in the face of unimaginable cruelty.

Fat Margaret: Skip The Line Ticket

📍 Fat Margaret

Tallinn's society and economy have been shaped by the sea for centuries. At the Fat Margaret cannon tower, you can learn about medieval shipping from the 16th century right up to the present day, through hundreds of unique artifacts. You'll also have the chance to see a medieval shipwreck known as the cog which remains in impressive condition. Learn about the Hanseatic trade network that benefited Estonia – from sails to steam power, you can find it all inside.
