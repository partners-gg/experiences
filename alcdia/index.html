Museo Sa Bassa Blanca: Hassan Fathy House Guided Visit

📍 Museu Sa Bassa Blanca

A visit to the Hassan Fathy House on the grounds of the Sa Bassa Blanca Museum is the perfect activity for all tastes, both for art lovers and for those who want to enjoy a unique visual experience. The Hassan Fathy House, designed by the great Egyptian architect Hassan Fathy, is home to works of different cultures and continents by great artists such as Domenico Gnoli, Vu Cao Dam, Yannick Vu, Meret Oppenheim, Henri Michaux, and more. In addition, its white walls, vaults, and traditional domes crown the terraces of the building that surround an inner courtyard with gardens and fountains. A visit is best enjoyed as part of a Hassan Fathy House guided tour, through which you can learn about contemporary art surrounded by nature near the Mediterranean coast.

Museu Sa Bassa Blanca: Skip The Line Ticket

📍 Museu Sa Bassa Blanca

The Museu Sa Bassa Blanca acts as a symbiotic space in which art, nature and architecture collide. It was founded by two artists – Yannick Vu and Ben Jakober – to promote cultural development as well as respect for the environment. Located in a spectacular setting, you'll be able to enjoy a unique sensory experience as you explore large outdoor sculptures, a house designed by pioneering Egyptian architect Hassan Fathy, underground rooms full of artistic treasures. A visit here will stimulate all your senses, leaving you with unforgettable memories.
