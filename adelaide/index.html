Gorge Wildlife Park: Entry Ticket
Visit the Gorge Wildlife Park, established in 1965 and still managed by the same family. Walk through 14 acres of land shaded by trees. Follow paths leading to one of the largest privately owned collections of Australian and exotic animals, birds, and reptiles in the country. Get close to the wildlife for an unforgettable experience.

ADELAIDE ·DAY TRIPS FROM ADELAIDE
Kangaroo Island: Day Tour from Adelaide
Visit Kangaroo Island, South Australia's top nature spot. Walk on the beach at **Seal Bay Conservation Park** with a National Parks Ranger, where you can see rare Australian sea lions up close. After lunch, explore **Flinders Chase National Park**. See echidnas, wallabies, reptiles, and koalas as you go through the park. Walk among the **Remarkable Rocks** for unique photos, then follow the boardwalk at **Admirals Arch** to see a rock formation and long-nosed fur seals. Finish your trip at **Kangaroo Island Wildlife Park**, where you can feed kangaroos and meet koalas. On the way back to Penneshaw for your ferry and coach transfer to Adelaide, watch for more wildlife.

ADELAIDE ·DAY TRIPS FROM ADELAIDE
Murray River: Day Tour from Adelaide with Cruise
Take a full-day **Murray River tour** from Adelaide, featuring a **2-hour cruise** along Australia's longest river. See South Australia's landscapes as you glide through the water, home to native fish, turtles, crayfish, and platypus. Start in **Adelaide** and enjoy a **lunch** during your outing. Pass by the **Angus and Torrens Valleys** and the **Walker Flat cliffs**. Stop at a **Mannum lookout** for more views. Learn about the days when **riverboat men** navigated the Murray, now with the comfort of a modern cruise. This tour provides a look into South Australia's history and natural environment. On the way back to Adelaide, enjoy a scenic drive through the beautiful **Adelaide Hills**, stopping at Melba’s Chocolate Factory for a sweet treat.

ADELAIDE ·ADELAIDE OVAL
Adelaide Oval Stadium Tour
Pull back the curtain of Australian sporting history with a 90-minute, behind-the-scenes tour of the iconic Adelaide Oval. The setting for titanic sporting occasions since it first opened its doors back in 1871, the vintage feel of the Oval has been preserved, and complemented with state-of-the-art facilities and architecture. This Adelaide Oval tour will take you on a journey across the Oval's storied past, narrating historic clashes that have unfolded before the roar of 50,000 fans. Climb the steps to the historic scoreboard, access usually off-limits areas, see gorgeous retro memorabilia, and more – all with the help of an expert guide.

RoofClimb Adelaide Oval
How about some adrenaline to go with your sightseeing?
