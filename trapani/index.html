Sicily Islands: One-Way Ferry Transfer from Trapani to Pantelleria
Take a ferry from Trapani directly to the island of Pantelleria. The journey lasts approximately 2 to 3 hours on a fast hydrofoil. Pantelleria is a volcanic island in the Mediterranean with unique geological characteristics. Its coastline is noteworthy for its features and is home to traditional stone buildings known as dammusi.

Favignana & Levanzo: Boat Tour from Trapani + Lunch
Begin the tour of Favignana and Levanzo islands from Trapani port. Arrive at Favignana and spend some free time exploring the town, shopping, visiting the Tonnara of Favignana Museum, or swimming at the beach near the port. Return to the boat and start the island tour along the coast, noting key spots like Scalo Cavallo, Cala Rossa, Grotta del Bue Marino, and Cala Azzurra Beach. Stop at one of these spots for a swim and enjoy a traditional Trapani lunch on the boat. After lunch, head to Cala Faraglione of Levanzo for free time to explore. Pass through other spots like Cala Fredda and Cala Minnola. Return to Trapani by evening.
