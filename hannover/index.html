SEA LIFE Hannover: Entry Ticket

📍 SEA LIFE Hannover

Step into the flippers of a marine biologist and enjoy a day of fishy fun with SEA LIFE Hannover tickets. This gigantic aquatic wildlife attraction is home to thousands of incredible creatures from deep blue oceans and fresh waters around the world. Get up close to crafty Cuban crocodiles, see piranha feeding frenzies, hang out with some gentle sea turtles and majestic rays, get hands-on in the interactive rockpools, explore a massive indoor rainforest, and lots more! From mangroves and coral reefs, to rivers and lagoons – wildlife lovers will be in their natural habitat at SEA LIFE Hannover.
