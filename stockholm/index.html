Experience the magic of the Christmas season by spending the holidays in Stockholm. With tickets for this private walking tour, a cheery local guide will show you around Old Town Stockholm. Falling snowflakes, roasted almonds, and warm gingerbread treats – the sights and smells of a Swedish Christmas surround you on the streets of Old Town Stockholm. Pass by the Storkyrkan Cathedral and the Royal Palace, all decked out for the season. On your merry Stockholm walking tour, count the advent candelabras in the windows and learn about Swedish Christmas customs as you listen to bells jingling on the streets. Cozy up with a steaming cup of spiced mulled wine and stroll through a local Christmas market to do some holiday shopping for everyone on your list.

STOCKHOLM ·VASA MUSEUM
Stockholm Old Town Walk + Vasa Museum
Starting from Stortorget, the main public square in Old Town Stockholm, you will set out on a guided walk of Sweden's capital city to discover its history and highlights. After exploring the hidden gems of Old Town, including the Royal Palace, the Swedish Parliament buildings, and the Nobel Museum, board a public ferry for a short sail between the nearby islands. Cross the harbor to the island of Djurgården, where more adventure awaits at one of the most popular museums in Sweden – the Vasa Museum. Enter the world-famous Vasa Museum, home to the world's oldest, best-preserved ship, the grand warship _Vasa_. This beauty was built in the 1600s when the Swedish Empire was the dominant naval force in Northern Europe. Not only is the _Vasa_ an artistic masterpiece, but she also has a fabulous story, including sinking on her maiden voyage. At the Vasa Museum, you'll get a brief overview of the major artifacts and the rest of the tour will be yours to explore independently.
