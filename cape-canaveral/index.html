The Wizard of Oz Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 The Wizard of Oz Museum

If you've got a feeling you're not in Kansas anymore, then you may be at the Wizard of Oz Museum in Cape Canaveral, Florida. With three clicks of your ruby red slippers, be transported to the magical world of L. Frank Baum's imagination. Step into an immersive light experience featuring state-of-the-art lasers and projectors that saturate the wall and floor of a gigantic room with quintessential scenes from America's first fairytale. See the Emerald City through the eyes of your favorite characters – from Dorothy's chaotic tornado, a visit to Munchkin Land, a stroll through the hypnotizing poppy fields and dark enchanted forest, to skipping along the iconic yellow brick road on your way to the glittering Emerald City. From there, explore the rest of the museum featuring valuable _Wizard of Oz_ memorabilia and collectibles from the world-famous franchise – including a first edition copy of the beloved book series.
