Jebel Jais Flight Zipline
Ride the Jebel Jais Flight Zipline – the longest in the world – stretching 2,830 m at an altitude of 1,680 m above sea level in Ras Al Khaimah. Jebel Jais is part of the Hajar mountain range, where you'll find a landscape shaped over millions of years, full of rugged rock faces and winding trails that attract challenge-seekers from across the region. The cooler climate encourages native trees and wild herbs, and is home to mountain foxes, goats, as well as ancient stone carvings. Glide above this varied terrain and finish your adventure on a transparent suspended platform set within the mountains.

RAS AL-KHAIMAH ·JEBEL JAIS
Jebel Jais Zipline
Not content with having the tallest building on Earth, the UAE is also home to another outstanding feat of human engineering: the world's longest zipline. Measuring 2,830 meters, you could start singing *Think* by Aretha Franklin at the beginning of your Jebel Jais flight, and finish your sky-high soul performance before reaching the end of the line – all while flying at speeds of up to 150 km/h! Now that's freedom. This is a true bucket list activity. At the top of the UAE's tallest mountain, Jebel Jais, you'll harness up with the help of professional staff, and zoom through the valleys with only the birds for company. **Facts about the Jebel Jais Zipline:** * Top speeds of up to 150 km/h * Flight time of up to three minutes * Longest zipline in the world, as recorded by Guinness World Records * 1,680 meters above sea level
