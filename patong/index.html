Phuket: Private Day Trip to Khao Sok with Longtail Boat Tour

📍 Khao Sok Day Trips from Pa Tong

Depart on a day-long adventure to Khao Sok National Park, often hailed as one of Thailand's hidden gems. With a private vehicle from Phuket and a 4-hour longtail boat ride, this tour offers an unforgettable exploration of Khao Sok's best features in one day. The day kicks off bright and early at 6:30 AM with a private hotel pickup from any location in Phuket. By 9:45 AM, you'll arrive at Khao Sok and head straight to the Cheow Lan Lake viewpoint. Gaze upon the beautiful vista before diving into your 4-hour private longtail boat tour, complete with a lunch stop at Cheow Lan Lake. During the experience, you'll spend 1.5 hours cruising through waters, followed by a 30-minute exploration of a mysterious cave, before a delicious Thai lunch. Guided by a seasoned local, you'll navigate the world's oldest rainforest, finally reaching the raft house. At 2:15 PM, the private boat tour ends, and you'll return to Phuket by 2:30 PM. By 6:00 PM, you'll be dropped off at your hotel.
