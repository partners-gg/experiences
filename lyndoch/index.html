Barossa Valley: 30-Minute Private Helicopter Flight

📍 Barossa Valley Helicopter Flights

See lush, serene views and vast plains from the start of your helicopter tour. Fly over the township of Lyndoch, the western ridge of Barossa Valley, and Seppeltsfield. Gaze at scenic vineyards, palm tree-lined roads, and the Seppelt Family mausoleum. Continue over Maggie Beer's farm, Wolf Blass winery, and the limestone quarry near Angaston. From Angaston, take in a 180-degree view of the Barossa Valley. Fly towards Tanunda and see historic sites like Chateau Tanunda and Bethany. Next, visit Rowland Flat and Jacob's Creek Winery, known for its large wine tanks. Finally, fly to the southern Barossa, over the Barossa Goldfields and the Whispering Wall.

Southern Barossa: 10-Minute Private Helicopter Flight

📍 Barossa Valley Helicopter Flights

Prepare for a helicopter tour starting from Hoffnungsthal Valley, flying northeast over Rowland Flat, the home of Jacob's Creek Winery. Continue to the southern Barossa, flying over Lyndoch, the Barossa Goldfields, and the Whispering Wall.
