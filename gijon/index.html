Bioparc Gijon Aquarium: Entry Ticket

📍 Bioparc Gijon Aquarium

Get to know the flora and fauna from the world's waters inside the 60 fresh and saltwater aquariums at Gijon Aquarium. With more than two million litres of water used to recreate 12 ecosystems, including the Caribbean Sea, the Pacific and Indian Oceans, the Subtropical Atlantic Ocean and plenty more. Keep your eyes peeled for houndsharks, grey sharks, moray eels, and rays. That's just a handful of the 4,000 animals from 400 species that you'll be able to see during your visit.
