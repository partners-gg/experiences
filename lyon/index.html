LYON ·MUSÉE DES CONFLUENCES
Musée des Confluences: Entry Ticket
Located south of Lyon, the spectacular structure of the Musée des Confluences is not only the merging point of the Rhône and Saône, but also of art and science. Inside, you can immerse your curiosity in the mysteries of human culture and natural science, with exhibitions that encourage dialogue between the disciplines of palaeontology, mineralogy, zoology, ethnography, archaeology, and science and technology. Egyptian mummies, a 155 million year old dinosaur skeleton, fossils, and a replica of Sputnik 2 are just some of the wonders you can expect to see inside. While the interior holds an incredible collection, the building is also a feat of art unto itself. Designed by the firm Coop Himmelb(l)au, the Musée des Confluences’ architecture suggests a place of discovery, wonder, and knowledge sharing dedicated to all audiences. Visitors can wander through a unique museum that facilitates dialogue between scientific disciplines to provide a more complete view of the world.

LYON ·ECLIPSO LYON
ECLIPSO Lyon: The Builders' Treasure - In the Footsteps of Egyptian Genius Entry
Join "The Builders' Treasure," a virtual reality expedition produced by Excurio and presented at ECLIPSO Lyon. The construction of the Great Pyramid is a mystery over 4,500 years old. Alongside Mona, a young and intrepid Egyptologist, and Bastet, a goddess, go on an adventure that blends science, mythology, and visual poetry. From the Giza plateau to the shores of the Red Sea, passing through the necropolis of Dahshur, you will observe the builders at work and infiltrate one of their ships in search of clues to try and solve the mystery of the pyramids' construction. This quest will give you a glimpse into the latest findings and lead you to explore the architectural theories that have emerged from them. From the quarries of Tourah to the ports of the Red Sea, follow the builders of a visionary civilization who joined forces to achieve monumental feats.

LYON ·AQUARIUM DU GRAND LYON
Aquarium du Grand Lyon: Entry Ticket
The Aquarium of Lyon is home to tropical species, four kinds of sharks, and fish from the Americas, Europe, and Africa. Keep an eye out for the colorful yellow-masked angelfish, the polka-dotted panther grouper, and prickly porcupinefish. The shark pit is the highlight of any visit. You'll have the chance to see Australian baby sharks along with several other shark species. Plus, all the usual aquarium suspects are there too like clownfish, seahorses, and jellyfish.

LYON ·LYON CITY CARDS
Lyon City Card
With the Lyon City Card, you can travel around the city with unlimited access to public transport and get direct entry to museums and their temporary exhibitions. The card also includes access to river cruises, guided walking tours, visits, and activities, along with discounts on leisure activities and shopping across the city. France’s third-largest city offers a wide range of cultural attractions and things to do. If you’re interested in art, you’ll have access to many of the city’s main galleries and their temporary exhibitions. Lyon has around 26 museums in total, covering subjects from modern art to film, that are included with the card. You can also see the city on a guided river cruise, move around easily using public transport, and enjoy discounts on a variety of activities. If you plan to shop, the card also offers savings in many shops and boutiques around the city.
