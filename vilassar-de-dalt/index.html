Illa Fantasia Waterpark: Fast Track Ticket

📍 Illa Fantasia Waterpark

Beat the Barcelona heat and give the kids a treat with tickets for Illa Fantasia, a family-friendly water park just outside of the city! There are 3 huge pools, an awesome range of water attractions including slides, a special area for kids, places for picnics and best of all: it's cool! With more than 22 watery attractions at Illa Fantasia, there's loads to keep you moving – try thrilling rides like Aquamania, perfect for the bravest and boldest aquatic adventurers! If adrenaline isn't what you're looking for, you can also take it easy and lounge around the 3 swimming pools, or pick a spot in the shade. Feeling active? Take the little ones to the children's park, put your swing to the test on the minigolf course, or board the pirate ship! It's nonstop splashtastic fun at Illa Fantasia – come with the family and soak it all up.
