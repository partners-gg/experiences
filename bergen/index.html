Bergen: Full-Day Fjord Cruise, Historic City Tour, & Fløien Funicular

📍 Bergen Day Trips

Embark on a scenic escapade through Bergen and her breathtaking fjords! Led by a knowledgeable English-speaking guide, stroll through historical landmarks, sail through awe-inspiring vistas, and witness panoramic views from the peak of the Fløien Funicular. The journey commences at the waterfront, where a mesmerizing 3-hour cruise to the Alversund Streams awaits. Get enveloped by steep cliffs and tumbling waterfalls as the ship navigates the narrow fjord passages, making way into the heart of Western Norway's countryside. Onboard a cozy boat fitted with sundecks and indoor lounges, soak up the one-of-a-kind Norwegian fjord views from every vantage point. Once the boat docks, it's time to explore the heart of Bergen. The guide navigates through the city's historic core, spotlighting some of the oldest and most iconic locations. The trail includes Saint Mary's Church, the Bergenhus Fortress, and the charming Hanseatic quarter, fondly known as Bryggen, on the waterfront. Throughout the journey, the guide provides engaging insights, making each step a delightful discovery.
