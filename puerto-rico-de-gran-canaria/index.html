Gran Canaria: Dolphin Watching Tour

📍 Gran Canaria Water Activities

Set sail aboard the _Spirit of the Sea_ in search of whales, dolphins, and other marine wildlife off the coast of Gran Canaria. With up to 30 species of local marine mammals identified, this area claims the greatest diversity of marine mammals in Europe. Head out from Puerto Rico and see what is really "under the sea" on a glass-bottom boat. Relax and roll with the waves as you sip a complimentary drink and let the professional, multilingual crew keep you informed about whale and dolphin sightings. Cool off with a dip in a sheltered cove, a feature included in the three-hour tour option. No whale or dolphin sightings? No problem. Get a free voucher for another dolphin excursion if you don't see any marine wildlife on your scheduled tour.
