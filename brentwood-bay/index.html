Roundtrip Transport from Victoria to Butchart Gardens

📍 The Butchart Gardens

Travelling to The Butchart Gardens? We’ve got you covered! Join Sightseeing Victoria for your fully narrated roundtrip transportation between Downtown Victoria, and The Butchart Gardens. Hop aboard a luxury coach in Victoria and be whisked to the privately owned 55-acre haven known as Butchart Gardens, just 35 minutes north of downtown. This dazzling green wonderland opened 100 years ago, and continues to enchant everyone who enters! Butchart Gardens is a _National Historic Site_ of Canada and it's not hard to see why. The five different gardens and five different water features combine to leave you enchanted by nature and its various blooms and plants. It's an essential Canadian experience. **Please note:** This ticket is for **Roundtrip Transportation Only**, and does not include entry to The Butchart Gardens.

Best of Victoria with Malahat Skywalk & Butchart Gardens

📍 The Butchart Gardens

Gear up for a captivating escapade through Victoria's finest gems with this intimate small-group tour. Beginning with a stress-free pickup from any downtown Victoria hotel, kick back and enjoy a drive sprinkled with fascinating facts and narratives. First on the itinerary, the Malahat Skywalk, an awe-inspiring spectacle that promises heart-stopping panoramas from sky-high walkways and an impressive 10-story spiral tower. Next, the adventure shifts gears onto a scenic ferry crossing. Soak in the picturesque vistas while cruising to the globally acclaimed jewel of horticulture, Butchart Gardens. The return trip to Victoria unveils a different perspective, spotlighting the city's remarkable landmarks. You will see sights including the Government House, Craigdarroch Castle, Beacon Hill Park, Breakwater Lighthouse and more.
