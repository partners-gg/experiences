Verona: Valpolicella Winery Tour with a Winemaker

MARANO DI VALPOLICELLA ·VALPOLICELLA WINE TOURS
Valpolicella: Food & Olive Oil Tasting + Optional Transportation
Start your adventure with a short walk through the Garden of Olives, and listen to the story of a local family and their production of wine and oil. Stop by the 15th-century farmhouse for a visit, and see the rooms where oil was kept centuries ago, as well as the original cellar. Learn about the production of Italian Extra Virgin Oil, and how to correctly identify a high-quality oil by the label! Afterwards, get some first-hand experience on the subject by tasting various regional types of olive oil. Try varieties from Puglia, Tuscany, Liguria, and Veneto, before tasting the Tenuta Santa Maria Valverde organic olive oil. Your tasting is combined alongside typical Venetian products, including cold cuts and cheeses with bread and special mustard. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the views from the terrace!

MARANO DI VALPOLICELLA ·VALPOLICELLA WINE TOURS
Valpolicella Vineyards: E-bike Tour with Wine Tasting
Discover the magnificent natural and architectural treasures of Valpolicella on this electric bicycle tour! Follow scenic, winding paths, take shortcuts along the vineyard rows, go off-road, and wade through small bodies of water during this adventure on two wheels. You'll visit picturesque medieval villages in the north-west of the province of Verona, and get a truly unique view of the valley and the iconic Lake Garda. Afterwards, return to Tenuta di Marano di Valpolicella and relax with a special local wine tasting, led by the producer themselves. Refresh yourself after all that biking with a light lunch based on local products, specifically chosen to pair with red wines. An unmissable tour for fans of good wine and fresh air!
