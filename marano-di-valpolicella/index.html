Verona: Valpolicella Winery Tour with a Winemaker

📍 Valpolicella Wine Tours

Live your best life. Visit a real Italian winery, soak in the sun, and sample the local specialty. Look out at rolling hills, lush green vines, and take a moment to relax. Start with a walk around the vineyard, while your guide tells you the five-generation family history of this estate. After that, learn how to dry grapes, see the ancient cellar where the magic happens, and finish up with a tasting of some fine wines, cheese, salami, and olive oil. It's Italian hospitality at its finest.

📍 Valpolicella Wine Tours

Explore Italy's Valpolicella region with an exclusive winery cellar tour and wine-tasting experience. Enjoy amazing views of the winery and visit the cellar to learn about wine-making techniques with a guide. Taste fine wines like Valpolicella Classico, Valpolicella Superiore, Recioto, and Amarone, experiencing the tradition from vineyard to bottling. End the tour with a tasting of six Valpolicella wines paired with local cured meats and cheeses, guided by an expert to deepen your knowledge of Italian wines.

Verona: Vapolicella Vineyard Tour + Wine Tasting with Lunch

📍 Valpolicella Wine Tours

Between the Lake Garda and Verona, in the heart of Valpolicella, you can join an exclusive winery tour and a Valpolicella wine tasting experience, perfectly paired with a light lunch of local specialties. Soak in the stunning landscape and savor a 2-hour experience in a unique setting. Listen to the history of the winery and learn about the production methods and techniques that go back generations. The experience includes a guided tour of the cellar, a tasting of 6 high-quality wines, such as the famous Valpolicella Classico & Valpolicella Superiore, Recioto, and Amarone. To top it all, indulge in a light lunch composed of delightful local produce.

Valpolicella: Food & Olive Oil Tasting + Optional Transportation

📍 Valpolicella Wine Tours

Start your adventure with a short walk through the Garden of Olives, and listen to the story of a local family and their production of wine and oil. Stop by the 15th-century farmhouse for a visit, and see the rooms where oil was kept centuries ago, as well as the original cellar. Learn about the production of Italian Extra Virgin Oil, and how to correctly identify a high-quality oil by the label! Afterwards, get some first-hand experience on the subject by tasting various regional types of olive oil. Try varieties from Puglia, Tuscany, Liguria, and Veneto, before tasting the Tenuta Santa Maria Valverde organic olive oil. Your tasting is combined alongside typical Venetian products, including cold cuts and cheeses with bread and special mustard. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the views from the terrace!

Valpolicella Vineyards: E-bike Tour with Wine Tasting

📍 Valpolicella Wine Tours

Discover the magnificent natural and architectural treasures of Valpolicella on this electric bicycle tour! Follow scenic, winding paths, take shortcuts along the vineyard rows, go off-road, and wade through small bodies of water during this adventure on two wheels. You'll visit picturesque medieval villages in the north-west of the province of Verona, and get a truly unique view of the valley and the iconic Lake Garda. Afterwards, return to Tenuta di Marano di Valpolicella and relax with a special local wine tasting, led by the producer themselves. Refresh yourself after all that biking with a light lunch based on local products, specifically chosen to pair with red wines. An unmissable tour for fans of good wine and fresh air!
