Basilica of Rennes: Immersive Sound and Light Show - LUMINISCENCE

📍 LUMINISCENCE Rennes

See the artistic production LUMINISCENCE in Rennes, inside the Basilica of Saint-Aubin – Notre-Dame de Bonne Nouvelle. This show highlights historical storytelling through 360° video mapping combined with musical performances. You can watch the live choir version on Fridays and Saturdays, where choir performances complement the audiovisual representation. Alternatively, the digital music rendition occurs on Wednesdays and Thursdays, with an original soundtrack accompanying the visual narrative.
