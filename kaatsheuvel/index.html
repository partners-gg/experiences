Efteling Theme Park: Entry Ticket + Transport from Amsterdam

📍 Efteling

Get ready to explore Efteling, the most enchanting theme park, where the magic of fairy tales comes to life amidst the thrill of world-class rollercoasters. The park brims with exciting attractions, guaranteed to create memories that last a lifetime. Brace yourself for the Python, a looping roller coaster that mimics the twists and turns of a slithering snake. This attraction boasts a heart-stopping double loop and a mind-bending corkscrew. For those who prefer a calmer ride, Joris en de Draak, a dragon-themed, double-lane wooden roller coaster, provides a relatively smoother ride, making it the perfect introduction for roller coaster rookies. Then there's Baron 1898, Efteling's latest offering that will take your breath away! Experience the gut-churning sensation of free-falling 123 feet at a speed of 56 mph, interspersed with dizzying spins. The Baron is a must-try for thrill-seekers craving an intense adrenaline shot!
