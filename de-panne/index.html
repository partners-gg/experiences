📍 Plopsaqua De Panne

Guaranteed water fun! Get ready for a water adventure at Plopsaqua De Panne! Right next to Plopsaland De Panne, this popular water park offers over 15 water attractions for all ages. Join Vic the Viking on a frosty journey through the Viking village – but beware! Several times a day, a thunderstorm erupts over The Storm Bath, bringing waves and heavy rain. Looking for even more excitement? Hop onto the Sliding Tires and twist your way down this super-fun slide in a giant tube! Party lovers won’t want to miss the Disco Slide, where flashing lights and music make for a fun ride. Still not enough? Take on the Sky Drop, a heart-pounding free fall from 20 meters high at a top speed of 40 km/h!
