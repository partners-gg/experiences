Broome: Sights Afternoon Tour

📍 Willie Creek Pearl Farm

Get ready for a thrilling journey with a visit to Willie Creek Pearl Luggers. Dive into the deep-sea stories of the early pearl divers while savouring the rich history of the perilous sea. Next, the adventure takes you to Chinatown to witness Sun Pictures, the world's oldest operating picture garden, a testament to the enduring spirit of cinema. The journey continues with a stopover at the local Spinifex Brewery Cable Beach. Here, you can indulge in a delightful beer tasting session complemented by a selection of light snacks. The next destination is the historic Town Beach, where you'll uncover the tales of Broome's One Day War. From there, the tour leads you to the Japanese Cemetery, a poignant reminder of the close relationship between Broome and its thriving pearling industry. The exploration doesn't stop there! The tour then ventures to Broome Port, famed for its status as the largest deep-water access port servicing the Kimberley region. At Gantheaume Point, marvel at the stunning red rock cliff face, discover ancient dinosaur footprints, and take in the beauty of Anastasia's Pool. The journey culminates with a visit to the picturesque Cable Beach before heading down to Gantheaume Beach. Here, you can relax with light refreshments and nibbles while watching the breathtaking sunset. To round off the adventure, the tour includes a drop-off service to your hotel. So, book your tickets now and dive into the exciting history and breathtaking beauty of Broome.

Dampier Peninsula: Full-Day Coach Tour

📍 Cygnet Bay Pearl Farm

Embark on a thrilling journey just a stone's throw from Broome, where the first stop is Beagle Bay, renowned for its exquisite Shell Church. Enjoy a morning tea in this serene setting (entry fees included, though Shell Church does ask for individual donations). Next, delve into the charm of Cygnet Bay Pearl Farm, nestled on the Dampier Peninsula's edge. Savor a gourmet lunch prepared by an expert chef before immersing yourself in the captivating Pearl Farm Tour. As Australia's oldest operating Pearl Farm, this family-run gem offers a unique chance to marvel at some of the finest South Sea Pearls in their showroom. Post-lunch, journey to One Arm Point - Ardyaloon, a secluded Indigenous community offering breathtaking views of the Buccaneer Archipelago. Dive into the rich culture and saltwater traditions of the Bardi Jawis at the trochus hatchery. Grab the chance to buy local art and artifacts (cash only) and bask in an afternoon swim at the beach. Wrap up the day with a relaxing coach ride back to Broome, arriving around 7:30 pm. This ticketed adventure promises a day brimming with discovery, beauty, and culture - a must-book for any curious traveler!
