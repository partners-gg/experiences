Anchorage: Guided City Tour & Chocolate Factory Visit

📍 Bus Tours in Anchorage

Join a guided tour in Anchorage. Visit shops and attractions, learn about Alaskan history, and try regional foods like smoked salmon and reindeer sausage. Observe the making of traditional Ulu knives. Take a stop at a chocolate factory near a waterfall to try handmade chocolate. Head to Earthquake Park to learn about the 1964 earthquake's impact.
