BILBAO ·GUGGENHEIM MUSEUM BILBAO
Guggenheim Bilbao Museum: Frank Gehry and Bilbao Tour + Admission
Join a knowledgeable guide and discover one of the most avant-garde museums in the world, the Guggenheim Museum, designed by Frank Gehry. With a surface area of 24,000 m2, the building represents an architectural landmark; this sculptural and spectacular structure is perfectly integrated into the urban fabric of Bilbao and its surroundings. Discover the history of the creation, and construction, of one of the most outstanding buildings of the 20th century and let yourself be amazed by Puppy, Mama, or Los Tulipanes, among other works of art that form part of the Guggenheim's exterior. Once the guided tour is over, you can explore the interior of the museum at your own pace and enjoy the audio guide that the museum provides to continue discovering this architectural marvel and its exhibitions.

BILBAO ·ITSASMUSEUM – BILBAO MARITIME MUSEUM
Itsasmuseum Bilbao
For centuries, the sea has been a gateway to the world for Bizkaia and its people. Perched on the banks of the city's estuary, Itsasmuseum details the Basque Country's seafaring past, from Ria de Bilbao to the Bay of Biscay. Delve into Biscay's former life as a shipbuilding hub in the 20th century, and discover the Ria de Bilbao's importance to the area's economic and social development. The Port Center features interactive and audiovisual exhibits explaining how the Bilbao Port operates.

BILBAO ·GAZTELUGATXE
San Juan de Gaztelugatxe, Bermeo, Mundaka & Gernika: Day Tour from Bilbao
Take a scenic bus route from Bilbao to discover the beauty of the Basque Country with a guided tour. Go on a tour from **Bilbao** and leave for **San Juan de Gaztelugatxe**, an islet that can only be accessed by a pedestrian path. Take a panoramic tour of the area and discover the dreamy cliffs – a feature of the HBO hit series, _Game of Thrones_. Admire the traditional fishing port, the **church of San Salvador**, and the monument built in honor of Juan Sebastián Elcano, a world-famous sailor born in this small town. See the **Church of San Vicente** and the **Basilica of Santa María del Coro** before heading back to Bilbao.
