Caen Memorial Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 Caen Memorial Museum

Discover 20th century history at the Caen Memorial Museum. Go on a journey from the end of World War I to the end of the Cold War thanks to a wide variety of archive films, photos and artifacts, including a 20-minute film summarizing key events of the period. Have an unforgettable visit learning about the events that left their mark on Europe – and the world – from 1914 to 1989 and beyond. Normandy: Day Trip from Paris with D-Day Beach, Cemetery & Cider Tasting

📍 Normandy Day Trips from Paris

Retrace the steps of the brave men who took part in the largest military operation in the history of the world! This unforgettable day trip from Paris lets you walk the sands of Omaha Beach, peer over the cliffs at the Pointe du Hoc, and step inside old German bunkers, complete with their cannons still pointing at England! On June 6th, 1944 an unprecedented event took place on the Beaches of Normandy. It was a highly detailed and extremely difficult offensive attack on the Third Reich by more than 150,000 Allied troops arriving by sea and air. Whether you’re a military history buff or someone who is just curious about D-Day, your guide will share the events of June 6th, 1944, so you can better appreciate the remarkable acts of bravery and selfless sacrifice that once shook the shores of Normandy. You will also get to sample some scrumptious Normandy apple cider at a family-owned Cider House.
