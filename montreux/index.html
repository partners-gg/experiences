Lausanne to Évian Ferry
Looking for a quick, money-saving way to get from one side of Lake Geneva to the other, that's also pretty easy on the planet? Simply hop on the ferry. Fort de Chillon History Museum: Entry Ticket
Learn about the enigmatic tactics of national defense, brought to life through interactive quizzes, immersive games, and breathtaking 3D virtual reality. Or else, walk in the shoes of a valiant soldier, accentuated by visuals and soundscapes. The museum's design, a masterpiece by the globally acclaimed François Confino, promises to impress guests. The museum also features a delightful restaurant, exclusive event rooms, a charming shop, and a panoramic terrace. Swiss Riviera Boat Tour
Switzerland’s staggering scenery is something to experience, and Montreux brings the best the country has to offer to one shoreline. Situated in the western Alps, you’ll sail the tranquil waters of Lake Geneva and pass by everything from a centuries-old castle to a wetland nature reserve. Your ship will bring you by a number of quaint villages too, but the star of the show is the Alpine views and the trees that dominate the lake’s coast.
