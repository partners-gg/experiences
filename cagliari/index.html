Cagliari: One-Way Train Transfer from Elmas Airport to Cagliari Station

📍 Sardinia Airport transfers

Take the train from Elmas Airport (CAG) to Cagliari Station for a quick and easy trip from the airport and get ready to see the sights in Sardinia. The train is the fastest and most reliable way to the city center from the airport. The trip takes just around 7 minutes, much quicker than a taxi or dealing with traffic. The airport's train station is a short 5-minute walk from the check-in area, making your transfer straightforward. With frequent departures throughout the day, you can find a train that fits your flight schedule.

Cagliari: Guided Boat Tour + Swim Stops + Snorkeling + Glass of Prosecco

📍 Cagliari Cruises & Boat Tours

Walk a few minutes from the city center to reach the departure point at Cagliari City Center (Via Roma). Board an 8/9 m RIB boat with a professional skipper who will guide you through the bays of the Devil’s Saddle. Navigate closer to the coast for better views and visit parts of the coastline only accessible by RIB boat. Listen to local stories from your skipper as you travel and look out for dolphins along the way. Stop at 4 locations chosen by the skipper. Relax onboard, swim, and snorkel with the provided gear. Meet other travelers onboard and enjoy a glass of prosecco during the tour.
