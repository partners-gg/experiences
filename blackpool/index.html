Blackpool Tower Ballroom: Entry Ticket

📍 Blackpool Tower Ballroom

Dating back to 1894, The Blackpool Tower Ballroom is famous for its unique sprung dance floor and spectacular architecture. It's a *must-visit* destination for dance fans from across the globe. Mid-way through every Strictly Come Dancing season, the BBC team pack up their sequins and head up north to this very place for a very special episode! Dance the day away to the sounds of the famous Wurlitzer organ, watch from the sidelines, or observe the floor in action from up on the balconies. The Blackpool Tower Ballroom is an experience to impress even those with two left feet!
