Gili Islands: Full-Day Guided Tour + Roundtrip Transfer from Lombok

📍 Day Trips from Lombok

Prepare for a full-day trip from Lombok to the Gili Islands. Begin the tour with an early pickup from your accommodation and head to Teluk Kodek Harbor. Board a glass-bottom boat and watch fish swim below. Visit Gili Air and snorkel in the clear waters to see fish, coral, and possibly a turtle. Head to Gili Meno, home to a turtle sanctuary and underwater statues known as "The Nest." Bring a waterproof camera for photos. Continue to Gili Trawangan for lunch and some beach time. Return to Lombok after exploring the islands, making a final stop at Malimbu Hill for photos before heading back to your accommodation.
