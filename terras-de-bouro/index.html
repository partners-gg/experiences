Peneda Geres National Park: Waterfalls Day Tour

📍 Peneda Geres National Park Activites

This all-encompassing experience starts with a visit to the Pedra Bela viewpoint to enjoy the view over the Gêres village. A visit to Cascata do Arado follows, to explore the various waterfalls and lagoons of the area (more than 3). Next, in the village of Fafião, you can have lunch in a traditional restaurant and see other fantastic landscapes of the Gerês Mountains. More waterfalls wait for you in the village of Pincães, like the Tahiti Waterfall, where you can dive and swim everywhere if the weather complies.
