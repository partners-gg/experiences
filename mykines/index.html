MYKINES ·MYCENAE, NAFPLIO & EPIDAURUS DAY TRIPS FROM ATHENS
Mycenae, Epidaurus & Nafplio: Full-Day VR Audio Guided Tour from Athens
Board a modern, air-conditioned coach at one of four central locations and travel to the Peloponnese region. Along the way to Mycenae, witness the Corinth Canal, an impressive engineering feat connecting the Aegean and Ionian seas. Explore Mycenae, a city of ancient Greek importance. Discover the history within the Mycenaean Acropolis, the tomb of Agamemnon, and the ruins of the Cyclopean walls that once protected this influential city. Go into the past with a visit to the Archaeological Museum of Mycenae. With an audio-guided tour available in eight languages and a virtual reality device at your disposal, the ancient world is open for exploration. Continue your journey to the ancient Theater of Epidaurus, a 4th-century masterpiece and one of the best-preserved Classical Greek structures. Finally, take a break in the lovely city of Nafplion, Greece's first capital post-independence and a significant port since the Bronze Age.
