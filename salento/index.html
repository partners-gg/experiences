📍 Outdoor Activities in Salento

Explore Cocora Valley on a shared tour. Start at the central square of Salento. The guide will carry a yellow umbrella for identification and pick you up. After that, transport will take you to the valley, famous for its tall wax palms and green landscapes. The guide will provide information about the area's plants, animals, and history. For simplicity, it includes all tickets and transportation from Salento. Cocora Valley features the world's tallest palm trees, the wax palms, which reach over 60 meters in height. The valley is part of Los Nevados National Natural Park, which gives visitors access to stunning scenery and opportunities for outdoor activities. Ideal for those interested in nature, this shared guided tour offers a convenient way to see one of Colombia's top destinations.
