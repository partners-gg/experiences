Pilsen: Historical Underground Tour with a Glass of Beer

📍 Pilsen Beer Experience

The tunnels, cellars, and wells at the Pilsen Historical Underground date back to the 13th century, offering a fascinating glimpse into the past right beneath the lively streets of Pilsen. Uncover ancient artifacts and learn how this underground world was used, giving you a glimpse into the daily lives of people in Middle Age Pilsen. Pilsen: Brewery Museum Entrance with a Glass of Beer

📍 Pilsen Beer Experience

Join an immersive experience at the Brewery Museum in Plzeň, the sole beer sanctuary in Bohemia. This unique venue holds the key to an unparalleled beer-brewing legacy, renowned worldwide. Dive into the vibrant history of Plzeň's pubs and explore the inner workings of the malt shop, kiln, rollers, and an intriguing beer laboratory. As an extra treat, each guest receives a beer voucher—redeemable at selected Pilsen eateries—for a taste of the iconic 0.3l Pilsner Urquell beer (available to those aged 18 and above).
