SEA LIFE Paris Aquarium

📍 SEA LIFE Paris Val d'Europe

Over 2250 creatures across 230 species are waiting to share their life underwater at SEA LIFE Paris. There are 12 species of sharks, sea stars, seahorses, green turtles, rays, and many more to meet! Touch starfish and more in the rock pools. Waddle on down to the penguin area for an all-new sensory experience with the black and white-suited gentoo penguins. Or create your own sea creature and see it come to life in the Virtual Aquarium. It's all the fun of diving, without getting wet.
