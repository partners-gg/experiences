St Johann in Tirol: Hornpark Climbing Forest

📍 Outdoor Activities in Sankt Johann in Tirol

Visit Hornpark Climbing Forest in St. Johann in Tirol for a high-ropes course that offers fun for all ages. Hornpark, next to Angereralm mountain lake, provides views of the Wilder Kaiser. With over 11 courses and 106 elements of different difficulty levels, there's a challenge for everyone. Designed for kids aged three and up, Hornpark is a family-friendly spot. St. Johann in Tyrol: Tandem Paragliding

📍 Outdoor Activities in Sankt Johann in Tirol

Get ready to soar! Start your adventure with a 20-min cable car ride to the mountain's peak, set up by the pilot. The pilot will give you a quick introduction to the basics of flight and gear you up. Then, it's lift-off! Launching from 1600 meters, you'll see amazing views of the region. Enjoy a calm glide through the sky or, if you want more excitement, ask your pilot for some acrobatics. Want to take control? You might even get a chance to steer the paraglider yourself! No prior experience is needed, making it a perfect opportunity to try paragliding in the Austrian Alps.
