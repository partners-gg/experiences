Livorno: Hop-on Hop-off Bus

📍 Acquario di Livorno

This hop-on hop-off sightseeing tour whisks you to all the cultural and historical highlights of this Tuscan port-city paradise. The tour starts from the central square and goes through the picturesque district of Venice, with its characteristic canals, the two Medici fortresses and the American market. It then continues towards the waterfront, with the large Terrazza Mascagni, the Aquarium, the birthplace of Amedeo Modigliani and the historic covered central market, until getting to the sanctuary of Montenero, a pilgrimage center.
