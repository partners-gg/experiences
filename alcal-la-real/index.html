📍 Fortress of La Mota

La Fortaleza' often attracts and surprises travelers with its imposing silhouette, history and heritage. Fortaleza de La Mota: Guided Tour

📍 Fortress of La Mota

During this 90-minute tour of Fortaleza de La Mota you'll join a live guide and walk through the monument, learning more about its secrets along the way. You'll get a feel for how people lived in the Middle Ages as you go through the medieval city and see areas including the alcazaba and its parade ground, the Mayor Abbey Church, and the Torre de la Cárcel. This Fortaleza de La Mota ticket also gives you access to an audiovisual show inside the abbey church that recreates the history of the monument.
