ROTTERDAM ·REMASTERED
Remastered in Rotterdam: An Audiovisual Experience
Experience the masters of Dutch art like you never have before. Famous works of art have been brought to life using 60 state-of-the-art projectors, 50 loudspeakers, 15 km of cables, 20,000-pixel resolution, and Europe’s largest indoor LED screen. You’ll be able to experience Hieronymus Bosch’s _The Garden of Earthly Delights_, Van Gogh’s colorful scenes, and Mondrian’s _Victory Boogie Woogie_ in immersive new ways that will give you a new appreciation for their talent. There are six rooms spread across 1,500 square meters, and there’s also the chance to create your own 3D digital art, which you can then scan and project onto the largest indoor screen in Europe. Get ready to walk through a pixel waterfall, sail through clouds, and swim with schools of digital fish!
