📍 Casa de los Balcones

Some buildings are alive with history – you can sense it the moment you step inside, and this certainly rings true for Tenerife's La Casa de los Balcones. La Orotava: Guided Tour with Gofio Tasting

📍 Tenerife Walking Tours

Take a tour called "Los Molinos de la Villa" and learn about the hydraulic heritage still present in the streets of northern Tenerife and the staple food of the Canary Islands, gofio, which has a history of five centuries. These mills used the power of water to grind roasted grain. Ten mills remain, situated along a pathway marked by an old water channel that once connected them. Along this route, you will also find old public washhouses and watering places, now regarded as ethnographic treasures. On this tour, you'll see the mills and learn about their function. Visit La Orotava, where two of these mills continue to operate. Watch their wheels in action and taste traditional gofio.

📍 Teide Volcano Visitor's Center

Get access to the "Science and Legend" exhibition at the Teide Cable Car Visitor's Center. Explore the exhibition and discover legends related to the eruption of Mount Teide around 1,000 years ago. After exploring the exhibition, head to the souvenir shop at the Visitor Center. You'll find a variety of treasures to commemorate your Teide Legend experience. Furthermore, treat your taste buds to a local culinary adventure at Spain's highest restaurant-cafe. Have delicious cuisine while taking in the scenic views of the National Park.
