Chiang Rai: Jungle Adventure - Group Trekking & Bamboo Cooking

📍 Day Trips from Chiang Rai

Behold the mesmerizing allure of Chiang Rai's lush jungle on an expertly guided tour that screams adventure and serenity. With a target audience as diverse as couples, families, and groups, this tour is designed to unveil the region's hidden treasures. The journey kicks off with a scenic hike from Pa Soert Hot Spring, meandering through tea plantations and bamboo forests, before a grand finale at the second largest waterfall in Chiang Rai. In between, expect pitstops at the agricultural heartlands of the farmland and rice fields, and cultural exchanges at Lahu, Akha Village, and China Village. And what's an expedition without a splash of fun? Pack a swim kit for a refreshing plunge into the waterfall. The climax is a unique bamboo lunch, served with handmade tools for a touch of authenticity. After the day's adventures, unwind in the comforting embrace of hot spring mineral water, before the return journey to the hotel. The entire journey spans a walking distance of approximately 10-12 kilometers, promising a fulfilling five-hour escapade. Don't miss out on this thrilling opportunity to explore the verdant expanses of the Chiang Rai jungle. Book this unforgettable experience now!
