Villa Colloredo Mels, Torre del Borgo & Beniamino Gigli Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 Civic Museum Villa Colloredo Mels

Immersed in the picturesque Marche countryside, Recanati is a stunning ‘balcony town’, offering enchanting views and a fascinating history of art and culture. See Renaissance master Lorenzo Lotto's four famous works, including the renowned *Annunciation*, and a section full of books, relics and works of art dedicated to Italian poet Giacomo Leopardi at Villa Colloredo Mels. Climb 36 meters up to the top of Torre del Borgo for breathtaking views from the Adriatic Sea to the Sibillini Mountains, and learn the story of Recanati at the MUREC inside. At the Beniamino Gigli Museum, savor the atmosphere of the opera and learn about the career of the famous tenor from Recanati.
