Combined Cambridge University Walking & Punting Tour With King’s College
In just an hour and a half, you'll walk through a thousand years of human history in Cambridge! CAMBRIDGE ·CAMBRIDGE CITY TOURS
Cambridge University Walking Tour
In just an hour and a half, your Cambridge University walking tour will guide you through a thousand years of human history: from an Anglo-Saxon church tower to a contemporary – and controversial – clock that doesn’t even tell the time properly, all surrounded by some of the greatest examples of Gothic and Neoclassical architecture in the world. See where Newton wrote his theory of gravity and hear the truth behind Newton's apple tree.
