Muir Woods and Sausalito: Small-Group Tour

📍 Muir Woods

Cross the iconic Golden Gate Bridge on your way to Muir Woods National Monument on this small-group tour. Start by admiring views of San Francisco, the Bay, and Alcatraz, with a stop for photos at the iconic Golden Gate Bridge. Next, pass Mount Tamalpais and the Marin Headlands en route to Muir Woods, where you'll have 1 hour and 15 minutes to explore the towering coastal redwoods. Stroll winding trails, admire the diverse flora and fauna, and visit the gift shop. Afterward, head to Sausalito, a scenic seaside town with Mediterranean charm. You will have 1 hour to walk along the waterfront, explore shops and cafes, and visit the colorful houseboat community. Then, you will be dropped back off in San Francisco or you may choose to stay in Sausalito and take the ferry back (at additional cost).

San Francisco City Tour + Muir Woods

📍 Muir Woods

Join this exciting full-day trip and venture north over the Golden Gate Bridge to an isolated canyon filled with ancient coastal redwood trees, also known as Muir Woods National Monument. Enjoy the cool, moist air, as you stroll through 1,000-year-old trees towering 260 feet tall. The canopy of coastal redwoods make up an important piece of ancient California history. You will never forget the crisp, fresh redwood fragrance on your journey to the majestic Muir Woods National Monument. Then, enjoy the sights of San Francisco on an audio-guided Hop on Hop off City Tour! With 16 different stops along the route you'll see all the sights at your leisure. You can even take a drive through the quaint seaside town of Sausalito – a Mediterranean-like village with boutique stores and sweeping views across the San Francisco Bay. * Entrance fee into Muir Woods is included in the tour
