Peoria Zoo: Entry Ticket

📍 Peoria Zoo

Fascination awaits at Peoria Zoo! A global array of animals are cared for inside, including Amur tigers, white rhinos, African lions, giraffes, and different species of monkeys. Your Peoria Zoo tickets will give you access to six exhibits that focus on different regions in the world, including Africa, Australia, Asia, and the tropics, plus a _Contact Barn_ and a Conservation Center_. The Africa Exhibit – _Zambezi River Village_ – recreates the African grasslands. This is where you'll find the lions, rhinos, giraffes, and zebras. You can also expect to see forest dwelling species like the Red River Hogs and Colobus monkeys. Continue on the path through the exhibit and see the bright colors of the mandrills, one of the largest species of monkey in the world.
