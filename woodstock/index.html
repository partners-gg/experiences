📍 Blenheim Palace

Sir Winston Churchill took his first breath here at Blenheim Palace in 1874, which is reason enough to add it to your 'sites from English history' checklist. In 2025, Blenheim Palace launches exclusive new experiences as part of its landmark roof restoration. Discover hidden areas and rooftop views at Britain’s Greatest Palace, all while supporting vital conservation work to protect this UNESCO World Heritage Site for future generations.
