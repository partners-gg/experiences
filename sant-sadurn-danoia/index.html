SANT SADURNÍ D'ANOIA ·CODORNÍU WINERY
Codorníu Winery: Guided Tour + Cava & Appetizers Tasting
Anna de Codorníu was the last member of her family to carry this renowned surname. In her honor, in 1983, a cava with Chardonnay was created and named after her. This visit pays homage to Anna, and will let you try three different varieties of cava named after her – all in a unique and atmospheric setting. Discover the secrets of cava production and admire the architectural work of Josep Puig i Cadafalch!
