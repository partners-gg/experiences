Milano Marittima: Romagnola Riviera Half-Day Boat Tour

📍 Ravenna Sightseeing Cruises

Sail away on a delightful half-day boat journey from Milano Marittima, tracing the sun-kissed Adriatic coast of Italy. As you glide along the shimmering sea, keep an eye out for playful dolphins in the waves. The journey offers a unique perspective of industrial platforms and the golden sandy beaches of Romagna and Marche. The adventure begins aboard the *Motonave Ghibli*, a vessel ready to take you out into the open waters of the Adriatic sea. Emilia-Romagna is renowned for its mouthwatering seafood, and the boat's bar doesn't disappoint. Sample a mouthwatering selection of local dishes, all while basking in the gentle sea breeze.
