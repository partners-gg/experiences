La Gomera: Guided Tour + Ferry Ride + Lunch + Transfer

📍 La Gomera Day Trips from Tenerife

Take a day trip to La Gomera from Tenerife. Travel by ferry with views of Mount Teide. Arrive in La Gomera and see deep valleys, ravines, and unique basalt formations with a guide. Visit the volcanic landscapes and Garajonay National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with one of the oldest laurel forests in the world. Walk through the laurel forest, full of ancient, moss-covered trees. Finish the trip with a Canarian lunch and a demonstration of Silbo Gomero, the island's traditional whistling language, considered by UNESCO as a Masterpiece of the Oral and Intangible Heritage of Humanity.

Tenerife: Island Tour with Masca

📍 La Gomera Day Trips from Tenerife

This excursion gives you the opportunity to experience the essence of Tenerife in just one day. Start the day visiting Candelaria, a coastal town that is home to the Basilica of Candelaria, one of the most important places of worship in Tenerife. On the seashore, you can admire the imposing bronze statues commemorating the ancient Guanche Kings of Tenerife. Continue to the Historic-Artistic Site of La Villa de La Orotava. Walk the cobblestone streets of its historic center to find places full of charm and culture, such as La Casa de Los Balcones, the Town Hall Square, or the Church of Nuestra Señora de La Concepción.
