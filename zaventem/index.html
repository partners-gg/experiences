ZAVENTEM ·AIRPORT TRANSFERS BRUSSELS
Train from Zaventem Airport to Brussels
Did you know that Zaventem is one of the biggest airports in the world? So, arriving at Brussels Airport can be overwhelming sometimes. Many taxis charge €50 to get to Brussels city center, while busses and private airport transfers jostle and honk in traffic. The train is the best connection between the airport and the city center. It departures every 10 minutes and the travel time is 15-20 minutes. You will arrive at Brussels Central Station where a lot of hotels are located. This is also the central stop for the departure of taxis, metros, trams and buses. A direct link railway line connects Zaventem International Airport with Brussels Central Station, and is the fastest and most convenient form of transport to the city center. Book your train tickets in advance, and thank yourself later!
