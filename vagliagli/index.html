Bindi Sergardi's Tenuta Mocenni: Guided Tour + Wine Tastings

📍 Bindi Sergardi's Tenuta Mocenni

Begin with a guided visit to the historic winery, learning about the winemaking process. Taste wine directly from the barrel during its aging phase. Siena: Wine Tasting + Bruschetta in Tuscan Vineyard

📍 Bindi Sergardi's Tenuta Mocenni

Explore a historic family estate with roots dating back to 1067. Wander through its grounds and visit the wine aging cellar, where cement tanks and wooden barrels hold precious wines. Taste a variety of grapes, including Trebbiano, Malvasia Bianca, Merlot, and Cabernet Sauvignon. Furthermore, try the Chianti Classico, featuring the region's famous Sangiovese grape. In addition, feast on traditional Tuscan bruschettas prepared with local ingredients like tomatoes, cheeses, spleen, and beans. The offerings change with the seasons for a fresh experience. The wine selection includes Bindo Toscana IGT, La Ghirlanda Chianti Classico DOCG, Calidonia Chianti Classico Riserva DOCG, Mocenni 89 Chianti Classico Gran Selezione DOCG, and Simbiosi Merlot & Cabernet Sauvignon Toscana IGT.

📍 Bindi Sergardi's Tenuta Mocenni

Get ready to be swept off your feet by stunning vistas as this Tuscan wine-tasting adventure unfolds before your eyes. This VIP ticket offers a tasting of four great wines at Tenuta Mocenni, a historic estate owned by the Bindi Sergardi family, an esteemed name in the world of Italian winemaking. The estate produces a range of exquisite wines, including Chianti Classico, Chianti Classico Riserva, and Super Tuscan blends, among others. Discover your favourite, sample homemade oilive oil and Tuscan bread, and try some local specialties like cheese, cold cuts of meat, and honey, Each wine reflects the unique characteristics of the land and the meticulous craftsmanship of the Bindi Sergardi family. This isn't just a wine tasting, it's an immersive sensory experience that will stay with you long after your last sip.
