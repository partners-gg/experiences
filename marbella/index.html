Marbella: Guided Tapas Tour

📍 Marbella Food Tours

Taste traditional Andalusian flavors on a guided tapas tour. Walk through Marbella's Old Town, where white walls and flower-filled plazas set the scene for a food tour. Visit three authentic taverns, selected for their local recipes. Try dishes like prawns in garlicky olive oil and chili, Spanish tortilla, grilled padron peppers, and jamón ibérico. Pair each dish with local wines or refreshing drinks. Listen to knowledgeable guides share stories about the culture and history of each dish. Taste the local cuisine and learn about Marbella's culinary heritage. This tour is suitable for all ages and provides a relaxed way to try the city's food.

Marbella: Guided Food & Market Tour

📍 Marbella Food Tours

Visit Marbella's market and try authentic local foods. Taste a "bocata" recommended by Michelin-starred chef Dani García. Choose the freshest fish from Paco, a skilled fishmonger. Enjoy Malaga’s unique produce that you can’t find in supermarkets.

Marbella Sun Bikes Tour

📍 Marbella City Card

The folks at Sun Bikes were so passionate about sharing their love of cycling, they started a business! So you're in safe hands as you ride the streets of Marbella – not only is there local knowledge, but you know you're on a quality saddle. Join one of the teams and see the best the city has to offer – from the Ferraris parked by the marina, to the classic architecture of the Chapel of Saint Juan de Dios dating back to the 16th century.
