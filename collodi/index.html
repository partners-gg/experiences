Pinocchio Park, Storico Giardino Garzoni & Casa delle Farfalle: Entry Ticket

📍 Pinocchio Park

Enter the Pinocchio Park, designed as a series of fun stages with sculptures, buildings, and green areas. Both adults and kids can revisit the Adventures of Pinocchio through works by 20th-century artists. The Garzoni Garden features many symbols and statues, some prominently displayed and others more discreet, offering an interesting story for each visitor. Visit the Butterfly House of Collodi, home to hundreds of butterflies from tropical or equatorial regions. These butterflies fly freely in a tropical garden filled with plants, flowers, and fruits, thanks to a well-made environment matching their native habitat.

📍 Storico Giardino Garzoni + Casa delle Farfalle

Find a wealth of statues and plants in the lush greenery of the Garzoni Garden. It houses hundreds of vibrant butterflies that come from tropical regions and live freely among the tropical vegetation present in the garden.
