Uxmal Archeological Site: Skip The Line Ticket + Guided Tour

📍 Uxmal Ruins

Visit the Uxmal ruins on the Yucatán Peninsula to see ancient Maya architecture. Explore the Pyramid of the Magician, known for its elliptical sides. Explore the Nunnery Quadrangle with its detailed carvings, and see the Governor's Palace for insights into Maya governance and society. Walk through the city’s plazas and ball courts to understand the layout and planning of the Maya civilization. Take a guided tour to learn more about the historical significance of each site.
