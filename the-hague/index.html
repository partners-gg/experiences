THE HAGUE ·MUSEUM PANORAMA MESDAG
Museum Panorama Mesdag: Entry Ticket
Mesdag's stunning, giant seascape is almost like an old-school optical illusion, or an IMAX initiative created before its time! It lures you in and makes you feel like you're really there. The Panorama Mesdag is the impressive result of the Belgian Panorama craze, which erupted around 1875. The inviting vista of the sea, dunes and old fishing village of ScheveningeIn was created in 1881 after some Belgian entrepreneurs asked Hendrik Willem Mesdag to paint a 'Hague Maritime Panorama.' Stand on a viewpoint above the dune, and witness the panoramic spectacular for yourself.
