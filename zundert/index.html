Vincent van Gogh House: Entry Ticket + Audio Guide

📍 Vincent van Gogh House

His work has touched the soul of many and inspired countless other artists. Get tickets for the Vincent van Gogh House and see ever-changing presentations and exhibitions based on his life and work. Did you know that Van Gogh reached the ripe old age of 27 before he painted his first piece? He painted _Starry Night_ from within the white walls of a French asylum, and he only sold one painting in his lifetime. Come and find out about the iconoclastic artistic revolutionary who died before fame found him. Hear a radio play that tells the story of his special relationship with his brother, Theo, and the concerns his parents had about his lifestyle. Watch a fascinating presentation on his life and see changing exhibitions based on the 20th and 21st-century artists who were inspired by Van Gogh's work. The Vincent van Gogh House is a living art center and an essential experience for art lovers!
