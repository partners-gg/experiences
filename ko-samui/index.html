Ko Tao & Ko Nang Yuan: Guided Speedboat Tour from Koh Samui + Meal

📍 Day Trips from Koh Samui

Start your island-hopping adventure from Koh Samui to Ko Tao and Ko Nang Yuan. Begin with hotel pickup and check-in at the pier, where you'll enjoy a light breakfast before boarding the speedboat. Depart from the private pier to explore vibrant marine life and coral reefs and possibly swim with sea turtles. Stop at Hin Wong Bay or Lighthouse Bay, where you can snorkel among natural corals. Afterward, take a tour around the island to see the coastal views. Enjoy a Thai buffet lunch and fresh fruit at Ko Tao Montra. Reach Ko Nang Yuan to hike to a viewpoint for views of the sandbar connecting three islets or relax on the beach. As the day ends, return to Koh Samui, hand in your snorkeling gear, and enjoy a transfer back to your hotel.

Tan & Pig Islands: Guided Speedboat Tour from Koh Samui + Pink Dolphin Spotting

📍 Day Trips from Koh Samui

Take a speedboat day trip to see nature and wildlife off Koh Samui's coast. Spot unique pink dolphins, snorkel at Tan Island, meet the boars of Pig Island, and have lunch. See the rare pink dolphins near Koh Samui. These animals swim close to the Thai mainland, offering a unique sight for all ages. After that, snorkel at Tan Island among coral reefs with clams, tropical fish, and marine life. Then, visit Pig Island, where wild boars wander freely. Have lunch on Mainland Beach. The trip includes hotel pickup and drop-off and a local guide for a smooth day on the ocean.

Ang Thong Marine Park: Guided Kayaking & Snorkeling Tour from Koh Samui + Meal

📍 Day Trips from Koh Samui

Take a speedboat tour of Angthong Marine Park from Koh Samui. Get picked up from your hotel and have a light breakfast at the pier. Then, board the speedboat and head to "Koh Wow," where you can snorkel in clear waters and see coral reefs and marine life. Tour around the 42 islands of the marine park and stop at the "Emerald Lake" (Talay Nai), which is surrounded by greenery. Enjoy a Thai buffet lunch with fresh fruits on another island. Visit Hua Ta Lap Island, where you can hike to a viewpoint, kayak, swim, or relax on the beach. Conclude the tour by returning to Koh Samui, dropping off your snorkeling gear, and receiving a transfer back to your hotel.
