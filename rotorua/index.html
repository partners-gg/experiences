Kaituna River: Grade 5 White Water Rafting

📍 Kaituna River Rafting

Join a 2-hour white water rafting trip from Rotorua, New Zealand. Receive a safety briefing with procedures and tips from instructors, then step onto the raft. Drift along the Kaituna River's deep canyon with native New Zealand plants. The river begins with mild rapids and gradually gets more challenging. Navigate through warm water and fast rapids with three waterfalls. Conquer the world's highest commercially rafted waterfall, the 23-foot (7-meter) Tutea Falls. Then, continue downstream for more rafting action. After the adventure, take a hot shower and view your photos on a big screen.

Rotorua: Guided Zipline Adventure

📍 Rotorua Ziplines

Undergo one of the country’s most awarded tourism experiences and engage in an aerial zipline tour in Rotorua's native forest that spans around two and a half hours. You'll move between tall tree platforms, which are connected by ziplines and swing bridges, within a protected ancient forest. The environment is home to a rich variety of birds due to conservation efforts. Professional guides are present to ensure safety, offer guidance for beginners, share interesting information about the area, and capture photographs during the activity. Visitors actively contribute to the ongoing preservation and restoration of the forest through participation in this program, which is recognized as a leading eco-tourism experience.
