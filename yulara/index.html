YULARA ·FIELD OF LIGHT ULURU
Field of Light Uluru: General Admission + Transfer
Visit the _Field of Light_ exhibit at Uluru. Created by artist Bruce Munro, this display features 50,000 spindles of light spread over an area the size of seven football fields. The lights cover the desert in shades of ochre, deep violet, blue, and white. Due to popular demand, the exhibit, which has been named Tili Wiru Tjuta Nyakutjaku in the local Pitjantjatjara language, will remain open indefinitely. This is Munro's most significant work to date and provides an expansive visual experience in the desert.

YULARA ·WINTJIRI WIRU
Wintjiri Wiru: Twilight Experience + Transfer
Step into twilight and see the magic of Wintjiri Wiru. Set on a desert dune with views of Uluru and Kata Tjuta, this event combines cultural storytelling with advanced technology. Drink Australian wines and eat cheeses and desserts as twilight changes the landscape. As night falls, sit in a sustainably built theatre and hear the ancient Mala story. Watch choreographed drones, lasers, and projections bring the story to life against the desert sky. Traditional Inma music and narration in Pitjantjatjara will connect you to the world's oldest living culture. This twilight experience blends natural surroundings and cultural heritage.

YULARA ·FIELD OF LIGHT ULURU
Field of Light Uluru: Sunrise Tour with Hot Drinks
Visit the *Field of Light*, the largest installation by artist Bruce Munro, located in the desert near Uluru. This display includes over 50,000 glowing glass spheres. Arrive before dawn and walk through the illuminated field at your own pace. As daylight approaches, see the desert oaks come into view. Move to a nearby sand dune to watch the sunrise. Have tea, coffee, or hot chocolate as the sun rises, lighting up the desert and Uluru. From this point, you can also see Kata Tjuta as the desert wakes up for the day.
