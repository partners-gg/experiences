📍 Estrella Damm Factory

There's nothing better than a nice cold beer in the place where it's brewed! Find out what goes into Melanie and August Kuentzmann Damm's beer at El Prat Brewery with a guided tour of the 140-year-old venue, including a tasting session. Learn about the beer manufacturing process and exactly what goes into each bottle of Estrella Damm.
