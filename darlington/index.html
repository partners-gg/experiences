Boeing 737-800 Flight Simulator Experience

📍 SimFly

Fasten your seatbelts and get ready for take-off! This flight simulator experience puts you in full control of flying and landing a Boeing 737 aircraft. It's the perfect surprise gift for anyone who's ever wanted to experience a day in the life of a pilot. After a pre-flight briefing from your instructor, head into the simulator room which hosts 18 original Boeing 737 passenger seats, authentic internal features such as the curved sidewalls and overhead lockers, and of course, the fully functioning Boeing 737-800 flight simulator! Armed with your new knowledge, taxi out, prepare for departure and take off, soaring high into the skies above. Feeling daring? Choose a storm, or cruise peacefully into a sunset. You'll fly the aircraft single-handedly, although your instructors will be there in case you need some help. Configure the aircraft to land at your chosen airport, and get ready to celebrate. You just flew your own plane!
