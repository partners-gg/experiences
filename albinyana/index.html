Aqualeon Water Park: Entry Ticket

📍 Aqualeon Water Park

Is there any better way to escape the Spanish sun than by combining a refreshing spray of water and the wind on your face? Aqualeon brings you both, with a healthy dose of adrenaline to boot. Aqualeon is located right beside a national park, so look out over tree tops before you slide down! Get your blood pumping on Kamikaze before jumping in a ring with a friend and taking on the Rapid River. Younger kids can splash around at Octopus Island and get a sense of adventure on the Super Slalom.
