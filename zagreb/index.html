Ljubljana & Lake Bled: Day Trip from Zagreb

📍 Day Tours from Zagreb

Head out in the morning from Zagreb to the charming capital of Ljubljana, Slovenia. Join a guided tour of the city's heart and see Jože Plečnik, gaze at the stunning Baroque and Art Nouveau edifices, and soak in the local atmosphere at popular food markets. Discover the intriguing tales of Napoleon's popularity, the love imbued in Slovenia's name, and the origins of the iconic Triple Bridge's moniker. Enjoy some leisure time to unearth the city's hidden gems at your own pace. Journey onwards to the Alpine resort of Bled. Take a leisurely drive around the lake, before setting sail on a traditional Pletna boat to Slovenia's one and only island. Ring the fabled fortune bell, bask in the warm sun and fresh mountain air, then ascend to the ancient castle perched high on a cliff overlooking the lake.

Ljubljana, Postojna Cave & Predjama Castle: Tour from Zagreb

📍 Day Tours from Zagreb

Depart from Zagreb, the capital city of Croatia and head to Slovenia's lovely Ljubljana, a city not only celebrated for its rich cultural heritage but also as an exceptional starting point for adventurous explorations across Slovenia’s natural landscapes. The old city center captivates with its distinctive architectural charm, an exquisite fusion of Baroque and Art Nouveau styles, and the 20th-century masterpieces of architect Joze Pleznik. The adventure doesn't end in Ljubljana. Venturing to the Karst region reveals the subterranean Postojna Cave, the world's most popular karst cave.

Zagreb: Culinary Tour with Food and Wine Tasting

📍 Zagreb City Tours

Taste your way through Croatian cuisine, where every region boasts its unique traditions and flavors that date back from antiquity to the present day. The contrast between the mainland and coast's food and preparation methods is striking, and this food tour offers a chance to savor it all in one delightful bite! The adventure kicks off at the Ban Jelačić Statue in the city's bustling main square. Here, an expert guide stands ready to lead the way, offering fascinating insights into Croatian tradition and the intriguing tale of Zagreb, all while hopping from one eatery to the next. You'll have the chance to taste up to six traditional Croatian dishes and sip on four exquisite wines.
