📍 Parco Giardino Sigurtà

Listen to the bees buzz and the birds sing within this slice of paradise in Verona – and winner of the World Tulip Award 2019. It's no surprise that Impressionist masters found inspiration in the Water Gardens, but see if you can find your way out of the park's maze!
