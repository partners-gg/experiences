Plaza Premium Lounge at Vancouver International Airport

📍 Plaza Premium Lounge

Passengers have access to first-class lounge facilities at Vancouver International Airport's Plaza Premium lounges, regardless of airline or ticket class. The lounges include luxurious, comfortable furnishings, showers, and nap rooms. Healthy eating is promoted through fresh and hearty ingredients and guests can enjoy signature hot food dishes, including Hong Kong-style fish ball noodle soup, and specially prepared delicious drinks and alcoholic beverages. Kick up your travel-weary feet with a first-class relaxation experience in the Plaza Premium Lounge. This peaceful oasis offers a convenient escape from the hustle and bustle of the airport. Plus, get access to amenities that run the gamut from useful to luxurious, such as WiFi, showers, and food and beverages.
