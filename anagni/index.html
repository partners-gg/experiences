ANAGNI ·MUSEUM OF THE CATHEDRAL OF ANAGNI
Cathedral Museum of Anagni (MuCA): Entry Ticket
With these tickets to the Cathedral Museum of Anagni, you'll travel back in time to relive - through a kaleidoscope of colors and images - the charm of the Middle Ages. The museum takes you on a 12-stop tour to visit various areas, rooms and chapels, all adorned with fascinating objects and tapestries. You'll also stop at the Crypt of St. Magnus, an incredible frescoed crypt that's almost a thousand years old. The museum preserves reliquary icons, sacred objects, mosaics, ancient tombs and sarcophagi, colorful textiles, images of saints and martyrs, frescoes with biblical stories, hagiographic cycles, and scientific and pagan descriptions of the creation of the cosmos. You'll need an audio guide to take advantage of all that – luckily it's free to download!

ANAGNI ·BONIFACE VIII PALACE
Bonifacio VIII Palace in Anagni: Entry Ticket
Step into the heart of medieval history with a ticket to the grand Palazzo di Bonifacio VIII. This ancient structure, nestled in the town center, dates back to the 13th century. It once belonged to the illustrious Caetani family, notably the home of Pope Bonifacio VIII himself. Within the palace walls, you'll discover a treasure trove of art and culture. Expansive frescoed halls, priceless paintings, exquisite artwork, and fine furniture await your exploration. Each room offers a glimpse into the opulence of medieval life and the intrigue of the period's politics. The palace's star attraction? The famous Sala dello Schiaffo or "Slap Room". Here unfolded a shocking event that sent ripples through medieval European history. As commanded by the leader of French Soldiers, Nogaret, Giacomo Sciarra Colonna struck the Pope. This audacious act set the stage for the Avignon period. Your journey into the past begins here, at the Palazzo di Bonifacio VIII.
