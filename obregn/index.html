📍 Cabárceno Nature Park

With environments that recreate the natural habitats of the animals inside, the Cabárceno Nature Park located near Santander is the perfect day out for the family to discover the beauty of nature and its creatures. Inside the park you can see more than 100 animal species in semi-freedom. The animals are free to roam and their natural instincts are uninhibited. If you want a taste of the wild, you'll find it in Cabárceno Nature Park. With over 20 km of roads that cross the park, you'll journey through different enclosures through wonderful gorges, peaceful lakes and suggestive rocky figures. The Parque de la Naturaleza de Cabárceno is designed for educational, cultural, scientific and recreational purposes, so you might learn a thing or two, too. In addition, throughout the park there are numerous parking areas and trails that allow you to appreciate nature's beauty. Discover the secrets of the animal, vegetable, and mineral world with numerous activities for the whole family.
