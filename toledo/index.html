Puy du Fou España: Park Entrance + El Sueño de Toledo Night Show

📍 Puy du Fou España

Puy du Fou España is a one-of-a-kind immersive park that throws you so far into its historical recreations you'll forget which century you're actually standing in! Experience life in a rural village in the Middle Ages, see craftsmen at work, sample *ye olde world* food and honey-scented tents, and even embark on an epic journey to the other side of the world with the Admiral Christopher Columbus. As well as park entrance, this ticket gets you a hot seat for the evening performance of "El Sueño de Toledo" night show. Forget poring over textbooks, discover Spanish history the fun way with knights, lights, dancing horses and drama around every corner!

Toledo Tourist Bracelet

📍 Toledo City Cards

Take the hassle out of your trip to Toledo, Spain, and streamline your visit with a Toledo Tourist Bracelet. With one wristband, you can visit seven of the most important and emblematic monuments of Toledo, with the option to revisit your favorites up to three times. Watch the culmination of Christianity, Islam, and Judaism unfold as you trace the religious history of Toledo at each monument: Real Colegio de Doncellas Nobles, Church of the Jesuits, San Juan de los Reyes, Cristo de la Luz, Santo Tomé, Church of El Salvador, and Santa María la Blanca. If you love art, don't miss your chance to see the El Greco's _The Burial of the Count of Orgaz_ in the Church of Santo Tomé! It's included with your Toledo Tourist Bracelet.

Puy du Fou España: El Sueño de Toledo Night Show

📍 Puy du Fou España

Forget pouring over textbooks, discover Spanish history the 'hip' way with knights, lights, dancing horses and drama around every corner. This is "El Sueño de Toledo" night show at Madrid and Toledo's top attraction, Puy du Fou España. From the kingdom of Recaredo, to the battle of Navas de Tolosa. From the discovery of America to the arrival of the railway, the dazzling depiction of Spain's most legendary men, women and moments will transport you through 1,500 years in just one night. The stage alone is something to remember. At a whopping 5-hectares in size, there's more than one thing to watch at any time. Better keep the drinks flowing! Note: Tickets for admission to Puy du Fou España, including all other live daytime shows, can be bought separately on Tiqets.

Toledo Zoo & Aquarium: Entry Ticket

📍 Toledo Zoo

Step into the wild at Toledo Zoo, a place for unique animal encounters and experiences. Recognized throughout the world for its conservation and breeding successes, the Toledo Zoo is leading the way to a better planet. Watch elephants, hippos, tigers, bears, gorillas, crocodiles, snakes, and an amazing array of rare birds. The Toledo Zoo cares for over 16,000 animals representing 680+ species! “Reach for the Starfish” in the aquarium’s interactive touch tank then “dive” into the depths of a 178,000 gallon aquarium where you will discover many of the ocean’s most elusive creatures.

Toledo Zoo: Lights Before Christmas Entry Ticket

📍 Toledo Zoo

Enjoy the magic of the holiday season at the 40th Annual “Lights Before Christmas” at the Toledo Zoo. Voted every year as one of Ohio’s best light displays, the Toledo Zoo showcases more than one million twinkle lights with daily visits by Santa and Mrs. Claus. Have a cup of steaming hot cocoa and a Christmas cookie. Take a ride down the ice slide into the Winter Village. Warm up by the fire, savor a s’more, and sing along with the strolling carolers. Don’t forget to stop by the formal gardens to see the 35’ spruce tree. It’s decorated with thousands of twinkle lights that dance to everyone’s favorite Christmas music.

Iluziona Museum

📍 Iluziona

Explore scenes inspired by Toledo’s myths and legends, and take fun 3D photos that play with perspective and space. The museum is great for families, friends, and anyone who enjoys photography. Every room offers something new to try and capture.
