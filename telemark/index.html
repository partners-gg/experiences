📍 Bø Sommerland

Get ready to make a splash at Bø Sommarland, Scandinavia’s biggest waterpark nestled in the stunning Midt-Telemark region of Norway. Towering pine trees, lush grass, and vibrant flowers surround you as you enjoy a day of fun and excitement in the park. Since opening in 1985, Bø Sommarland has been a go-to destination for families in the warm summer months. From unique attractions to Scandinavia’s largest splashpad for the little ones, there's something for everyone. The park's friendly staff adds to the welcoming atmosphere, making your visit even more enjoyable. Don’t miss out on this seasonal gem – traditionally open the first weekend of June and closing around mid-August.
