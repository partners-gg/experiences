Hartley's Crocodile Adventures: Roundtrip from Cairns

📍 Hartley's Crocodile Adventures

Saltwater crocodiles have anger management issues. Snap up a ticket for Hartley's Crocodile Adventures and see for yourself. Home to the biggest, meanest crocs in Queensland, this reptile retreat is one of Australia's most iconic wildlife attractions for a reason. After getting transported from Cairns to the park, you'll be set loose inside, where you can see jaw-inspiring feeding shows, take a cruise through croc-infested wetland waters, see koalas up close, and lots more.

Hartley's Crocodile Adventures: Entry Ticket

📍 Hartley's Crocodile Adventures

Take a bite out of life at Hartley's Crocodile Adventures! Let the experienced and knowledgeable staff show you how fascinating these scaly creatures can be behind their notoriously toothy grins. With your Hartley's Crocodile Adventures tickets, climb aboard a safari-style cruise boat to tour the park. Bring your binoculars to do some bird-watching, or scan the surface for signs of what's lurking beneath – like the telltale "crocodile roll". Get great views of the crocs from the deck overlooking the lagoon and enjoy the chance to see them snacking as boat cruises entice them to feed. From there, visit the walk-through aviary full of exotic birds, snap a photo of the photogenic koalas and wombats, and get a glimpse of the slithery snakes and ravishing reptiles.

Hartley's Crocodile Adventures: Hartley's Koala Breakfast

📍 Hartley's Crocodile Adventures

Start your day with a hot Aussie breakfast at Hartley’s Crocodile Adventures. Enjoy scrambled eggs, bacon, hash browns, pork chipolata sausages, a croissant, toast with jam, cereal, fruit juice, fresh fruit salad, and brewed tea or coffee. Vegetarian options are available with advance notice. Between 9:00 and 9:30, a wildlife keeper will bring a koala to the dining deck. You can pat the koala and learn more from the keeper. After breakfast, attend the scheduled wildlife presentations and take a boat cruise on Hartley's Lagoon.
