Augusta: Whale Watching Tour

📍 Augusta Whale Watching

Board the 'Alison Maree,' a 23-meter catamaran, to see humpback and southern right whales, and potentially blue and minke whales. Enjoy a comfortable ride with modern amenities, including fresh bathrooms and a Captain's Lounge. Listen to the expert commentary by the marine science-trained crew and you can also spot New Zealand fur seals, dolphins, and sea birds while enjoying complimentary refreshments.
