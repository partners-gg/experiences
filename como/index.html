COMO ·VILLA DEL BALBIANELLO
Villa del Balbianello Park
Originally commissioned in the late eighteenth century by Cardinal Durini, a prominent writer and patron, the Villa del Balbianello Park was intended to be a haven of literary leisure and delight. Fast forward to modern times, and the property was acquired by Guido Monzino, an entrepreneur, collector, and passionate adventurer. In 1974, Monzino transformed the villa into a personal retreat where he could meticulously preserve the memories of his exciting life, including his historic achievement of becoming the first Italian to summit Mount Everest.

COMO ·DAY TRIPS FROM COMO LAKE
St. Moritz & Tirano: Bernina Express Panoramic Tour from Lake Como
Hop on a morning bus from Como’s city center and enjoy the views of Lake Como as you head to St. Moritz. Take time to stroll along the lakeside, shop, or try treats from a local patisserie. In the afternoon, head to the train station and board a panoramic carriage on the Bernina Express to Tirano. Experience a scenic journey on this train, passing through over 50 tunnels and 196 bridges. See the Morteratsch Glacier, Montebello Bend, Lago Bianco, and Laj Neir before reaching Alp Grüm. After arriving in Tirano, take a return bus to Como for a late evening arrival.
