Akumal Monkey Sanctuary & Rescued Animals: Guided Tour

📍 Akumal Monkey Sanctuary & Rescued Animals

Due to pollution, deforestation, or other factors, the animals at Akumal Sanctuary had to surrender their natural habitat. Because of the devastation of their homes, most are unable to return to the wild and have been rehabilitated in this sanctuary. Join the trainers and staff who are part of the animals' journey, and participate in feedings and friendly wildlife interactions.
