Templer Park: Half-Day Tour from Kuala Lumpur

📍 Templer Park

Just a short drive from Kuala Lumpur lies a tropical oasis. Escape the bustle of the city and rest your bones by a sparkling waterfall, meander lush jungle trails, and spot monkeys dangling from the treetops. Travel from your hotel by air-conditioned vehicle, with an English-speaking driver guide KL is one of the world's fastest developing cities, so naturally, it's really busy! On this half-day tour to Templer Park, let your soul breathe in the fresh jungle air as you stop for picnics in scenic clearings or take a dip in refreshing waterfalls. If you're up for it, hike the million-year-old limestone hill, Bukit Takun. After your adventure in the mossy greens of the Malaysian rainforest, you'll be returned refreshed to your hotel.
