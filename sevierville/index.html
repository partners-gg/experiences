The Wild Stallion Mountain Coaster at Skyland Ranch

📍 Skyland Ranch

Get ready for an unforgettable ride, with a visit to the Wild Stallion Mountain Coaster! This is the longest mountain coaster in the U.S. Southeast, and provides a thrilling, eight-minute ride that is sure to be one of the highlights of your visits. You can ride the Wild Stallion Mountain Coaster during the day for scenic views of Sevierville and the Smoky Mountains, or ride it at night to see the sparkling city lights.

Skyland Ranch: Entry Ticket + Scenic Skyride Chairlift

📍 Skyland Ranch

Nestled in the foothills of the Smokies sits a ranch with awesome mountain views. Hitch a chairlift ride up the hillside and sit down to some tasty BBQ - ain't nothing like the taste of meat fresh off a grill! You will meet a horse named Maverick, an adorable mini highlander bull called Diesel, and other miniature animals you won't see anywhere else. There's also a talented lineup of entertainment each day, and 6,000 square feet of shopping opportunities! Shop for everything from toys and Christmas decor, to western wear. After all, if you're going to a ranch, you have to dress like a cowboy. Play games, try your hand at roping a steer, and challenge your friends in the SkyLand Shootout BB Gun shooting range. Come hungry because you're about to discover some of the best food anywhere in the Smokies. From the SkyLand Cafe & Bakery to the three delicious-smelling food trucks, there's something for even the pickiest eaters to devour.
