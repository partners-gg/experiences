Swiss Vapeur Parc: Entry Ticket

📍 Swiss Vapeur Parc

Hop aboard a lovely 1/4 scale train replica at Swiss Vapeur Parc for a scenic 2 km tour through a green landscape. Located by Lake Geneva, this park showcases idyllic Switzerland across a vast 19,000 sqm area. Whether you ride the train or stroll, the park offers an unforgettable adventure for all ages. Witness a dozen authentic steam locomotives, meticulously modeled after German, Swiss, and American designs, running on coal and water. Explore over thirty intricately designed buildings, including the towering Kuklos of Leysin and the beautiful Château d’Aigle, each crafted to perfection. However, Swiss Vapeur Parc isn't just about trains and architecture. With 130 species of plants and trees, it's a blooming paradise waiting to be discovered. The creators have poured their hearts into designing an extraordinary setting that guarantees an unparalleled experience highlighting both trains and nature.
