Yamnuska Wolfdog Sanctuary: Entry Ticket + Guided Tour

📍 Yamnuska Wolfdog Sanctuary

Get up close and personal with some of Canada's most intriguing animals with tickets for Yamnuska Wolfdog Sanctuary. Whether you choose the self-guided look around or opt to add an introductory tour to your ticket, you'll rarely get as close to packs of wolfdogs as you can at Yamnuska Wolfdog Sanctuary. The introductory tour allows you access inside one of the wolfdog enclosures for a 30-minute presentation on these fascinating animals. It's the perfect opportunity to ask any burning lupine questions, learn about wolf conservation, and take cool photos!
