Mallorca Island Tour: Bus, Train, Tram and Boat
Discover the Northwest Coast of Mallorca, its mountains, cliffs, and other coastal treasures on this panoramic Mallorca Island Tour: Bus, Train, Tram and Boat. Your guides will combine bus, train, tram and boat transportation to bring you the best of the island, both on land and at sea. Head to Palma and ride the scenic train to Sóller, where the vintage train – created in 1912 with the railway line – will rattle you through the Valley of Sóller in a wooden wagon. See olive groves and orange trees, pass the natural barriers of the Alfábia Mountains, chug through 13 tunnels, and cross several bridges and a famous viaduct. Reaching the city of Palma is a real old-school adventure. Depending on the schedule, you'll have free time for lunch in Sóller or La Calobra, where a local tram will bring you to the picturesque coastal village of Port de Sóller for more sightseeing. You'll fall in love with Mallorca on this magical day out!
