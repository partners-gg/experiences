Heide Park Resort: Entry Ticket

📍 Heide Park Resort

Heide Park Resort is fun for the whole family! With more than 30 attractions especially for children under ten years old, Heide Park is the perfect family escape. Get wild with tickets to northern **Germany's largest amusement park**, home to extraordinary adventures for families and thrill-seekers alike. Adrenaline junkies can get their kicks on Germany's only wing coaster, Flight of Demons, or fight the battle of the giants on Europe's highest and fastest wooden roller coaster, Colossos. The newest ride, Toxic Garden, is a Transylvania-themed thrill ride with five turns to shake your world around! For the little ones, check out **Peppa Pig Land**, the themed world based on the hit TV show and book series for young children, or immerse yourself in Dreamworks' whimsical world of _How to Train Your Dragon_.
