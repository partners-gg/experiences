BiBo Park: Skip The Line Ticket

📍 BiBo Park

Multiple studies have shown that engaging with plants has a positive impact on humans, both from a psychological and emotional perspective. Visit this new multifunctional space in Ibiza, a place to observe, learn, relax, and enjoy the nature of Ibiza and Formentera through Biotechnology. Take a guided tour and learn how science and technology are helping us achieve our goals to ensure a better future for the environment. Visit the world’s first “piano” to be made from living plants. You can literally watch plants activate electronic systems with a human touch and produce a show of lights and music! A symbiosis between nature and technology – is this the way to a greener future?
