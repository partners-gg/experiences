G2G Animal Garden: Entry Ticket

📍 G2G Animal Garden

Located about 30 km from KL City Center, the Goat 2 Go petting zoo is part of the exhibition park and showgrounds of Malaysia Agro Exposition Park Serdang (MAEPS), a center for agriculture-based events and activities. While the petting zoo may be relatively small, there are plenty of opportunities to get up close with the different species of animals here. Meet creatures including parrots, peacocks, chickens, cats, rabbits, ostriches, deer, Bengal cats, raccoons, goats, and more – it's even possible for kids to enjoy a wonderful pony ride in the petting zoo's Pony Joyride experience!
