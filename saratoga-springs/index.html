📍 Saratoga Automobile Museum

The Saratoga Automobile Museum's mission is to preserve, interpret and exhibit automobiles and automotive artifacts. Come and celebrate the automobile and educate yourself on the role of the automobile in New York State and the wider world. In addition to technical and design aspects, you can learn about the history of the automobile as well as the future social and economic impacts of this speedy mode of transport. If you're visiting later in the year, the museum also organizes the Saratoga Motorcar Auction, a nationally acclaimed vintage and collector car auction held each September. The Auction is the Saratoga Automobile Museum's largest annual fundraising effort.
