Dubrovnik: Elaphite Island Hopping + Optional Lunch
Join a trip to the Elaphite Islands to explore their beaches, villages, and scenic spots. Your guide will share interesting facts about each island along the way. The first stop is Koločep Island, where you’ll have 1 hour and 15 minutes of free time. You can join a guided hiking tour or visit a local honey farm to taste local products (at your own expense). Next, you’ll visit Šipan Island and spend 1 hour and 15 minutes in the village of Suđurađ. Here, you can see the historic Skočibuha family mansion or take part in a wine tasting (at your own expense). The final stop is Lopud Island, where you’ll have 3 hours to explore at your own pace. Departure times may vary depending on daylight. You can hike to Fort Sutvrač, take a golf cart to Šunj Beach (at your own expense), or enjoy lunch at selected restaurants (at your own expense).
