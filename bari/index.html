Palazzo Simi: Guided Tour

📍 Palazzo Simi

Once home to the wealthy Simi de Burgis family, this palace has strong influences from Renaissance architecture. The site itself can be traced all the way back to the Roman Empire, and at one point it was even a Byzantine church. The rich history of the site has left numerous archaeological treasures for you to discover. After you've explored above ground – and admired the white stone staircase – you can see relics, and the building's foundations as they would have been centuries ago.

Basilica di San Nicola & Crypta: Guided Tour

📍 Basilica di San Nicola

The Basilica di San Nicola is the most popular landmark in Bari, Puglia. This atmospheric Romanesque basilica dominates the Piazza San Nicola, with its square features and pearly white walls. On this guided tour, you'll explore the 12th-century basilica and its crypt with a learned host, who'll detail the history and architectural secrets of the site. You can also see the final resting place of jolly old St. Nick, whose remains are housed in a special altar surrounded by ornate columns.

📍 Day Trips from Bari

Prepare for a guided day tour of Ostuni, Polignano a Mare, and Cisternino from Bari. Visit Ostuni, known as the "White Town," where you'll see the town spread over a valley of ancient olive trees. Alberobello, Monopoli & Polignano Day Trip from Bari

📍 Day Trips from Bari

Prepare yourself for an exciting journey from the grand Teatro Petruzelli in Bari. Your adventure kicks off with a visit to Monopoli. Visit the town’s historic center and check out its narrow lanes and white-washed abodes. Then, go to the Cathedral of Santa Maria della Madia and the seafront at Porto Vecchio. From there, head to Alberello to see its unique trulli houses. Explore the UNESCO-protected Rione Monti and Aia Piccola neighborhoods. Remember to visit the Trullo Church of St. Anthony. After that, try traditional Puglian dishes at a local restaurant. The next destination is Polignano, famous for its dramatic cliffs and clear waters. Visit Lama Monachile Beach and enjoy the views from the Roman bridge. Finally, visit the historic center for its narrow streets and sea vistas.
