📍 Château de Bourdeilles

Located on a rocky hill, Bourdeilles Castle features two notable buildings that will interest any history enthusiast. The 13th-century medieval fortress, with its impressive donjon, stands out prominently. Additionally, the Renaissance residence inside houses one of Aquitaine's richest collections of furniture. It's a place full of history ready to be explored. Visit and see it for yourself!
