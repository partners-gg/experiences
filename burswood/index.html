Wave Rock: Guided Tour from Perth

📍 Wave Rock

Explore Perth Hills and the escarpment on a scenic drive to the farming region. Enjoy morning tea in York, the first town founded in the Avon Valley. Travel along the Wheatbelt Highway, passing through Corrigin and Kondinin. You’ll also visit the Dog Cemetery, honoring the role of dogs in farming life. See the Hippo’s Yawn rock formation and have lunch at the Wildflower Shop. Optionally, visit a wildlife park, view the antique lace collection, or explore the Toy Soldier Museum. Continue to Wave Rock and Aboriginal sites, and end the tour with a stop at Mulka’s Cave before returning to Perth.

📍 Pinnacle Desert

Visit Caversham Wildlife Park in Swan Valley to see and feed koalas, wombats, and kangaroos. Drive through Lancelin and enjoy a 4-WD adventure over the sand dunes, plus optional sandboarding. Pinnacles Desert, Yanchep & Swan Valley: Day Tour from Perth + Lunch

📍 Pinnacle Desert

Start your tour with a visit to the peaceful Yanchep National Park. Stretch your legs and see koalas and kangaroos. Continue to the Pinnacle Desert in Nambung National Park, take a stroll through the unique landscape, and see amazing limestone formations. If there's time, visit the Pinnacles Desert Discovery Centre to learn about the area's cultural and natural heritage. Next, visit the lobster town of Cervantes and get a behind-the-scenes tour of Western Australia Rock Lobster processing. Enjoy an included fish and chips lunch, or upgrade to a half-grilled lobster for an extra cost. Head back towards Perth with a stop in Swan Valley to sample chocolate, nougat, and gourmet nuts.
