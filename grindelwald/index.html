Jungfraujoch: Roundtrip Train from Grindelwald

📍 Day Trips From Grindelwald

Gaze at the Eiger on a roundtrip escapade to Jungfraujoch, dubbed the 'Top of Europe,' which houses the highest railway station on the continent, at an altitude of 3454 meters. The journey from Grindelwald to Eigergletscher station is nothing short of a spectacle. Hop on the 'Eiger Express' cable car for a brisk 15-minute ride on this contemporary wonder, with a view of the 1800-meter-high Eiger North Face. At the Eiger Glacier, transition to the cogwheel train for a journey through the rugged mountain ranges of Eiger and Mönch, culminating in Jungfraujoch.

Glacier Canyon and Crystal Museum

📍 Gletscherschlucht

Older than the dinosaurs, it took Mother Nature eons to carve out this incredible canyon. The Upper Grindelwald Glacier sits 1,400 meters (4,600 ft) above sea level, making it one of the lowest glaciers in the Swiss Alps. You can now walk one kilometer into the gorge via a wooden walkway and explore the unique rock formations and tunnels. There's also information points along the way so you can learn about the unique history of Glacier Canyon. **Expect plenty of beauty.** Inside the canyon, rushing water cascades freely from the mountains into the Lütschine River below, sometimes causing a sudden rush of wind through the canyon and a rapid temperature flux as rays of light reflect along the steep walls. Looking for an even bigger spectacle? Every Friday until 22:00, the canyon walls light up so you can enjoy an immersive experience in one of nature's most beautiful locations!

Indoor Ropes Park Grindelwald

📍 Indoor Ropes Park Grindelwald

The sky-high peaks and sheer cliff faces of the Swiss Alps aren't for your average climber. Don't fret, you can still get your adrenaline fix from the 40+ challenging elements housed inside Grindelwald's Indoor Ropes Park! Channel your primate prowess as you clamber, climb, and even fly around five tricky obstacle courses. Navigate giant icicles, journey across a spider's web, and swing yourself over the Hinterstoisser Traverse. It's not quite the Matterhorn, but it's a great start!

Grindelwald First: Day Trip from Lucerne

📍 Firstbahn AG

Depart for an early-morning drive past five different mountain lakes to the idyllic Bernese Oberland. After a photo stop in the town of Interlaken, journey on to the mountain resort of Grindelwald. From the village centre, take the aerial cableway up to Mt First, where many different summit attractions are. Venture on the First Cliff Walk by Tissot and enjoy views of the cliff face from the 40 m long single-rope suspension bridge at dizzying heights. Enjoy a short visit to Lauterbrunnen (available from April 01, 2026), nestled between towering cliffs and cascading waterfalls. Opt for a short hike or relax on an inviting bench with a view of the Eiger Glacier. Enjoy time at leisure to explore the glacier village before you take a public train back to Interlaken, where you meet your tour guide for the return trip in the late afternoon.
