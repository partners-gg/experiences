Alligator Bay: Entry Ticket

📍 Alligator Bay

Snap up some Alligator Bay tickets and see what's lurking at the foot of Mont Saint Michel. With over 700 cold-blooded creatures from across the continents, including Europe's largest collection of alligators, as well as frogs, turtles, crocodiles, lizards, and more, it's a reptile zoo on a totally different scale! In the alligator greenhouse, explore a trail filled with waterfalls, cabins, and mysterious lanes surrounded by caimans, alligators, and crafty crocodiles. See a rare Mississippi albino gator, snakes and lizards of every color and pattern, and so much more. Make sure you catch a crocodilian feeding frenzy during your visit and be glad you're not on the menu!
