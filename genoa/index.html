Genoa: Old Town Guided Walking Tour

Join this guided walking tour, which begins on Strada Nuova (Via Garibaldi), a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and takes you through Europe’s largest medieval historic centre. The ticket includes entry to the Strada Nuova Museums, which display artworks by Italian and international artists from the 15th to the 19th centuries, with a strong focus on the Genoese and Flemish schools. You’ll also visit the Sant’Agostino Museum, which houses important works from medieval Genoa inside its cloister and Gothic church. As you walk through the city, you’ll learn about Genoa’s history as a maritime republic and see key landmarks, including the Palazzi dei Rolli on Via Garibaldi, the Cathedral of San Lorenzo, Palazzo Ducale, and the Teatro Carlo Felice.
