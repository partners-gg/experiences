Cap Norfeu: 2-Hr Glass Bottom Boat Trip from Roses or Santa Margarita
Take a step back from the beach towns and appreciate just how beautiful the coastline of the Costa Brava is on this cruise toward Cap Norfeu. ROSES ·COSTA BRAVA BOAT TOURS
Cadaqués: Boat Tour from Roses
Take a trip to the village of Cadaqués from Roses. Cruise along the coast of the Cap de Creus Natural Park on a tour that blends nature, history, and culture. Along the route, you will see the Rosas Yacht Club, the fishing port, Rosas Lighthouse, Trinidad Castle, and the various coves of Cap de Creus Natural Park from the boat. A special highlight is Cabo Norfeu, where the Tamariu Cave (a natural hole in the rocks) and the rock formation called El Gato (named for its shape) can be seen. Before arriving in Cadaqués, the boat passes in front of the Bay of Jóncols.
