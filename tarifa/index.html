Gibraltar Whale and Dolphin Watching from Seville

📍 Tarifa Water Activities

Take a trip from Seville and sail between the coasts of Europe and Africa while keeping a keen eye on the water – seven different species of whales and dolphins are known to swim here! You’ll be whisked away from Gibraltar, and spend two hours on the Strait of Gibraltar. You can keep your eyes peeled from the upper deck, or head to submerged windows below deck to get a more complete picture.
