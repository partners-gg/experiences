Ducal Palace of Gandia: Audio Tour

📍 Ducal Palace of Gandia

Visit one of the most important historic buildings in Valencia, the Ducal Palace of Gandia, and trace the history of Valencian civil heritage through one the city's most important and influential families: the Borjas. Learn about historic characters in this famous family, such as the Duchess Maria Enriquez, San Francisco de Borja, and many more. The interior of this spectacular palace will leave you speechless. Its breathtaking halls and grandiose rooms showcase a cross-section of Valencia's diverse cultural history, with dramatic Gothic architecture, beautiful Mudejar motifs from Spain's Moorish past, and elegant Baroque aesthetics all completing for your wondering eyes. Its the result of its seven centuries of antiquity rolled into one history-filled building! A richly detailed audio guide will help fill in the blanks as you explore this Valencian marvel.
