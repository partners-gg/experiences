📍 Plopsa Indoor Hasselt

Celebrate 20 years with Bumba! In 2025, Plopsa Indoor Hasselt celebrates its 20th anniversary, marking the occasion with a full year of special events, magical moments, and shows! Discover 25 attractions and go on fun adventures with your favorite Studio 100 heroes. Ready for action? Race down one of the longest slides in the country or take on the Wickie Coaster, where you'll speed through dark caves. At The Lighthouse, you'll soar up and down at breathtaking speed Ahoy, young pirates! Join Piet Piraat aboard The Wild Sea for an adventure. Feel like dancing? Show off your moves in the K3-Disco and sing along to the biggest hits of Hanne, Marthe, and Julia. Meanwhile, the little ones can explore the colorful Bumba’s Playground, hop around on The Frogs, or enjoy a ride on The Ducks with Beetle Kurt and his friends.
