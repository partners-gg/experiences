Legends In Concert

📍 Legends In Concert

Legends in Concert is known as the pioneer of tribute acts. What started in 1984 as a limited six-week engagement in Las Vegas now celebrates 35 years of entertaining millions of people from around the world across its multiple venues! You're going to know most of the songs, and you're definitely going to sing along. Tap your toes to the razzle and dazzle in a cool beach setting as you watch a cast of talented singers and dancers take on the musical numbers of the Legends. Hard Rock Cafe Myrtle Beach Dining Experience

📍 Hard Rock Cafe Myrtle Beach

The Hard Rock Cafe Myrtle Beach is the premier destination for music-themed dining in South Carolina. Located on the iconic Myrtle Beach Boardwalk, the cafe offers an unforgettable atmosphere, with live music and a menu featuring classic American favorites like juicy burgers, succulent sandwiches, and epic sides and salads. With a lively atmosphere, friendly staff, and an ever-changing array of memorabilia from some of the world's greatest musical acts, the Hard Rock Cafe Myrtle Beach is the perfect place to experience the best of the beach.
