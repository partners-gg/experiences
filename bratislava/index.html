Bratislava: Guided Walking Tour + Castle Entry

📍 Bratislava City Tours

Meet your guide and begin your walking tour of Bratislava's historic center. Start with an introduction to Bratislava and Slovakia. Learn about the history of Slovakia, from the time of the old Slavs and the Great Moravia Empire. Hear stories from when Bratislava was the capital of the Hungarian Kingdom, with its kings and queens crowned here. The guide will share events from the era of Czechoslovakia, touching on Slovakia's communist period and the Velvet Revolution, leading to its 2004 membership in the European Union. See the Opera House and the House of Reduta. Continue to the main square, passing by the Old Town Hall. In Franciscan Square, view some of the oldest buildings in the city.
