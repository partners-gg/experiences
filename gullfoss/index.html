Gullfoss Waterfall: Snowmobiling Tour

📍 Gullfoss Waterfall

If you've got your own wheels and want to add a special touch to your Golden Circle trip, look no further. Join the guide for a snowmobile adventure in the Icelandic highlands, offering stunning views of the Langjökull glacier and its surroundings. There are three daily departures from Gullfoss Café, where you can park your car and grab lunch. The drive in a supertruck through Iceland's rugged landscape is an adventure in itself. The view out of the window, a vast countryside and mountain ranges, is a rare sight for many travelers. Once you reach the glacier hut, you'll get your snowmobile gear. Friendly guides will brief you on safety and operation before you head out into the winter wonderland.

Iceland: Ice Cave and Glacier Monster Truck Tour from Gullfoss

📍 Gullfoss Waterfall

Join a guided tour of Langjökull Glacier for an adventure into icy wonders. The experienced team will share the glacier's beauty, stories of the Icelandic Highlands, and fascinating glacier facts. Travel comfortably in Sleipnir monster trucks with panoramic windows and large tires for an exceptional view. Explore an impressive ice cave at the center of the glacier, where volcanic ash from past eruptions is preserved in the ice walls. Don't forget your camera to capture the scenic landscapes of Langjökull.

Langjökull Glacier: Guided Monster Truck Tour

📍 Gullfoss Waterfall

Board Sleipnir monster trucks for an adventure to Langjökull, Iceland's second-largest glacier. Expert guides will share stories of the Icelandic Highlands, glacier facts, and Nordic mythology. Stop at a scenic spot on the glacier for hot cocoa, coffee, or tea with Kleinur (a traditional Icelandic pastry). You can even sample pure glacier water. Thrill-seekers can sled down slopes or play golf on the ice (depending on weather and glacier conditions).
