Maó Harbor Cruise with Underwater Views

📍 Yellow Catamarans

Enjoy some of the best views in Minorca during this cruise of the Port of Mahon. There's colonial-era homes, sun-weathered stone fortresses, and a mix of sea cliffs and green hills. During the cruise, at the mouth of the port, you can also head to the semi-submersible lower deck of the ship, and enjoy views of what's going on beneath the water. You'll pass by 16 scenic spots, and absorb the whole skyline of the old city as your tour reaches the end of the harbor. Keep your camera ready!

Menorca: Lazareto Island Guided Tour

📍 Isla del Lazareto

The Lazareto de Menorca is a historic site that hosts guided tours on Menorca island. It was built in 1793 as a quarantine area for sailors who might be carrying diseases. After about a hundred years, it became a home for members of the Ministry of Health and their families. Over time, it was renovated and turned into a space for events, congresses, and meetings. In 1993, it was declared a Cultural Asset of Interest (BIC) – it's got great architecture and you'll have plenty to learn!
