De Hoge Veluwe National Park

📍 Het Nationale Park De Hoge Veluwe

Visit De Hoge Veluwe National Park, one of the largest nature reserves in the country covering 5,400 hectares of forests, heathlands, and sand dunes. Explore the park on foot or by bike on the many trails, and spot wildlife like red deer, wild boar, ravens, and sand lizards. You can even borrow one of the park's free White Bicycles. Check out the Museonder, an interesting underground museum, and grab a meal at the Park Pavilion. Your ticket to De Hoge Veluwe National Park gives you access to beautiful landscapes and diverse wildlife. With extensive walking, cycling, and horse-riding trails, plus a unique museum, the park offers a mix of nature and culture.

Kröller-Müller Museum: Entry Ticket

📍 Kröller-Müller Museum

Explore the renowned Kröller-Müller Museum for an art-filled trip. Discover masterpieces from famous artists like Van Gogh, Seurat, Mondrian, Picasso, and Monet. Admire over 160 fascinating sculptures in the museum's sculpture garden. With changing exhibitions, each season brings a fresh reason to visit. Located in a national park, the museum's setting adds a special appeal. Absorb yourself in the blend of art, nature, and architecture at the Kröller-Müller Museum in Otterlo.
