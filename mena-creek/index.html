Paronella Park: Entry Ticket

📍 Paronella Park

José Paronella's dream was to build a castle. He chose a special part of Australia and created Paronella Park. This fairytale-like park in North Queensland is rich in history and packed with picturesque charm. This Paronella Park ticket includes two guided tours, designed to help you experience the best of it. **The Dream Continues Tour** A 35-minute guided walk through the highlights of the Park with a guide, who'll explain the story of José Paronella’s dreams and vision. Departs every 30 minutes from 9.30 am until 4.30 pm. **The Darkness Falls Tour** Learn the secrets of Paronella Park at Night - a special way to extend your Paronella Park Experience. Tour runs every night and lasts approximately 45 minutes. **Self-guided Botanical Walk** Take yourself on a walk with a 16-page booklet containing details of 88 of the Park's special flora and fauna. The trees will be identified throughout the park as you walk.
