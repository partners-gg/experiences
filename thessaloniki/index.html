Hop-on Hop-off Bus Thessaloniki

📍 Thessaloniki Bus Tours

Discover the best of Greece’s second city at your own pace with tickets for the Hop-on Hop-off Bus Thessaloniki. This six-stop tour will bring you to the city’s most iconic sights, including the White Tower of Thessaloniki, the historic and charming neighborhood of Ano Poli, and the waterfront artwork _The Umbrellas_ by Zongolopoulos. Your tour includes an audio guide with 12 languages, as well as the option to hop on, or off, at any of the bus’s stops.
