Strap on crampons, grab ice axes, and hike up 300 meters to the scenic blue icefall. Black Sand Beach: Super Jeep Tour
Join the Super Jeep tour and explore the black sand beaches of Iceland. Witness the remains of the earliest local airport and the Hvanneyjarviti lighthouse as you ride along the coastline. Continue to the treacherous entrance of Höfn harbor, where you might spot playful seals and lovely mountain views. The highlight of the tour is a visit to a mystical glacial lagoon beneath the Fláajökull or Heinabergsjökull. Keep an eye out for reindeer along the way. Led by a knowledgeable guide, learn about the history of the airport, the harbor entrance, and the glaciers' impact on the environment.
