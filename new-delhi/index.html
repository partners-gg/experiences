New Delhi: Guided Day Tour + Roundtrip Transfer

📍 New Delhi City Tours

Start your full-day tour of India's capital in Old Delhi, beginning at the Red Fort, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan. Ride a rickshaw through Chandni Chowk, a bustling shopping area, to reach Jama Masjid, a 17th-century mosque and the largest in India. After a local lunch, head to New Delhi to visit Qutub Minar, the tallest brick minaret in the world, followed by the Tomb of Mughal Emperor Humayun. Stop by India Gate, a World War I memorial for Indian soldiers, and visit the Lotus Temple, shaped like a lotus flower. End the tour by driving past the Presidential House and Parliament Buildings before returning to your accommodation.

New Delhi: Guided City Tour + Roundtrip Transfer

📍 New Delhi City Tours

Start your day with a private tour of Old and New Delhi. A friendly driver-guide will pick you up from your hotel or the airport in a comfortable, air-conditioned vehicle. Begin the tour at Jama Masjid, one of India’s largest mosques, and explore the bustling Chandni Chowk area by taking a rickshaw ride through the narrow lanes to visit the famous spice market. Stop at Gurudwara Bangla Sahib to learn about the Sikh religion and see the world’s second-largest donation kitchen. Continue to the 16th-century tomb of Emperor Humayun and get a glimpse of the famous India Gate. Enjoy lunch at a multi-cuisine restaurant before visiting the Parliament House and the Presidential Palace. End your tour with visits to the Qutub Minar and Lotus Temple before your drop-off at the hotel.
