The Giants of Sila: Entry Ticket

📍 The Giants of Sila

The Mollo family began planting Calabrian pines and sycamores on their land around 350 years ago. Since then, one of the most beautiful forests in Europe has had time to grow. The Giants of the Sila dominate the skyline, and will stagger you as you look up from their awesomely wide trunks. Nature has been allowed to take its course in Sila National Park. As you take to the walking trails and step under the boughs of fallen giants, make sure to keep an eye out for the local wildlife!
