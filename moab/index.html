Fisher Towers: Rafting Experience with Lunch

📍 Rafting in Moab

Travel the river alongside Fisher Towers and the La Sal Mountains in Moab, Utah. Start with a drive along Highway 128, which is a designated National Scenic Byway, leading to the rafting launch point. Before starting, listen to a safety briefing and learn essential rafting techniques. During the trip, navigate through several whitewater segments like Onion Creek Rapid, Professor Creek Rapid, Rocky Rapid, and Whites Rapid. Look around to view the canyon's features and learn about the area's geology, history, and natural ecosystem. Whitewater rapids are typically classified as Class II, with Class III possible during higher water levels. Participate in swimming along the way, and a meal is included as part of this activity.
