📍 Caribe Bay

There are lots of waterparks in Italy, but there's only one Caribe Bay. Repeatedly named the best waterpark in Italy, this tropical island-themed resort takes you all the way to the Caribbean without ever leaving the vicinity of Venice. You can zip down enormous waterslides or relax on life-size beach while drinking exotic cocktails, along with oceans of other attractions, activities, and stunningly designed areas, this fun-drenched water world is the perfect escape for all the family. You can enjoy your time in Pirates’ Bay playing with your little pirates or, if you prefer, you can take a dive and sunbathe in Laguna De Oro or Playa Paraiso – the most relaxing zones. Everything you need is at Caribe Bay!
