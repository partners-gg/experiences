Segovia Guided Tour: City, Cathedral & Alcázar

📍 Alcázar of Segovia

Discover Segovia in full with a tour of its most emblematic monuments: the Aqueduct, Cathedral and Alcazar. With your Spanish-speaking guide leading the way, walk through narrow streets highlighting the influence of the different cultures that contributed to the prosperity of one of Europe's lesser-known gems throughout the Middle Ages. Admire the arched Roman Aqueduct in Plaza del Azoguejo, old noble houses, atriums and towers of Romanesque churches, and corners full of legends. Go inside the grand Gothic cathedral, and walk through the Jewish quarter en route to the jewel of the city, the Alcázar of Segovia.

Toledo & Segovia: Day Trip from Madrid + Alcázar of Segovia Entry

📍 Alcázar of Segovia

The city of Toledo – known by many as the second Rome – was declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO in 1986 for its cultural heritage. During your tour you’ll get to know why. Throughout the day, you’ll visit the Cathedral of Toledo, the Mirador del Valle, the Monastery of San Juan de los Reyes, and the Bridge of San Martin. Make sure you take some time to explore the city’s narrow streets, squares and gardens, too. In Segovia, learn about the Aqueduct, one of the most superb feats of engineering that the Romans left scattered throughout their vast empire. Then, you’ll turn your attention toward the Segovia Cathedral and after that continue through the neighbourhood of the Canonjías until you reach the famous Alcazar, a symbol of the art and history of Spain. This tour will leave you enchanted by the beautiful views these two cities offer. And while your eyes will drink in beauty, you’ll also learn plenty about the architecture and history from an expert guide.

Segovia: City & Alcázar Guided Tour

Travel back in time with a guided tour of Segovia's old city and get a look inside it's grand Alcázar. With your Spanish-speaking guide leading the way, you'll visit all the cultural hotspots in a city designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1985.
