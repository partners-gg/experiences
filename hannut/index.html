📍 Plopsaqua Hannut-Landen

Discover the magic of Plopsaqua Hannut-Landen! With over 15 sensational water attractions, you’ll be fully immersed in the world of Maya the Bee and Bumba. Step into the water paradise of Plopsaqua Hannut-Landen, where your wildest dreams come true! Drift along the Wild River and soak in the beauty of Maya’s flower-filled world. Flip, Maya’s best friend, claims to have the most exciting slide in The Water Garden. Test both Flip’s Slide and the Aqua’Splash to see which one gets your heart racing the most! Face a tropical storm with waves in The Storm Bath, or twist and turn on a tube through the hairpin bends of the Sliding Tires! For the true daredevils, the Sky Drop offers a 20-meter free fall.
