Universeum: Entry Ticket

📍 Universeum

Book tickets to Universeum, a public science center and museum in Gothenburg, and one of Sweden's most visited places. See everything from rockets and planets to monkeys and atoms, and almost everything in between. Investigate the world, from tiny molecules to vast galaxies, to learn where you fit into the universe. Listen to animal talks or watch a show. Conduct experiments at the Chemistry Lab, or learn about the human body at the Humans exhibit. Understand math’s role in daily life at the Mathrix. Bring children to the Miniverseum. Walk through the wilderness and find monkeys and sloths in the Rainforest.
