Outdoor Ropes Park: Interlaken

📍 Ropes Park Interlaken

Get your kicks with over 120 obstacles on nine different challenging courses, surrounded by stunning nature and the mountains of the Bernese Oberland. The Ropes Park in Interlaken is a fun-filled forest playground fit for the whole family. From little thrill-seekers to adult adventurers, there's something for everyone here. Even beginners are catered for. Whether you're scaling wooden bridges, braving Tarzan swings or strapping into a harness to try out a new zip line, you'll be taken care of by the experts, and everyone receives safety instructions before they start. The Snowli Kids Courses are suitable for all kids who can walk on their own. There is no minimum age or height required. Parents can even assist from the ground.
