ARLES ·MONTMAJOUR ABBEY
Montmajour Abbey: Entry Ticket
At the time of its initial construction, Montmajour was an island some 43 meters above the marshland terrain that surrounded it. However, a lot has changed since the building's humble beginnings. This ticket lets you explore one of the most fascinating sites around Arles. You'll understand why Van Gogh made it one of his subjects, and how more than a thousand years of development shaped its purpose and its grand exterior.

ARLES ·ARLES DAY TRIPS
Camargue: Guided Tour from Arles + Saintes-Maries-de-la-Mer Visit
Start in Arles, behind the tourist office and in front of the Best Western Atrium hotel. Spend four hours discovering the region with a guide. See the ponds and marshes where flamingos and many other birds live. Pass through different private estates and see Camargue bulls and horses. Follow along the edge of the natural reserve and enter the Parc Naturel Régional de Camargue, away from usual tourist routes. Take multiple photo stops. Spend 30 minutes visiting the village of Saintes-Maries-de-la-Mer.
