📍 Hervey Bay Cruises

A whale watching cruise out in Hervey Bay is an extraordinary, mesmerizing, and absolutely krill-iant way to spend time in the great outdoors. Take to the water for a morning or afternoon trip, with an expert guide from the region at the helm to search out and educate you on these wondrous creatures. Your multi-level vessel boasts a hydrophone so you can listen to the whales beneath – mimicking their song is encouraged. Two meals are included; adventuring is hungry work!

K'gari: Guided Boat Tour from Hervey Bay + Lunch

📍 Fraser Island

Visit the remote west coast of K’gari (formerly Fraser Island), a UNESCO World Heritage site, from Hervey Bay. Avoid the crowds and discover lesser-known areas known only to locals. Travel to K’gari on a comfortable catamaran. Spend the day snorkeling, kayaking, swimming, and bushwalking. Try a tube ride through clear waters for fun. See attractions like Wathumba Creek, Awinya Creek, and Bowarrady Creek.

Noosa: Day Tour with Dolphin View, Sea Kayak & Beach 4x4 Adventure

📍 Fraser Island

Join an eco-tour to Double Island Point. Travel by 4X4 across Teewah Beach and the Tewantin cable ferry into Great Sandy National Park. Kayak in clear waters and look for dolphins, humpback whales (June-Nov), turtles, and rays. This beginner-friendly tour (ages 6+) includes views of Rainbow Beach’s colored cliffs and Fraser Island (K'Gari), with expert guides leading the way.
