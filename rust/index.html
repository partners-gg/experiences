Rulantica: Moonlight Ticket

📍 Rulantica

Go on an aquatic adventure at Rulantica, a year-round water park near Europa-Park in Rust, catering to everyone, from thrill-seekers to families and those looking to relax. The moonlight ticket allows you to enter after 19:00 on a selected day, letting you enjoy a little water fun and unwind after a long day. It features over 50 slides and attractions, 3 swim-up bars, 3 saunas, a steam bath, and 14 Nordic-themed areas. Experience the wild waters of _Vildstrøm_, the slides of _Svalgur Rytt_ and _Vinter Rytt_, or relax in the serene _Frigg Tempel_ and S_kog Lagune_. Race towards the finish line head-first in _Vikingløp_, Europe’s largest speed slide. With spraying geysers, a mystical grotto, and surrounded by volcanic rock, _Svømmepøl_ invites you to enjoy a swimming experience in a rugged, Nordic landscape all year round. This newest addition features a large heated outdoor pool with geysers, bubble loungers, and a swim-up bar.
