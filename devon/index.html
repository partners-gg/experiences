RHS Garden Rosemoor: Entry Ticket

📍 RHS Garden Rosemoor

Nestled in the Torridge Valley and surrounded by woodland, Rosemoor is an enchanting place to visit, whatever the weather or season. It's the quintessential "English country garden!" The inspiring planting displays enhanced by the natural beauty of Devon make photography a must. Explore the largest rose gardens in the South West, the beautiful hot garden, a storybook Devon cottage garden and a lake, stream and waterfall. The fruit and vegetable garden might inspire you to get green-fingered yourself, and don't miss a stroll – or a roll – in the Victorian Stumpery, where endless flower-filled meadows and bluebell-carpeted woodlands await.
