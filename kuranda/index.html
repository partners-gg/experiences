📍 Rainforestation Nature Park

Queensland's beautiful rainforest is waiting to be discovered at Rainforestation Nature Park. The wildest wildlife is waiting to meet you too – look out for marsupials and the imposing cassowary!

Kuranda Koala Gardens: Entry Ticket

📍 Kuranda Koala Gardens

Kuranda Koala Gardens is a boutique wildlife attraction located in the world-famous Kuranda rainforest village, Queensland. Grab your Karunda Koala Gardens tickets and explore the home of an amazing array of Australian animals, including wallabies, wombats, quokkas, gliders, and freshwater crocodiles. You can even hold a koala and take home a souvenir photo for an extra cost (paid on-site). The Aussie Bush Collection has a fascinating combination of marsupials and reptiles, all living in harmony together within the exhibit. Many animals are experts in camouflage, so take your time and see if you can spot them all. In the Nocturnal Wonders exhibit, visitors get the rare opportunity to see some night-loving marsupials up close.

Birdworld Kuranda: Entry Ticket

📍 Birdworld Kuranda

At Birdworld Kuranda you can immerse yourself in one of the most unique wildlife attractions of Tropical North Queensland. Birdworld Kuranda replicates the natural habitats of almost 60 native and exotic species that roam this unique rainforest habitat. There’s hand-feeding and photo opportunities aplenty, so make sure you bring your camera! Flying around this paradise of birds are species from the vanishing rainforests of the world, including some of Australia’s most precious and beautiful feathered friends. There are brilliant Amazonian macaws, the endangered and stately cassowary, cheeky rainbow lorikeets, galahs, cockatoos and many more. Naturally landscaped with waterfalls, ponds, exotic and native plants, Birdworld Kuranda is a place where you can join the birds in their beautiful world. Surround yourself in their rainforest habitat and be amazed at the more than 350 birds of all shapes, sizes and colours on display, including the Finch Aviary.
