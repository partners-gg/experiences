Safari Thoiry: Entry Ticket

📍 Safari de Thoiry

Leave the urban jungle of Paris behind you and see some real wildlife! Just 40 km west of Paris, Thoiry Wow Safari lets you drive into the heart of nature on a fun-packed safari adventure for the whole family. This 150-hectare wildlife park is home to 1300 animals from more than 150 different species. From grazing herds of zebras to tree-munching giraffes, curious rhinos, laughing hyenas, lions, tigers, bears, and more, you'll meet creatures big and small – all from the safety and comfort of your car. Be sure to swing by the recently added state-of-the-art gorilla habitat, and see the world's biggest apes monkey around together. You'll see spectacular varieties of rare birds of every color in the Australian aviary, while fans of reptiles and creepy-crawlies will be in for a treat in the vivarium.
