HONG KONG ·HONG KONG CRUISES
Hong Kong: Aberdeen Boat Tour + Floating Museum Visit
Aberdeen was once one of the most significant fishing ports in Hong Kong. Take a closer look at the city's golden days before urbanization changed everything! Experience the unique and authentic culture of Hong Kong's fishing village and witness the bustling floating community. On this tour into the past, you'll enjoy a relaxing 30-minute sampan ride with amazing views and audio commentary on Hong Kong's charming fishing history. Then step aboard an original fisherman's houseboat, and see how they lived and worked. You can even enjoy some rare and authentic 'boat noodles' (floating kitchens) on a lunch tour.

HONG KONG ·AIRPORT TRANSFERS HONG KONG
Hong Kong: Airport Express to and from Kowloon/HK/Tsing Yi Station
Travel seamlessly from the city to the airport in under 24 minutes with the Hong Kong Airport Express. This efficient 35.3-kilometer ride operates every 10 minutes from 5:50 AM to 1:15 AM. Each station offers convenient facilities including check-in counters for major airlines at MTR Kowloon and Hong Kong Station. Airport Express shuttle buses run regularly, and porters are available to assist with baggage. For night flights, in-town check-in services allow you to enjoy more of Hong Kong before departure. Plus, complimentary shuttle buses are provided for Airport Express passengers traveling to and from Airport Station, connecting major hotels at Hong Kong and Kowloon Station.
