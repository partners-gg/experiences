Lake Louise Sightseeing Gondola Ride

📍 Lake Louise Ski Resort

Deep blue waters, engulfed by thick emerald-green forest and snow-capped mountains – it's no wonder Lake Louise is considered a photographer's dream. There's an easy way to experience the serenity of Canada's turquoise icon. The Lake Louise Sightseeing Gondola takes you to heights of 2,000 meters, offering utterly unforgettable views of the water, the Rocky Mountains, and the surrounding wildflowers and natural springs.
