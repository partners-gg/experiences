Khao Sok: Private Longtail Boat Tour at Cheow Lan Lake

📍 Khao Sok Day Trips

Embark on a mesmerizing journey, starting right at the doorstep of your hotel in Khao Sok or meeting at the pier in the heart of the breathtaking Khao Sok National Park. Climb aboard a traditional longtail boat and set sail for an adventure through the world's oldest rainforest. This wonderful voyage is an elixir for those who are enchanted by the serenity of a boat tour amidst exquisite landscapes. Glide around the tranquil Cheow Lan Lake on the longtail boat, immersed in the harmonious symphony of pristine nature. The boat then navigates its way towards the Diamond Cave. Spend 30 minutes unraveling the mysteries of this celestial cave adorned with coral, stalagmites, and stalactites. The journey concludes at the raft house, a sanctuary of tranquility. Bask in the calm setting or take a plunge into the captivating emerald waters. The return journey offers a feast for the eyes with awe-inspiring views of the towering limestone cliffs. If the option with hotel transfers was selected, be prepared for a cozy ride back to your hotel in Khao Sok, leaving you with unforgettable memories etched in your heart.
