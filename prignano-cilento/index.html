📍 Alento River Oasis

Put the 'great' back into the 'great outdoors' in the National Park of Cilento, where the Alento River Oasis (Oasi Fiume Alento) is waiting to envelop you in a world of natural wonders! This huge Community Importance Site boasts a harmonious link with nature, thanks to its earth dam along the course of the Alento river. This creates an artificial lake for humans to play on, while the surrounding lakes are left alone to preserve the wildlife. Enjoy educational workshops, mountain biking, boat trips, bird watching, fishing, archery, and more! You can take a loop on the electric bus, settle down for a picnic, go horse riding, or take to the lake in a canoe - it's up to you.
