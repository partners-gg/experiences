Château de Montsoreau – Museum of Contemporary Art

📍 Château de Montsoreau – Musée d’art contemporain

Art in a Renaissance-style castle – what could be more culturally extravagant for a day trip? In the sprawling Loire Valley, just two hours from Paris, come and explore the Art & Language branch of the Conceptual art movement. It's the world's largest collection exclusively focused on this movement. From a critical approach to modernism to a collaboration with a rock band and traditional art like paintings, drawings, and sculptures, it's the coolest castle adventure we can think of!
