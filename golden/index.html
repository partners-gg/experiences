Kicking Horse Mountain Resort: Powder Express Shuttle from Banff + Ski Lift Pass

📍 Kicking Horse Mountain Resort

Discover Kicking Horse Mountain Resort, one of the most thrilling ski resorts in Canada. You’ll arrive right after the gondola opens, so you can squeeze in as many runs as you like, with around six hours of time on the hill. With plenty of steep and deep powder, Kicking Horse offers heart-pumping vertical runs, steep chutes and wide-open bowls. Featuring abundant soft snow and high alpine terrain, Kicking Horse has something for everyone. Wide open bowls and long, long groomed runs for cruising, steep chutes and tree runs for the more adventurous. There is almost always fresh powder to be found. Enjoy a spectacular lunch at the Eagle’s Eye – Canada’s highest restaurant. Enjoy this quintessential mountaintop dining experience, at 7,700 feet and enjoy the breathtaking views of the six surrounding National Parks.

Golden Skybridge: Entry Ticket

📍 Golden Skybridge

Bird call, the whistle of the wind, and pure Rocky Mountain air – it's all yours as you stand 426 feet above a canyon on the Golden Skybridge. With Golden Skybridge tickets, a three-kilometre stretch of forested trail nestled deep in the Columbia Valley, plus Canada's two highest suspension bridges, are yours to enjoy. The first is 130 metres high and 150 metres long, and the second is 80 metres high and 140 metres long. As you cross high above a canyon with its own waterfall, make sure to loosen your grip on the railing and stop to enjoy the once-in-a-lifetime view. There's also a mini adventure park with tree forts along the route, so littles ones can enjoy playing on slides and a small ropes course. If you're hungry, stop by the open-air Village Grill which serves up barbecue-style favourites, along with wine and craft beer.
