CRÉCY-LA-CHAPELLE ·PARROT WORLD - THE IMMERSIVE ANIMAL PARK
Parrot World: Day Ticket
Parrot World is an immersive animal park located 10 km from Disneyland Paris and only 30 minutes from Paris, dedicated to the treasures of South America. Visitors can observe animals from the Amazon and Patagonia in a setting close to their natural habitat. The park is designed like a nature reserve where visitors can search for iconic South American animals such as parrots, jaguars, giant otters, Humboldt penguins, capybaras, and many others. In addition to the animal exhibits, there are also dining options and a large playground for children. The park offers a fun and immersive experience where visitors can be transported to the colorful landscapes of South America.

Parrot World: Annual Pass
Witness the vibrant world of Parrot World, created by entrepreneur and traveler Eric Vignot. Inspired by his love for parrots, he designed an immersive animal park where these colorful birds guide visitors through their habitat. Learn about the endangered natural habitats of parrots and other species from the southern hemisphere. Through the Parrot Wildlife Foundation, Parrot World is dedicated to animal protection and educating visitors about the importance of ecosystems. Explore realistic landscapes filled with jaguars, giant otters, Humboldt penguins, and scarlet ibises, all while the parrot guides flutter alongside.
