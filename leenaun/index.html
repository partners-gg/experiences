Killary Fjord Boat Tour

📍 Killary Fjord Boat Tours

Prepare to be amazed at the incredible, awe-inspiring beauty of the rugged North Connemara landscape onboard the Connemara Lady, with a 90-minute sightseeing tour, led by an expert guide. See the spectacular scenery of Killary Harbour all around you, and explore Ireland's only glacial fjord on this catamaran cruise. With informative commentary outlining the natural and social history of the Fjord, as well as the ancient mythology surrounding these waters, you'll have an amazing time out on the waves! The “savage beauty” of North Connemara’s spectacular scenery will blow you away as your tour creeps up to the mouth of the Fjord, the gateway to the majestic Atlantic coast beyond. Prepare to be wonder-struck!
