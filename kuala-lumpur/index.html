Ever wonder what a shark does all day? Come find out for yourself with tickets to Aquaria KLCC, a state-of-the-art aquarium right by the iconic Petronas Twin Towers. Watch the lives of 5,000 landbound and aquatic creatures in action beneath the Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre! Submerge yourself beneath thousands of gallons of seawater as you uncover different oceanic zones.
