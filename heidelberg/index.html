BODY WORLDS Museum Heidelberg - Anatomy of Happiness

📍 BODY WORLDS Heidelberg

There are more ways to learn about anatomy than just reading biology books. A ticket for BODY WORLDS Heidelberg is the most insightful glimpse into the human body around. Explore around 200 exhibitions inside, all delving into what it is that makes us happy and the chemical reactions that occur to get us there. The bodies you’ll see inside have undergone a plastination process by Gunther von Hagens, so you can really understand what’s going on under your skin.

Neckar River Kayak Tours in Heidelberg

📍 Haßloch Water Activities

A tour that has it all. It offers unforgettable moments in one of the most beautiful cities in the world. Explore the best of Heidelberg, including the Old Bridge, the waterfront, the castle, and much more. Best of all, you're seeing it from a unique perspective as you paddle along the Neckar River. You'll travel in comfortable and very safe single and double kayaks. Don't worry, no experience is required. Groups are small with a personal touch making it the perfect activity for families, as well as team and company outings. Your tour starts in a central location in Heidelberg on the Old Town side. First there's a short exercise in paddling technique and a few words about safety. Then, it's time to put the kayaks in the water and paddle leisurely up the river, along the Marstall, the Old Town, and through the Old Bridge.
