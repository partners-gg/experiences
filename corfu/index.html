Paxos & Antipaxos Blue Caves Cruise (Lakka Village) From Corfu Port

📍 Corfu Cruises

Departing from Corfu, take a look back at the old town and its historic Venetian fortresses before cruising across to the north side of Paxos and admiring the natural bay of Lakka. Unspoiled by mass tourism, this traditional village is perfect for travelers seeking some tranquility and relaxation away from the crowds. Only a five-minute walk from the west side of the village, you'll find two beautiful beaches surrounded by crystal-clear water – perfect for swimming or sunbathing! Continue sailing south along the west coast of Paxos, admiring the rugged coastline as you go, before entering the spectacular sea caves (famous for their bright blue water) and finally arriving at the small island of Antipaxos. Here, you will have the opportunity to swim and enjoy the crystal-clear water.

Saranda & Butrint National Park: Guided Day Tour from Corfu + Lunch

📍 Corfu Cruises

Travel to the port city of Saranda for a 20 km guided bus tour along the southern coast and its unspoiled shores and islands. Visit small villages and see the fertile plains leading to a large lake. Arrive at the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Butrint, where the ancient Greek city of Butrint was uncovered by archaeologists. Tour the Venetian Tower and see the remains of the theater and Temple of Asclepius. Continue to the ruins of the ancient gymnasium and Roman Baths, and view the mosaic floor in the Roman Baptistery of Butrint. Explore the Ancient Cyclopean Walls and get views of the lake and plains from the top of the hill. Return to Saranda for lunch, and then take a walking tour before sailing back to Corfu.

Parga, Syvota & Blue Lagoon: Full-Day Boat Cruise from Corfu

📍 Corfu Cruises

Sail from the Old Town and see the thick woods of the Kanoni Peninsula. Pass Mon Repos villa and gardens on the way to Plataria and Syvota. Continue down the coast to Parga with its notable architecture and bays. See the remains of an 11th-century Venetian Castle of Parga built as defense against pirates and Turks. Visit a small island with a church dedicated to the Virgin Mary and Venetian and French-style buildings. Walk the narrow streets and have lunch at a taverna overlooking the sea, or swim at one of the beaches. Cruise around Syvota's islands and drop anchor at the Blue Lagoon Beach, where Arabian pirates once moored their ships. Swim in the clear waters before returning to Old Town of Corfu.

Corfu: Winery Tour + Wine & Olive Tasting

📍 Corfu Food & Drinks

Visit Theotoky Estate located at Ropa Valley near the village of Giannades, in central Corfu. It is about 17 km from Corfu Town. The estate covers more than 300 acres with pine trees, olive groves, and vineyards. Take a 20-minute tour of the winery facilities and cellar. Learn about wine and olive oil production. End the tour with tastings of the estate's wines and a light snack.

Paxos & Antipaxos Blue Caves Cruise (Gaios Village) From Corfu Port

📍 Corfu Cruises

No Greek holiday is complete without a sea cruise, and this miniature odyssey will give you Mediterranean memories that will last a lifetime.
