Aberdeen: Guided City Centre Walking Tour

📍 Aberdeen City Tours

Explore Aberdeen with a local guide on this 1.5-hour walking tour, available every day at 2 pm. Learn how Aberdeen grew from a fishing village to a key player in the oil industry. Walk through some of the city's oldest streets, pass through tunnels, and hear about the people who shaped its history. You'll also see street art throughout the city. Your guide will show you where to find some of the most interesting pieces, from small corners to large buildings.
