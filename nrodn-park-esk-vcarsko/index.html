Bohemian Switzerland & Saxon National Parks: Tour from Dresden

📍 Bohemian Switzerland National Park

This tour takes you through two stunning areas: the Saxon and Bohemian Switzerland national parks. Walk across the magnificent Bastei Bridge above the Elbe River for some mind-blowing views. After that, stand beneath the natural phenomenon of Pravčická brána, the largest sandstone gate of its kind in Europe and a truly incredible sight to see. After lunch, take a boat ride through a deep gorge carved deep into the surrounding sandstone walls before heading back to your hotel.
