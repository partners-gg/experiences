GYMPIE ·MARY VALLEY RATTLER
Mary Valley Rattler Train Experience
Take a train to simpler times and discover the antique charm of the historic Mary Valley Rattler. Hop aboard a lovingly restored steam or diesel locomotive, and enjoy a relaxing ride through a pastoral expanse of natural scenery and vintage stations. Speaking of which, the Gympie Station on Tozer St has been meticulously restored to its former glory, and is not to be missed. Here, you can learn the stories of the Mary Valley Rattler line, its people, and its history. The popular Rusty Rails Cafe offers a dine-in experience before or after your rail journey, or you can buy takeaway treats to enjoy on board. You can even purchase a bottle of wine and a local cheese platter for the ride. The gift shop offers a selection of local goodies as well as speciality giftware and souvenirs to help remember the adventure. Full steam ahead!

Mary Valley Rattler: Silver Bullet Train Ride from Gympie to Dagun
Board the Heritage Silver Bullet Railmotor 2000 Class from Gympie to Dagun, departing on Fridays. Travel through the hills of the Mary Valley and reach Dagun Station. The experience lasts two hours, letting you relax and see the views. Additional cold and alcoholic beverages can be purchased from the onboard menu.
