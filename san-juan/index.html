Vega Baja Forest: Guided Hike to Caves + Waterfall

📍 Puerto Rico Rainforest Adventures

In the center of Vega Baja, the island's most stunning vistas are interwoven with mountainous terrain. This experience starts with a hike into the vibrant jungle, home to crystal clear waters and cave views. Upon arrival at the first cave, you can bathe peacefully beneath a waterfall. Then, the route leads to a subterranean cave waterfall. Post-exit, the river is crossed at different points, revealing ancient Taino carvings on the stone - a testament to the land's rich history. The journey continues into a second cave, where the thrill intensifies as one gets to swim underground. After emerging from this subterranean swim, the hike resumes through the lush tropical jungle.
