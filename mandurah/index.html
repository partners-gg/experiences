Mandurah: Giant & Dolphin Guided Sightseeing Cruise

📍 Mandurah Cruises

Explore the Giants of Mandurah, an outdoor exhibition featuring large sculptures made from recycled wood by artist Thomas Dambo. These sculptures highlight local beauty and emphasize the need to protect nature. Navigate Mandurah's waterways and wetlands on Bindjareb Noongar land. Take a boat ride to view 'Seba's Song,' a giant sculpture best seen from the water. Find five hidden sculptures throughout Mandurah. Discover four of them to unlock the location of the fifth secret giant. Along the way, spot wild dolphins and birdlife as you uncover these sculptures.
