Zoo Koki: Entry Ticket

📍 Zoo Koki

It all started as three brothers' small exotic bird collection in 1982. Today, Zoo Koki is one of the best zoos and botanical parks in Spain. While never losing sight of its humble beginnings, the zoo has expanded its collection to include over 150 species of incredible wildlife from around the globe. See beautiful big cats from jaguars to lynx and ocelots; majestic birds of prey, emus, tortoises, and lots more. The zoo is split into thematic zones that reflect the habitats of each creature, while over 300 species of exotic flora spread around the park give it a decidedly botanical feeling.
