Cathedral of Saint Paul: VIP Docent-Led Tour

📍 Cathedral of Saint Paul

Join a VIP docent-led tour of Saint Paul’s Cathedral. This 90-minute tour offers access to spaces typically reserved for clergy and staff. Visit the Sacristy to see where priests and liturgical ministers prepare for Mass. See vestments, chalices, crosiers, and sacred vessels. Learn about the rituals and traditions from your docent. Take an elevator to the choir loft for a view of the cathedral’s nave from above. Observe the arches, mosaics, and three rose windows from a new angle. Move to the lower level to see the cathedral museum. View artifacts from the original 1858 cathedral, including the organ console, sacred relics, and historic photographs. Learn how the cathedral has changed over the years.
